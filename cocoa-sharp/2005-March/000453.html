Go check out the downloads page at www.mono-project.com for an installer 
disk image of Mono for OSX. That's all the users of your apps will need. 
I think Cocoa# is included with the 1.1.4 installer image. Your users 
will need to have Mono installed from a disk image provided by the Mono 
team.
