Hello Tim,

> Can anyone tell me what it means when there's two items in one?  Like 
> this -> setCellAttribute:to:
> - (void)setCellAttribute:(NSCellAttribute)aParameter to:(int)value
>
> To me that looks like to different methods, but it may not be in ObjC. 
>  Can someone explain this, and offer some info on how to translate a 
> method in that form?

It's a method with two arguments. You need to use 
"setCellAttribute:to:" as selector and pass two (pairs of) arguments - 
an IntPtr and an int.
