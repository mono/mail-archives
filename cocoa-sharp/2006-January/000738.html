kangaroo schrieb:

> 5: Native.ManagedToNativeArray seems to throw out primitive types; is  
> this correct?  Also foreach would probably be a little more elegant  
> here.

Guys, sorry but I am really opposed to this method: I believe this 
should be an overloaded constructor for Cocoa.MutableArray either way 
and not inside Native! Let's not crowd Native too much with this sort of 
conversion methods.
The static ToSelector method is fine with me.

> 6: If you want to move all the DllImports into NativeMethods.Foo I'm  
> cool with that; but lets do it to all of them and split it out into  
> its own .cs

I'm fine with kangaroo's proposal as long as we keep some kind of 
structure/grouping. But getting all those little imports to one or only 
a few places would certainly be good. Maybe make the class internal.

While we're at it I would also vote for adding some descriptive private 
methods like e.g. LoadFramework that call into the strlen import. The 
Interop and core classes are really hard to grasp compared to the 
higher-level App Kit wrappers.
