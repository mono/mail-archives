I'm trying to compile CocoaDoc and have different problems :

	mcs CocoaDoc.cs -d:SYSD -out:CocoaDoc.exe -pkg:monodoc -

	mcs $(NAME).cs -d:SYSD -out:$(NAME).exe -pkg:monodoc -

Current/lib/mono/cocoa-sharp/webkit-sharp.dll \
	-r:/Library/Frameworks/Mono.framework/Versions/Current/lib/mono/ 
cocoa-sharp/cocoa-sharp.dll

	CocoaDoc.cs(147,4): error CS0117: `Cocoa.Browser' does not contain a
