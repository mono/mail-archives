Tim,

   Piecing together your e-mails; it seems that you're packing  
NSToolbarItem's into the returned NSArray; which is calling  
isEqualToString: on them; because NSToolbar expects (at least in the  
case of toolbarAllowedItemIdentifiers: and  
toolbarDefaultItemIdentifiers:) a NSArray of NSString.  Please see:  
http://www.macdevcenter.com/pub/a/mac/2002/03/15/cocoa.html?page=2  
for a decent objc example of how a NSToolbar is supposed to be used.

-g
