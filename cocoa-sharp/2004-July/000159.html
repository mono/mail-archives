Ken,

thanks for your email.

1) We've chosen to go with the /Library/Framework way (with links to 
/usr/bin) for it seemed the most friendly and closest to the MacOS X 
ways.
2) I believe you just have to go to the packages directory and double 
click on the Mono installer to de-install it. Maybe the cocoa# list can 
help us with that question
3) and 4), yes we're working on it. We're reached one goal where we 
provide a working installer for Mono only on MacOS X. We're going to 
try to make it easier on MacOS in general now, providing Monodoc, 
Monodevelop and GTK/GTK# packages. I suggest the cocoa# list for 
information on XCode+Mono as well as the bindings of course and a 
general source of great information on Mono MacOS X from the Mac 
community

Erik
