Heya Steve.  Thanks for reporting this.

The easiest hack to just get things running would be to make a symlink
from /sw/lib/libiconv.2.dylib to /usr/lib/:

sudo ln -s /sw/lib/libiconv.2.dylib /usr/lib

Keep in mind, however, that this is a dirty, rotten hack, and that Apple
may not support you any more if you do this type of thing :)

Cheers,

C.J.
