Nope its not in the path, but that's what documentation is for!  :-)

I'm concerned about convention here.  Normally software installed by 
the user goes in /usr/local and software installed by the system goes 
in /usr.

I don't know of any framework that installs symlinks to /usr/local/bin, 
but I don't know of another framework like Mono.framework.  (Yes, 
there's JavaVM.framework, but its installed by the system...)

Also, /Library is the analog to /usr/local...
