-	Deadlock on spin down: can be reproduced by just bootstrapping mono:
one of the mcs will just hang after a 'Successful completed' message
-	Threadpool, and delegation core JIT tests are still failing
-	Mod_mono seems to have some little-endian issues

What is the time table for addressing those issues?
