> 	I had this problem too, but then I updated my /etc/mono/config =
file
> with some extra dll maps like this:
>
> <dllmap dll=3D"libgtk-win32-2.0-0.dll" target=3D"libgtk-x11-2.0.so.0"/>
> <dllmap dll=3D"libgobject-2.0-0.dll" target=3D"libgobject-2.0.so.0"/>
> <dllmap dll=3D"libglade-2.0-0.dll" target=3D"libglade-2.0.so.0"/>
> <dllmap dll=3D"libgdk_pixbuf-2.0-0.dll" =
target=3D"libgdk_pixbuf-2.0.so.0"/>
> <dllmap dll=3D"libpango-1.0-0.dll" target=3D"libpango-1.0.so.0"/>
> <dllmap dll=3D"gtkhtml-3.0" target=3D"libgtkhtml-3.1.so"/>
>
> It seems to work fine now.  Hope this helps.
