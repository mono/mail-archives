If you want to do something in Cocoa#, sometimes you need to see how Cocoa 
itself handles the problem.
This is how the localiztion mechanism works.

http://developer.apple.com/documentation/Cocoa/Conceptual/LoadingResources/Concepts/BundlesAndLocalization.html

The short answer is, if you can take advantage of NSBundle and you follow 
the requirements laid out here, it basically comes for "free".  You would 
place a separate localized NIB containing your menu in the proper locations. 
NSBundle figures out which one to load.
