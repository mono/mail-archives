I had a similar problem... (if not the same, i don't remember).

Try this:

export PKG_CONFIG_PATH=/sw/lib/pkgconfig:/usr/X11R6/lib/pkgconfig

instead of this:

PKG_CONFIG_PATH=/sw/lib/pkgconfig:/usr/X11R6/lib/pkgconfig

before you launch the configure script.
It solved the problem and "./configure" ended successfully, but after 
that i had another error during "make" which i still fail to resolve...
