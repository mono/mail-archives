You likely have a conflict between software that fink has installed and 
what the Mono.framework installed.  If you are using fink then you might 
just want to use it for all of GTK# fink install monodoc will get you 
everything you need after about 3 days of compiling.

We are still working on a pkg installer for GTK#, but its not easy.  The 
best bet is probably to wait until we get it done and volunteer to do 
test compiles.
