You can override any method in C# the problem is calling the super 
method (right now).
If you create your own method and register it, it will be overridden.  
The 'virtual' and 'override' keyword do not apply here, since the C# 
classes are just wrappers and virtual would not make sense.  Even if 
you call the base implementation you will end up in the most derived 
implementation since the real execution is done in the objective-c 
runtime.

Please log a bug in bugzilla.ximian.com product cocoa# or ask us online 
@ GimpNet #cocoa#.
