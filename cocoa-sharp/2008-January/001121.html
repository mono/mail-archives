Hi Michael,

We have been talking about the situation of cocoa sharp in the past few
days. The project seemed to have died, but like Dr. Frankenstain would say:
It's alive!!!! It's alive!!!! We are back on track and waiting for Geoff
(Norton) to post his new bindings for ObjC v2. Here is a quote of what he
said:

"I'll try to get something in SVN by end  of week; then perhaps we can
organize an IRC meeting for all interested parties to hash things out."

Which means that the best thing to do is to wait until he commits the new
code and meet on IRC at some point. There are a number of developers that
are going to help, trust me this is very exciting, the group has made a
great improvement since last Friday, you should read the list archives to be
more or less uptodate.

I hope this helps,
