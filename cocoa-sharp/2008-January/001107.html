Alright what you are seeing is the legacy I f how cocoa sharp came to be

When geoff, urs and I started all of this the idea was that www.cocoasharp.com 
  would host everything because we weren't part of mono.   
Unfortunately attrition and slow uptake took it's toll.  Urs, adham,  
and myself all fell off the mono wagon for various reasons

FWIW there are4places, there is also a Novello forge project for  
cocoasharp.

That said I think cocoasharp encompasses all things mono and mac  
because the people involved are the same and the needs are common

Given that, the appropriate location is probably mono OSS for general  
mono on mac and cocoasharp for cocoa related issues
