Hi,,
I finally found a solution to initialize a NSArray with several  
objects....

NSArray myArray = (NSArray)NSMutableArray.ArrayWithCapacity(500);
myArray = myArray.arrayByAddingObject("NSToolbarCustomizeToolbarItem");
myArray = myArray.arrayByAddingObject("NSToolbarFlexibleSpaceItem");
myArray = myArray.arrayByAddingObject("NSToolbarSpaceItem");

It's odd but this is the only way that really works !
