I don't know what level of development is taking place among others, but it
seems like development is pretty "warm" on this project.  You can accomplish
a fair bit with the library as it stands now - ( grab source from repos ) -
but I imagine there's still quite a bit of work left to do.

Full enumeration of Cocoa classes is quite a ways off, I imagine (I talked
about an automated approach earlier with one of the devs...but like many
other people, am pressed for time) - and, the project could probably use
some stubbing out of core OS functions that exist outside the realm of Cocoa
classes but provide a fair level of benefit to anyone doing OS X dev in
Mono.

Still, as said earlier, you can accomplish a fair amount at the moment in
terms of getting the core parts of an application together.
