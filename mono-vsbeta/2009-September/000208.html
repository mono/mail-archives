Since the VM is based on SLE you will not have access to those repositories unless you register it.  You could register with an eval license from here: http://www.novell.com/products/desktop/eval.html  I'm pretty sure I put the registration packages on the VM.  Run YaST and go to Online Update Configuration (assuming it's there) and follow the instructions.  Once you have registered you should have access to the repositories.

We are planning to move the VM to openSUSE in a couple of weeks.  Then you will have easier access to repositories.  Installing gcc would probably be okay but the kernel-source package is huge and only a few people would need it.
