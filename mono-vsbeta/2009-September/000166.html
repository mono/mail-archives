It is extremely uncool to get a 3.5MB attachment in my e-mail from someone I
don't know for code I don't care about filling up my inbox.  At least use
something like http://sendspace.com, http://yousendit.com, or
http://megaupload.com .
It doesn't take a lot of sense to realize that 3.5MB being broadcast to many
random people you don't know is out of line.

A lot of these guys seem to have little sense for what kind of content
should go into a mailing list.  There needs to be some kind of moderation
for this list.

I don't mind being subscribed to this list to get updates on Mono-vsbeta, or
even e-mails with interesting issues or discussions, but this sudden rash of
spammish e-mails is a bit too much.  Especially when I didn't expect it, as
I had all but forgotten about this after all this time.  A confirmation
e-mail would have been nice.
