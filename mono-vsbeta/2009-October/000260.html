Good news everyone!

We just released Beta 4 of MonoVS!

The release notes are here:
http://mono-project.com/ReleaseNotes_MonoVS

-- Updating Windows --

You can download the new .msi with your credentials here:
http://go-mono.com/monovs/

(Close all instances of VS2008 and install)

-- Updating Linux --

There is a new VM for this beta.  We switched to openSUSE 11.1, so it's 
much easier to install additional software.  You also no longer need 
your credentials to update the Linux server piece.

You can also use your old VM if you want, however we ran our tests on 
the new one, so there might be issues.


Thanks for your feedback so far, and let us know what works or doesn't
with the new Beta!

Jonathan
