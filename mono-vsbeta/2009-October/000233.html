Good news everyone!

We just released Beta 3 of MonoVS!

The release notes are here:
http://mono-project.com/ReleaseNotes_MonoVS

-- Updating Windows --

You can download the new .msi with your credentials here:
http://go-mono.com/monovs/

(Close all instances of VS2008 and install)

-- Updating Linux --

There is not a new VM to download for this beta.

To get the updated Linux server piece, double-click the "Update MonoVS
Server" icon, enter your credentials, and follow the instructions.


Thanks for your feedback so far, and let us know what works or doesn't
with the new Beta!
