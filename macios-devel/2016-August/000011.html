Bonjour,

If you’re using our first Xcode 8 preview [1] you might have noticed that updating to beta 4 cause issues with the simulator support (but device support seems fine).

This is a known issue [2] and will require updates to both Xamarin.iOS and the IDEs (e.g. Xamarin Studio and our VS support). Our second preview will include those fixes along with updated and new API.

In the mean time you should consider holding to beta 3 until newer builds are available (or switch to device-only development).

Regards,
Sebastien
