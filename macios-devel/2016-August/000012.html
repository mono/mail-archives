Bonjour,

We’re happy to announce that we started publishing Xamarin.iOS (and XM) packages from our main development branches!

You can access the packages from our wiki [1]. Right now we have builds for `master`, `xcode8` (which is the most active this summer) and should soon have packages for `cycle8` (successors to what we offer in our alpha channel).

Please note that those packages are non-validated (e.g. no QA) as they come directly from the build bots that produce the packages (no filter). We offer them *as-is* as they can be useful for our open source contributors and for customers that want to confirm a specific bug fix.

Regards,
Sebastien

[1] https://github.com/xamarin/xamarin-macios/wiki#continuous-builds
