In case anyone is bothered by this bug, there was a very simple workaround in
my case.

Before:

bitmap.SetResolution(96/zoom,96/zoom);
gfx.DrawImage(bitmap, 0, 0);

After:

gfx.DrawImage(bitmap, 0, 0, bitmap.Width*zoom, bitmap.Height*zoom);
