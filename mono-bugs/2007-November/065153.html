The Page Turn Application
(http://silverlight.net/samples/1.0/Page-Turn/default.html) but also PopFly
seem to not always perform the "leave" animations.

In Popfly this happens when the trail of objects does not retract, Page-turn
might be a simpler test case that has the same problem.   

To reproduce:
* Load the site
* Click on the bottom right icon that shows two pages [ | ]
* Wait for the images to show up
* Click on an image and as it animates, quickly move left or right.

If you wait, it will properly vanish when you move, but if you move before the
animation is complete, the image will stay on the screen.

The sample can be downloaded from:
http://silverlight.net/samples/1.0/Page-Turn/PageTurn.zip
