The shee-bang is wrong. Change it to `#!/bin/sh' or at least `#!/usr/bin/env
bash'... On FreeBSD /bin/bash does not exist. There are quite a lot of shell
scripts with a hardcoded bash location...

You can locate them using:
grep -rm1 '^#! */bin/bash' .
