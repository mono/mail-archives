This is implemented in our asf demuxer (ffmpeg's demuxer doesn't support
reading non-audio/video stream/data, and never will).

do
"export MOON_DEMUXER=true"
before running firefox, this will cause our demuxer to be used (in which case
the lyrics do show up).
