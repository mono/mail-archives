Description of Problem:
The registration confirmations are being sent from a mailserver without proper
dns entries.

Steps to reproduce the problem:
1. register using MonoDoc 
2. Check the mailserver logfiles from your mailserver


Actual Results:
The email is rejected because of a missing dns entry.

Expected Results:
The email being accepted (with a correct dns entry).

How often does this happen? 
Always.

Additional Information:
Work around: explicitly allow this mailserver in your mailserver configuration
