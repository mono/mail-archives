The svn protocol does much better, and http consumes a lot of bandwidth.


Some other ideas... why do we have anonsvn.mono-project.com in the first place?
 Why not just provide read only access from mono-cvs over the svn protocol?

Or, have viewcvs on anonsvn.mono-project.com?

But, it would also help to provide readonly anonymous access over svn:: if only
to help out monobuild so it bypasses the ssh overhead, and also doesn't have to
deal with the 30 minute delay.

That way monobuild could run from a read-only checked out tree and we wouldn't
have to worry about checking in as 'builder'.
