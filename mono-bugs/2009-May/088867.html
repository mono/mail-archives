MDB does not work when Intel XD bit is disabled in BIOS settings.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
AMI BIOS (Mainboard: ECS Elitegroup P35T-A, CPU: Intel Core 2 Quad Q6600):

Disable "Intel XD bit" under "Advanced Options" in BIOS settings.
