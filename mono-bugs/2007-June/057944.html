+
+1. Draw a solid background.
+2. Draw a white rectangle with alpha:

+            TextureBrush tb = new TextureBrush(bm_for_brush);
+            g.FillRectangle(tb, ...);

+Actual Results:

+The colors are different.

+Expected Results:
