Description of Problem:

mono-2.4.2p1 on opensuse 11.1 x86_64

When creating a new add, the link for the newly created ad appears in the list
of ads, however, when viewing the ad itself, no data fields are shown
(location, description, price, etc).

To reproduce:

1. login (test, test)
2. click "Post an Ad" in the top menu bar
3. Select "Electronics"
4. check "For Sale"
   fill in Title, Description, Price, Location.
5. Click "Finish"
6. Click "Go to My Ads page"

The new ad will appear in the lsit of adds

7. Click on the link for your ad

The next page is blank with the exception of a ':' however, all the information
for the ad should be displayed.

This works with mono-2.4 so this is a regression.
