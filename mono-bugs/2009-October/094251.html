Steps to remove the 2.0 RPM so that the lower versioned RPM can be used
instead:
1. In the "Edit Appliance" area of MonoVS, click on the "Software" tab
2. Click on "Upload and manage RPMs"
3. Click on the 'X' on the same line as the 2.0 RPM
4. Click "Back to the software overview"
5. In the "Search for software" section, search for your RPM again
6. Notice that this time it comes up with the 1.0 version of the RPM (since the
2.0 version is no longer in your personal repo).
