Yep, the method simply hasn't been implemented yet.  We should not have any
missing methods when we release 2.0.  Hopefully they will be implemented and
functioning, but at the very least they will be stubbed out so that your app
won't crash.  It just wouldn't actually disable the control.

http://mono.ximian.com/class-status/2.0/.
