--- Comment #1 from Jonathan Pobst <jpobst at xamarin.com> 2011-08-22 23:18:12 UTC ---
I think you misunderstood the steps.

When you do Start Emulator, it just starts the emulator.  You need to leave the
emulator running and then go back and choose that emulator in the Select Device
window.

That will start deploying your app to the emulator and launch your app.  

Basically you are going to start the emulator and leave it running as long as
you are developing (because booting it up takes forever).
