Description of Problem:
If you set the FormBorderStyle-Property of a Form to
FormBorderStyle.FixedDialog, the window-icon in the upper left corner should be
invisible. But it is visible and you can click it to open the system-menu.

Steps to reproduce the problem:
1. Create a new Form and that the Poperty FormBorderStyle =
System.Windows.Forms.FormBorderStyle.FixedDialog;
2. Launch it: On Windows XP (.NET 3.5) the window-icon is invisible, on Linux
it is visible.
