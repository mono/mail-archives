+crashes.

+1. Compile attached sample using make
+2. mono mono-service.exe TestServiceThreaded.exe
+In another shell
+3. kill `cat /tmp/TestServiceThreaded.exe.lock`
+4. less /var/log/messages
+
+Actual Results:
+SIGILL and exception.
+
+Expected Results:
+Service terminates normally.
+

+Always.
