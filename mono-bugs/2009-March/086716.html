Description of Problem:
If you try to post an ad in ClassifiedsStarterKit without logging in, then the
ad will fail to post with an obscure message. What should be happening is that
between steps 2 and 3 below, it should force you to login.


Steps to reproduce the problem:
1. Make sure you're logged out of CSK
2. Click "Post an ad"
3. Click "Auto"
4. Fill out Title, Description, Price and Location
5. Click "Finish"
6. Notice the "Error 23503" message
7. If you login, it adds the post without an error.


Actual Results:
doesn't have you login, and errors

Expected Results:
it should force you to login

How often does this happen? 
every time
