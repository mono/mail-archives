+Summary: ComboBox: different ComboBox height on Linux/MWF and Windows/SWF and other problems
+
+If you take a close look at the attached screenshot you can see that the
+height of a MWF ComboBox is different to the height of a SWF ComboBox.
+(SWF ComboBox has one pixel more aka is one pixel higher).
+
+Another problem is, that the selection/focus (filled) rectangle in a SWF
+ComboBox is different to the one in a MWF ComboBox. It has a (white) one
+pixel frame at the top, left and right side whereas MWF hasn't one. (SWF
+ownerdrawn ComboBoxes have that 1 pixel border on all sides).
+
+Next problem is, that ownerdrawn ComboBoxes should honour that area and not
+draw over that bottom 1 pixel frame as MWF does it now.
+
+And another problem is, that the text control inside a ComboBox is just to
+big/high so it draws over the inner bottom border line (but maybe that is
+related to the wrong ComboBox height).
+
+Changing the internal border style to Fixed3D can't be used because on
+windows a ComboBox with a default size of 121,21 returns 121,21 for
+ClientRectangle.Width and .Height.
