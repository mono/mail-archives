Pressing the TAB-key twice to show completions on "System." causes the
interactive csharp command line utility to crash.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Start csharp in a shell window.
2. Enter System. (System followed by a period, nothing else)
3. Press the TAB-key twice.
