Image of ListView with groups behaves poorly

Steps to reproduce:

1. Create a ListView with View=View.Tile or Details (I've tried both)
2. Add groups to the list
3. Add enough entries in various groups so as to require a scroll bar.
4. Scroll up and down

Actual Results:
Entries flicker or altogether disappear as scroll bar moves up and down. Unable
to click on entries. Some entries may vanish when clicked on - others may
appear below.

Expected Results:
Items should be grouped under their respective headers and scroll smoothly with
the scroll bar.

This makes the control unusable with groups.
