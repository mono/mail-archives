The repro for bug #325037 (see gert/interactive/bug82370 in SVN) shows a
combobox with a drop down box that is not correctly drawn.

After checking the "Show Through" checkbox, the combobox does not close again
and the text cursor is constantly flashing. At that time it is not possible to
move focus to another control until the "Show Through" checkbox is unchecked
again.

I can reproduce this on both X11 and Win32. On Windows, the checkbox also is
not correctly drawn when checked.
