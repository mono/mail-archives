Test Case

If you have 2 MDI child windows, clicking on the bottom one does not bring it
to the front, though it does give it focus.

This regressed in r91008, a change to Control.

To reproduce with attached test case:
- Click File->New.
- Click File->New
- Child 2 will be on top of Child 1.
- Click the titlebar of Child 1.

Child 1 should come to the top and be on top of Child 2.
