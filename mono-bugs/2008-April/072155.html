The output of mono-find-requires has changed in mono 1.9 by not adding new
lines at certain places. Here's a diff of the mono-find-requires output for
gecko-sharp between mono 1.2.6 and mono 1.9:

--- old 2008-04-15 12:47:17.000000000 +0200
+++ new 2008-04-15 12:47:06.000000000 +0200
@@ -1,8 +1,8 @@
 libatk1.0_0
 libglib2.0_0
 libgtk+-x11-2.0_0
-libmozilla-firefox2.0.0.12
-libmozilla-firefox2.0.0.13
+libmozilla-firefox2.0.0.12 libmozilla-firefox2.0.0.13
 mono(glib-sharp) = 2.12.0.0
 mono(gtk-sharp) = 2.12.0.0
+mono(gtk-sharp) = 2.12.0.0 libglib2.0_0
 mono(mscorlib) = 1.0.5000.0


There should be a newline before libglib2.0_0, else the filtering in Mandriva's
rpm scripts is broken.
