Description of Problem:
If you click submit twice on the dbpage1 test, the results disappear and never
come back. I think this is probably a bug in the testcase and not in asp.net
(thus the minor severity).

This happens under both xsp and xsp2, and happens on both 1.9.0 and 1.9.1p3, so
this isn't a regression.


Steps to reproduce the problem:
1. switch user to root
2. cd /usr/lib/xsp/test
3. run xsp
4. open a browser to http://<server>:8080/webcontrols/dbpage1.aspx
5. under "Person Filter" put %s%
6. click the "submit" button
7. notice that the results are there like they should be
8. click the "submit" button again
9. notice that the results have disappeared


Actual Results:
the results disappear.


Expected Results:
the results should stay there.


How often does this happen? 
every time.
