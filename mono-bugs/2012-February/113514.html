System.InvalidOperationException: egl.EglMakeCurrent failed: 0x3009

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Create a new OpenGL Application project
2. Deploy on a Ice Cream Sandwich device (Android 4.0), such as Galaxy Nexus or
Nexus S
