--- Comment #1 from Thomas Wiest <twiest at novell.com>  2008-12-15 16:48:37 MST ---
To be clear, clicking on the login link always took us to the login page.
However, as stated above, previously to test this, we'd click on the
"Authentication" link instead of the login link. This, once we logged in would
take us to the "logged in" page that we could use to "sign out".

There isn't a way to get to the "sign out" page now (at least we haven't found
one).

So, one of two things needs to happen:
 * Fix the login link to take us to the sign out page by default (preferred).
 * Change the authentication link to take us back to the page.


Also, if you click on the authentication link, you get a list of things.
Clicking on any of them just gives you the link again. I think that's a bug.
