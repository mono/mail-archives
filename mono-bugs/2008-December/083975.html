When a connection is obtained from the pool, its connection is no longer reset.

As a result, temporary tables and settings are not reset. You may even end up
in a different database than you intended.

This regression was introduced in r118704:
http://anonsvn.mono-project.com/viewvc?view=rev&revision=118704
