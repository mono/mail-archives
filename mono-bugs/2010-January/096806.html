> At what line? I see AC_PROG_CXX but not AC_CHECK_TOOL(CXX, g++, g++).
> For gcc, an extra check is done besides AC_PROG_CC.

It turns out that, according to the full thread in
http://lists.gnu.org/archive/html/bug-autoconf/2002-04/msg00056.html
AC_PROG_CXX is expected not to bomb out if after AC_PROG_CC, as awkward this
can be (as it's not symmetric to the behavior of AC_PROG_CC in the first
place).

Thus, am changing this from RESOLVED->INVALID to RESOLVED->FIXED. Addressed in
r150055 and r150077.
