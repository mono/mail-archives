This is a PPC-specific Mono runtime bug:

ERROR:mono-debug.c:439:allocate_data_item: assertion failed:
(table->current_chunk->current_offset == table->current_chunk->allocated_size)

You can work around it by removing "--dbug" from the monodevelop startup
script, MonoDevelop.app/Content/MacOS/monodevelop
