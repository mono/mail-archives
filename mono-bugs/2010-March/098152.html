--- Comment #1 from Sebastian Bertho <ca-novell at bertho.de> 2010-03-11 11:19:06 UTC ---
At least on Ubuntu this is a issue with fontconfig:
fontconfig resolves the font name "MS Sans Serif" or "Microsoft Sans Serif" to
"Luma", which is a font of the "thai"-Package and does not support german
umlauts.

So removing the ttf-thai-tlwg package resolves this problem for me.
