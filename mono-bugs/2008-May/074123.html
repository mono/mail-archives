Are these rpms from the build service or from mono-project downloads?

Looking at 1.9.1 from the build service (from factory distribution):

wberrier at wberrier:~> rpm -q --requires mono-core | grep modu
libgmodule-2.0.so.0()(64bit)  

And from 1.9.1 that we ship from mono-project.com:

rpm -qp --requires mono-core-1.9.1-2.novell.i586.rpm  | grep modu
libgmodule-2.0.so.0

So whatever package that profiles gmodule should get pulled in automatically
via the automatic rpm generation.
