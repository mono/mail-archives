With MonoTouch we had the option to simply build and upload the app to the
device without actually debugging or running the app from the IDE.  Sometimes I
just want to push out a new build onto the device and manually run it from the
device.

Right now release mode prevents debugging from starting, but it still launches
the app and even waits for it to close.  

I don't propose that either option be removed as they exist, but a 3rd build
config, or just menu option to "Upload to Device..." should be included
