actually, it's a couple things. one, because android likes lowercase, i'm
suggesting that the default project template folder name for "Resource" should
be "resource." just so people get kind of used to it. like i said, it's not a
big deal.

the second thing, which i've bugged elsewhere, is that when you build with
upper case letters in your resources, it errors. sure, making that more of a
visible error goes a ways to helping idenitify it for people, but i still think
just allowing them to do whatever and then lowercasing it on the build is a
better idea. it's just a much more pleasant experience.
