+------- Additional Comments From duncan@ximian.com  2004-06-08 14:12 -------
+Sangu -  On Fedora Core 2, gtk-sharp depends on the gtkhtml package 
+shipped as part of the distribution, which is version 3.0.10.
+
+If you updated your version of gtkhtml without keeping an older
+version behind, then your configuration will not supported, as there
+are too many variations.
+
+Since we do not package gtkhtml-3.1.x for FC2, your current
+configuration is not supported. To fix this manually, you'll have to
+manually edit gtkhtml-sharp.dll.config, in
+/usr/lib/mono/gac/gtk-sharp/, to point to the correct version of the
+library.
