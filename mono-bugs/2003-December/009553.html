+
+Console.WriteLine("Backspace\b");
+Console.Write("Backspace\b");
+Console.WriteLine();
+Console.WriteLine("Backspace\b ");

+Backspace
+Backspac
+Backspac

+
+Backspac
+Backspac
+Backspac

+Always

+specific, but also reproducable in .NET, thus probably being a
+specification bug.

+not filed a bug report at microsoft.
