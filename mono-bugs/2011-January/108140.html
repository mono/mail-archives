Attached test case demonstrates problem.

If after opening a toolstrip menu, the mouse is hovered over a sub menu on that
toolstrip menu, clicking on another item (not it the sub menu) causes Keyboard
capture to remain with the sub menu.



Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Run Attached Test case.
2. Click Win 
3. Click "Menu Item 3" (moving mouse down the middle of the menu items, not
around the menu) 
4. Attempt to type.
Actual Results:  
Nothing is typed into the bottom Textbox.

Expected Results:  
Typed text should appear in the bottom TextBox.
