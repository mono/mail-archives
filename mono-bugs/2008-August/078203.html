On X11, press and holding the mouse button immediately fires the DragEnter
event and the DragOver events. On MS, the DragEnter event is only fired after
you've moved the mouse pointer (for more than SystemInformation.DragSize) or
when you release the mouse button without having moved the mouse pointer.
