If you want to add some sounds and photos as examples, you can do it by:

1- In a running system, add them and import them to the banshee and f-spot.
Upload to the overlay section the following (as tar.gz):
    /home/tux/.config/banshee-1
    /home/tux/.cache/banshee-1
    /home/tux/.gconf/apps/banshee-1
    /home/tux/.gnome2/f-spot
    /home/tux/.gconf/apps/f-spot

2- Upload those sound and photos files using the overlay section to the same
location you had.

3- Set the screensaver as showing the photos on your folder. This will change
your .gconf/apps/gnome-screensaver/%gconf.xml . Upload this file, too.

4- Go to your nautilus and add as bookmarks the photos and sounds folder. You
can also edit by hand the
nautilus/metafiles/file:%2F%2F%2Fhome%2Ftux%2FDocuments.xml file. Upload it to
the overlay section.

5- Create a README.legal with the legal stuff of your photos and sounds and
upload it to the /home/tux/Desktop
