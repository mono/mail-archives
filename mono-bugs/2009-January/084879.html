In order to customize the applications menu using the suse studio, upload a
customized "applications.xbel" to the folder
"/home/tux/.local/share/gnome-main-menu/" on the "overlay files" section.
Replace tux for your username.

This way you can remove the applications on the "recent applications" as well.
