Currently, the signing task produces an intermediate -Signed.apk and a
-Signed-Aligned.apk after the alignment step.  Since an unaligned signed apk is
useless, and -Signed-Aligned is a little TMI/wordy, we should make the
-Signed.apk the result of the task and use an intermediate filename for the
unaligned apk and remove it after alignment.
