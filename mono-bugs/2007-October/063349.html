When you start playing, in Moonlight you will notice sometimes when you click
on a destination for your player that two piano notes sound rapidly, one of
them interrupting the other.  

This seems to be caused by the computer response to your move triggering the
audio before the animation for your move is done.

To reproduce:

1. Choose a long destination for the cowboy, and listen how the second piano
note sounds before the cowboy makes it to the target location.
