--- Comment #2 from Marek Habersack <mhabersack at novell.com> 2010-09-01 23:30:21 CEST ---
This can happen when two instances of xsp or mod_mono use the same application
virtual and physical paths. Once any of the two instances is restarted, it will
clean the compilation/cache directory, pulling assemblies from under the other
application.

Another reason might be that the operating system hosting the service cleans
the /tmp directory periodically from a cronjob.
