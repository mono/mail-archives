Description of Problem:

mono-x-2.4.2.3 on SLES-10 i586

Graffiti crashes when purging a story after it's been deleted.

To reproduce:

1) Untar the attached graffiti file and run ./setup_test.sh
2) create a story in Graffiti
3) Delete the story from the "Published" sub-tab of the "Posts" tab
4) Go to the "Deleted" sub-tab in the "Posts" tab and click "Destroy"

Graffiti crashes at this point with the following error: "Error: Thread was
being aborted"
