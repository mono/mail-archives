Repro

A menu item that is a combo-box or a text-box is unusable on Mono both Win and
Linux.

A) Once the menu is dropped-down, if the mouse crosses the space either side of
a combo-/text-box then the menu closes immediately.

B) When the actual combo-/text-box is clicked, the menu closes immediately.

C) In the combo-box case, if the click was to the 'drop-down' arrow them again
the menu closes, but the combo-items remain on screen.

There are slight differences between the Win and Linux behaviour, Linux doesn't
show A), instead only a click on the empty space makes the menu close -- it
should do nothing, or give combo-/text-box focus(?).
