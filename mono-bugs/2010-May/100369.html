Here's a workaround in the meantime:

--- BEGIN csharp-shell ---
#!/bin/bash
WC=`wc -l < $1`
let "WC -= 1"
TEMP=$RANDOM.tmp
tail -n $WC $1 > $TEMP
csharp $TEMP
rm $TEMP
--- END csharp-shell ---


--- BEGIN test.sh ---
#!/usr/bin/env ./csharp-shell
System.Console.WriteLine("Hello World");
--- END test.sh ---

Place csharp-shell in /usr/bin or /usr/local/bin if you like.
