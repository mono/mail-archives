--- Comment #3 from Jonathan Pobst <jpobst at novell.com> 2011-03-03 19:27:06 UTC ---
Wow, I've never seen anything like this.  Let me ask some questions to get the
full workflow of what you are doing.  I keep finding there are alternate ways
of doing stuff that I didn't account for.

- You create a new project from the template.
- How are you triggering running it? (F5, Debug->Start Debugging, etc)
- Are you deploying to an emulator or device?
- Does the app successfully build and deploy?
- Does the app successfully run?
- Will it stop at a breakpoint?
- How are you "stopping" debugging? (Closing app on device, Debug->Stop
Debugging, etc)
- Are there any error messages in the Android log? [1]
- If the app successfully deployed, can you go run it on the device without VS
and it properly works?
- What version do you have installed? (Help->About Visual Studio->MonoDroidVS)

Sorry for the barrage of questions!

[1] http://monodroid.net/Documentation/Guides/Android_Debug_Log
