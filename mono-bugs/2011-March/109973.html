On iOS 4.3 on iPad in landscape mode a UIPopOverController did not come back to
its original position after dismissing the keyboard. It happends when Keyboard
is dismissed by code or by tapping the dismiss key.

Monotouch 3.99.11 Alpha

iOS 4.3 release Version

iPad


Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1.Use iPad in landscape mode
2. Build a modal popover whitch has a big size and whitch would be resized when
you bring the keyboard into front.
3. dismiss the keyboard
Actual Results:  
The popover did not come back to its original position and did not have the
right size. It stays in the resized and moved position.

Expected Results:  
The popOver should be resized and moved to its original place to see it in a
full view in a normal way.
