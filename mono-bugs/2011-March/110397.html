The rectangle passed to DrawToBitmap is supposed to be the *target* rectangle
only. The upper left hand corner of the target rectangle in DrawToBitmap needs
to correspond to the pixels at Control.Location in the real control. In other
words, the current Mono behavior is only correct when both (a) the target
rectangle has a Location of the origin, and (b) [as far as clipping is
concerned, so (b) only affects some controls] when the Control.Location is at
the origin.
