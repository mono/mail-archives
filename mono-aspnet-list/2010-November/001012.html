https://github.com/mono/mono/commit/4f3157b972885abc03137a9c9a6248b8832e7bf7

https://github.com/mono/mono/blob/4f3157b972885abc03137a9c9a6248b8832e7bf7/data/net_2_0/DefaultWsdlHelpGenerator.aspx
