Web parts are not supported niche used almost only by Microsoft
and either way you cannot use MS Report Viewer control anyways.

You are likely just seeing "number" of the missing members.
There aren't many missing stuff in reality. 2399 are from WebParts
which we don't support, and 1072 (out of 1191 in S.W.Configuration)
are from BrowserCapabilitiesFactory, out of total 4653 missing stuff.
They are just for example.
