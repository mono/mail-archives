Marcelo,

Try to put all aliases inside the virtual host element:

<VirtualHost *:80>
         ServerName elomeno.com
	ServerAlias www.elomeno.com www.elomeno.com.br elomeno.com.br
         Include /etc/apache2/vhosts.d/elomeno.include
</VirtualHost>
