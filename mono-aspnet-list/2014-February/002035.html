This error you mentioned in your blog post:

System.UnauthorizedAccessException Access to the path
> "/Library/Frameworks/Mono.framework/Versions/3.2.6/etc/mono/registry" is
> denied.


Is caused by Microsoft.Web.Infrastructure.dll trying to access the
registry. Simply delete it from your bin directory and Mono will use its
bundled version instead.
