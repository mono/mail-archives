Hi everyone!

Next year, the Mono Project is going to have a devroom at FOSDEM (6/7
February in Brussels). We are very excited about this opportunity, and
we want you to give a talk about your cool Mono project, hack, idea,
experiences and anything else you feel people should know about Mono.

We want to make this a fun room and we want to accomodate all kinds of
talks, so our timeslots will be very dynamic - you tell us how much
time you'll need, and we'll do our best to slot you in!

We have the whole Sunday to schedule, so submit your talk proposals now!
