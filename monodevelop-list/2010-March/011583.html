On Thu, Mar 25, 2010 at 10:24 AM, Stefano Canepa <sc at linux.it> wrote:
> Hi all,
>   I have a little but annoing problem with MD formatting. I
> changed my preference for code formatting and code snippets to have
> open braces every time in a new line and tab as two spaces.
>   When I reform a source file MD keeps changing braces position.
> What I'm doing the wrong way?

Formatting options are "policies", automatically stored in the
solutions to ensure consistency in style across users & machines (and
they can be overridden per project, in which case they are stored in
the project).

Changes made in the "default policies" dialog only affect new
solutions, or solutions without policies.
