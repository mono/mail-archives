I was working on porting MonoDevelop to windows. Part of the issue is
the dependencies on Gnome libs and GnomeVFS.. all of it fairly simple to
get around. If I spent a good week and a half at it then I'm sure I
could have it working to some extent. I don't have the space or a fast
enough machine to dedicate to building the dependencies over and over
like I was doing anymore. I have gtksourceview working on win32 as well
but its not tested to well. I'm not sure but MD might use VTE as well. I
ported gtkembedmoz and gecko# all to get cracking on MD.=20
