Theoretically, support for Chinese texts is not a big issue in MonoDevelop as 
the Mono platform supports the UTF-8 character set required for typesetting 
Chinese (both traditional, and simplified).

What you do have to check on your platform, is the existence of an appropriate 
Chinese font. Also, all computers you're gonna deploy your application on must 
have such a font installed.

About a Chinese translation of MonoDevelop, please don't ask me. :-X
