On Tue, Apr 21, 2009 at 8:07 AM, Kevron Rees <tripzero at nextabyte.com> wrote:
> I'm using a notebook tab in my mobile app.  The default tabs are too
> small for use on a mobile system though (in vehicle), is there anyway to
> enlarge them?

Select the labels on the tabs, check the "user markup" property, and
put <larger></larger> around the labels' text (google GMarkup for more
tags you can use). You can also remove the label widget and insert a
container, so you could pack images in there too.
