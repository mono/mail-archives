On Thu, Apr 23, 2009 at 4:31 AM, georgius <dannypaiva at gmail.com> wrote:
>
> Is there another way of adding references on MonoDev
> apart from right-clicking on the 'references' item in a
> 'solution' panel? (I'm using a Mac and the "normal"
> combination of ctrl-key plus trackpad button click is not
> working...)

Quick workaround: Edit->preferences>keybindings, add a keybinding for
the "edit references" command.

Also, I believe recent Macs have support for true right-click with
some kind of multi-finger tap on the trackpad.

> On Visual, of course, I could access it in several different
> ways, including the 'project' menu...

You can insert the command into the Project menu using a simple
.addin.xml file. The mailing list have an example of this I posted a
while back.

For the next release, I'll add it to the project menu. There's also a
patch in bugzilla for opening the dialog when double-clicking on the
references folder, and I'll apply that too.
