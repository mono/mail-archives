For now, until someone produces packages for anything other than the 
commercial operating systems, your choices are basically building from 
source or using the outdated version that comes from your distro's repos.

If you look around then there might be an Ubuntu PPA with a newer 
version in, though. Just make sure that you trust the PPA owner!
