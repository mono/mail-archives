On Fri, Oct 22, 2010 at 11:05 AM, Dale Ragan <dale.ragan at sinesignal.com> wrote:
> You will need to do two things:
>
> 1.  When running nunit-console, you will need to pass -framework flag and
> set it to mono-4.0
>
> 2.  Modify nunit-console.exe.config by adding under the <configuration>
> section:
>  <startup>
>    <requiredRuntime version="v4.0.####" /> ### = released version of the
> .net 4.0 runtime.  I'm not sure what it is off the top of my head
>  </startup>
>
>  and adding under <configuration><runtime>
>
>  <legacyUnhandledExceptionPolicy enabled="1" />
>  <loadFromRemoteSources enabled="true" />
>
> HTH,
>
> Dale
