The fact that the root of the stack trace is a segfault in
gtk_moz_embed_render_data indicates that this is most likely a problem
with the unmanaged GtkMozEmbed library, which is part of Mozilla. It
may be that reinstalling Mozilla (perhaps a newer version) will help
by fixing the read permissions on all of its components, assuming
that's where the problem is. Do other GtkMozEmbed-based programs (e.g.
Epiphany) work?
