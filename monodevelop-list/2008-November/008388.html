On Thu, Nov 6, 2008 at 12:01 AM, Brett Senior <brett_senior at yahoo.com.au> wrote:
> Today I downloaded the latest MD from SVN and took the defaults in terms of
> the profile.  It compiled and installed OK - but when I try to run it - I
> get the following error in an 'unhandled exception' dialog.  Any ideas what
> is wrong ?
>
>
> System.InvalidOperationException: Type
> 'MonoDevelop.AspNet.ProjectFolderNodeBuilderExtension' not found in add-in
> 'MonoDevelop.AspNet,1.9.1'

The addin version numbers have been updated, and the addin db appears
to be out of sync. and re-running MD.

If you're using "make run", just rebuild: "make clean; make". If it's
installed (though I recommend against this for unstable versions)
remove the files in ~/.config/MonoDevelop/addin*.

Then re-run MD, and it'll regenerate the addin DB.
