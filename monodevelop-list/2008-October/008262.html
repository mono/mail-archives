> /usr/bin/mono checking for gmcs... no configure: error: Can't find

You need to install gmcs (and probably a bunch of other mono development
packages).  You can get most of these with `sudo apt-get build-dep
monodevelop`, but you're going to have to get used to seeing configure
errors that indicate something is missing, then figuring out which
packages provide the missing stuff.

Usually you can figure this out by searching in apt or google.
