reference to Mono.Addins.dll, eventhough it does have 
Mono.Addins.Setup.dll properly referenced.

-r:/opt/mono/lib/mono/mono-addins/Mono.Addins.Setup.dll
MONO_CAIRO_CFLAGS =

-r:/opt/mono/lib/pkgconfig/../../lib/mono/1.0/Mono.Cairo.dll
