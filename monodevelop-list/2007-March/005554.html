> I cant seem to get a 'Bind to Field' on the new monodevelop!

You don't need it with new projects, as it now uses partial classes to
bind everything automatically. Old projects will still need "Bind to
field" unless you convert them to the partial class model.

More info and details of converting are at
http://www.monodevelop.com/Release_notes_for_MonoDevelop_0.13#New_features_in_the_GTK.23_designer
