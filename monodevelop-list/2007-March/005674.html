Hi Folks,

NUnit 2.4 is now available in final form. Lots of changes took place during
the Betas and even after the first release candidate. We recommend you
upgrade to it.

If you haven't been following the Betas and RCs, you may be surprised at the
scope of the changes. You can review them all in the release notes at
http://nunit.org/?p=releaseNotes&r=2.4 or
http://nunit.com/nunit/?p=releaseNotes&r=2.4.

NUnit 2.4 is available for download from Sourceforge and from the nunit.org
and nunit.com sites. Note that the files you want all contain -r2- in their
name. The version of the NUnit assemblies for this release is 2.4.0.2.

Charlie
