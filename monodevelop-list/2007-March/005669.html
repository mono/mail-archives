Hi All,

I just released NUnit 2.2.10, planned to be the final release in the 2.2
series. It fixes a few bugs but is otherwise compatible with NUnit 2.2.9.
See the release notes at http://nunit.org/?p=releaseNotes&r=2.2.10. Download
it at http://nunit.org/?p=download.

If you're planning to switch to NUnit 2.4, I suggest waiting a day rather
than upgrading to 2.2.10. But if you expect to stay with the 2.2 version for
a while, this is for you.

Charlie
