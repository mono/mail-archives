> . When I try to start it though I get the following
> error :
> 
>    monodevelop
> 
> ** (./MonoDevelop.exe:31461): WARNING **: The
> following assembly referenced from
> /usr/lib/mono/gac/gnome-sharp/2.6.0.0__35e10195dab3c99f/gnome-sharp.dll
> could not be loaded:
>      Assembly:   glib-sharp    (assemblyref_index=0)
>      Version:    2.4.0.0
>      Public Key: 35e10195dab3c99f

> variable, or in the location of the executing assembly
> (/usr/lib/mono/gac/gnome-sharp/2.6.0.0__35e10195dab3c99f).

You have a mixed *-sharp-2.5.x and *-sharp-2.3.x install. Either
downgrade gnome-sharp to the 2.3.x stuff, or use all the *-sharp-2.5.x
stuff. *-sharp-2.3.x are the suggested packages, as the 2.5.x stuff is
hard masked due to upstreams request it not be packaged.
