Actually, here's an issue for y'all to consider:

MD was started in order to be a C# IDE on linux. However, with the 
advent of Boo, and perhaps other languages (IronPython, etc) which will 
target IL, is it right/safe to associate C# with monodevelop in the logo?

Thinking forward, do developers want MD to be "The C# IDE" which does 
other languages or "The Mono IDE" which is primarily focused on C#? It's 
a question of position.
