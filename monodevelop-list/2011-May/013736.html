Hi,

We haven't done much work on error reporting, so help on this is
welcome. I don't know NBug, so I can't tell if it is suitable or not for
MonoDevelop. In general, we try to reduce dependencies on third party
libraries, unless we can really use all the functionality they provide.
For example, we replaced Log4Net by our own logging service, which is
much more simple and does just what we need. I don't know how NBug fits
here. Maybe NBug support could be provided as an add-in?
