AFAIK trolltech was trying to integrate Qt's main loop with glib's main
loop (see
http://doc.trolltech.com/4.2/qt4-2-intro.html#desktop-integration) which
should easy every try of integrating any designer for qt4 inside
monodevelop.

Also, I've never seen Nokia interested on mono, and I have never seen
any mono por for their mobile platform. Maybe, what you mean is mono for
Internet Tablets (N8x0), but Nokia never contributed to that port. It
was done by the comunity.

I don't really think MonoDevelop should get much benefit from
integrating Qt designer or whatever. Gtk has good foundations and should
provide all needed functionality provided by Qt. To me, it makes more
sense if someone ports the gui part of monodevelop to Qt.
