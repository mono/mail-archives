Will file a bug tomorrow, it had something to do with the format of
<ProjectGuid> element in my templates. I was using
<ProjectGuid>12345-... and had the issues. Once I switched to
<ProjectGuid>{12345-...}</ProjectGuid> - note the curly braces,
everything is working as expected. I am not sure where the issue is
though, XBuild, MD or F# addin.
