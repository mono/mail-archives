	Hello, Neil,
First of all, you should understand, that GUI toolkit, that MD uses, is  
not a MD's property. It's just a Gtk-sharp2 with Stetic. So 1st think  
to seek is Gtk-sharp2 docs. But soon you can find it not very detailed.  
Your next step - GTK documentation (gtk-sharp is very close by design  
to it's ancestor).

Reference:
1. http://mono-project.com/Category:Developer_Resource
2. http://www.go-mono.com/docs/index.aspx?link=N%3aGtk
3. http://gtk.org/
