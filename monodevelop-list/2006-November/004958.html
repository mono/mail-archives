Hello,

> In the translation addin I'm working, I'm experimenting with an
> abstraction interface that hides if you are using gettext or
> ms-resources translation support, even loading the
> "translation"-provider at run-time so that you doesn't have a
> hard-reference on Mono.Posix, and can make the translatable app work
> in windows.
> 
> What do you think people?

I think that adding a new layer of abstraction and coping with two
different systems for translations means that you are mostly doubling
your work, and will not be able to focus on getting one right.

I advocate simplicity, I would propose to get the gettext-based version
working first, and working well, and cope with other systems later, as a
refactoring exercise.
