Hi,

 I just ran into this error and figured out how to get stop it from being a
problem, at least for me.
 I went to  http://www.gzip.org/ http://www.gzip.org/  and downloaded gzip
for windows.
 I copied gzip to the bin folder in C:\Program Files\monodevelop.
 Finally, I renamed gzip.exe to zip.exe... 
 and I am not getting this error any more.

 Good luck - hopefully this was the file specified by your particular error
message,

 Dominick
