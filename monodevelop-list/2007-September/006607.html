The Google SOC project for integrating the Mono debugger in MD was not
successfully completed, so there isn't anything usable right now.

However, our plan is to continue working on the debugger. The debugger
add-in will not be included in MD 1.0, but we are likely to do a release
in sync with MD 1.0 or shortly after (MD 1.0 will be released before the
end of the year).
