The monodevelop 0.12 that came with openSUSE 10.2 works fine under
both GNOME and KDE; after adding the Mono repository for openSUSE
10.2, though, I experienced crashes when running monodevelop 0.15 on
KDE.

I notice, however, that running monodevelop as a different user works:
> su - anotheruser
> monodevelop

surprisingly, as long as su - is used, monodevelop works (i.e. instead
of anotheruser, it could have been any user, including the one
currently logged in)

Running monodevelop under GNOME, needless to say, works as well.

The problem seems to involve GTK, leading me to suspect it's some
theming problem. GTK-Qt, perhaps?

Would appreciate any help. Meanwhile, I'll just have to su - to myself
before running MD.

Thanks,
