Hi,

> How easy would it be to import NI (DAQ, WinForms) libraries into
> monodevelop?

As long as the libraries don't contain P/Invoke calls (there is a tool 
on the mono-project.com website that detects them) or relies on 
something that is not purely .NET (such as something from COM), then *in 
theory* the dll can just be imported into your project.

The DAQ element may cause an issue as it more than likely talks at a low 
level to the hardware or OS.
