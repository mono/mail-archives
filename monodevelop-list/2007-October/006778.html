Hi All,

I believe that NUnit has grown about as far as it can without some major
Refactoring. With lots of new ideas that we would like to implement, it's
time  for a restart. I have formed the NUnitV3 group on Google to discuss
what this  will involve. We don't want to do a lot of up-front analysis, but
we do want  to reflect on where we have been and what general approaches we
will be taking  to this new release. 

Currently, the NUnitV3 group is private, in order to keep the signal/noise
ratio at an acceptable level. If you're active in NUnit, Mono or related
development projects, I'd like to have your input. Just request an
invitation at http://groups.google.com/group/nunitv3 to join.

Charlie
