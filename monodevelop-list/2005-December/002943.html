I can see your point. Propose it to the LSB people. The worst that
would happen is that they will ignore it. Next worst thing, they could

Just yesterday I run uppon a Delphi/Kylix community project. It was a
very large project with a great following. It started out just because
somebody complained about having to wait on Borland to do anything.

They might just be awaiting for your idea. If I where them, though, I
wouldn't choose ~/.config/, but rather a new top level directory that
didn't belong to anybody. That way it would save them of the fixed
noise "but why use that one, why not choose mine instead?".
