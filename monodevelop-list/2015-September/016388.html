> I recently installed Monodevelop 5.9 under Linux Lite 2.6 (Ubuntu 14.04
> variant), and can't seem to find the proper monodevelop-debugger-gbd package
> to install for C/C++ debugging.
The monodevelop package from Xamarin
(http://www.mono-project.com/docs/getting-started/install/linux/#debian-ubuntu-and-derivatives)
already installs the dll:
/usr/lib/monodevelop/AddIns/MonoDevelop.Debugger.Gdb/MonoDevelop.Debugger.Gdb.dll

When I go in MonoDevelop to Tools/Add-in Manager, on the Installed Tab
search for gdb, I find: GDB Debugger support version 5.9.5.

I hope this helps,
  Timotheus
