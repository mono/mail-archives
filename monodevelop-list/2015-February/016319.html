Hi folks,

it's a long time ago when I wrote you about the toolbar with 16 buttons
of which only 10 are compiled and run, the remaining 6 ones being shown
in Stetic, but not appearing neither in the ready-to-use window, nor in
the gui.static file generated by Stetic. At least I now do have a
workaround: Drag the button which is eaten up into a position where it
is sorrounded by buttons which don't cause any problems. In my
particular case I had to move buttons #11 to #16 into positions between
#1 and #10. Then re-complile the project, drag the buttons into their
original positions, and compile for a second time. Then they will be
integrated into the solution.

Nevertheless, this remains a bug in Stetic.
