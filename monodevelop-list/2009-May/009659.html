There is no Hg integration in MonoDevelop yet, and AFAIK, there aren't 
packages for Castle Monorail and ActiveRecord, and NHibernate. There are 
packages for NUnit, they're nunit-console, libnunit2.4-cil gnunit2 
monodevelop-nunit, etc. A simple apt-cache search nunit oughta turn up 
everything you need.
