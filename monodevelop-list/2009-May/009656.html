On Sun, May 3, 2009 at 2:28 PM, ylegall <ylegall at gmail.com> wrote:
> How can I use a custom syntax-highlighting scheme?
> I assume I have to edit some xml files somewhere?

Use one of the existing schemes as the basis for your scheme:
http://anonsvn.mono-project.com/viewvc/branches/monodevelop/main/2.0/src/addins/Mono.Texteditor/Styles/

You can then add it with "Add" button in the Syntax Highlighting
options panel (it gets copied into a subdirectory of
~/.config/MonoDevelop/).
