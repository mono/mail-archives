Apparently 'reply' will reply to just the one person you are responding to, while 'reply all' will address to them individually as well to the list. 
I've seen other listserves configured this way. Once one is used to it, it is easier to take a more personal conversation 'off line' (as it were) by hitting just 'reply', and makes one more aware that one is replying to the whole list by hitting 'reply ALL'. It takes some time to get used to it though. ;)
