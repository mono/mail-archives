> Hi folks,
>
> I am using MD 2.2 on a German-language openSuSE 11.2 / KDE 4.3.5 system. To
> make a number of screenshots, however, I need to switch to English language,
> but I don't find a corresponding option in the MD settings. Even changing the
> KDE language to English didn't help.
>
> So how can I switch the MD language to English? Thank you for any helpful
> hints.

The usual Gettext way, the eng vars LANG, LANGUAGE, or LC_MESSAGES etc.

Probably
"LANG=en monodevelop"
will work.

In MD 2.4 you can override the MD UI language from within MD.
