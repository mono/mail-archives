I would recommend building the entire mono stack and install to a
separate location (e.g. /usr/local/mono-2.10.8) coupled with a couple
of scripts. That way you won't risk messing up the distribution's
default install.

http://www.mono-project.com/Parallel_Mono_Environments

Also see this earlier post of mine where I have some notes and scripts
from my own build experiences:
http://permalink.gmane.org/gmane.comp.gnome.mono.devel/38653
