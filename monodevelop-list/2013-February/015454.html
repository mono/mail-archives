http://www.go-mono.com/mono-downloads/download.html

Where you'll see "Latest Stable Version: 2.10.x" and "Beta Version:
3.0.3" downloads.  You want the 2.10.x one.  If you think you already
have the correct framework then maybe try reinstalling it.
