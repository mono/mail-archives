Thanks to David Lechner's suggestions (and his app's packaging which I 
was able to study), I finally managed to grasp how to go about packaging 
my own C# app.  As a result, I created a tutorial on Scribd called 
"Packaging a C# Desktop Application for Debian-based Distros" 
(http://www.scribd.com/doc/160967740/Packaging-a-C-Desktop-Application-for-Debian-based-Distros) 
that's kind of geared towards noobs like myself, if anyone's 
interested.  Interestingly, yesterday, I linked to it from Linux Mint's 
community website (http://community.linuxmint.com/tutorial/view/1373) 
and it's amazing how the very first people to vote on the tutorial 
down-voted it (without so much as a comment), apparently due to it's 
connection to Mono!
