the MonoDevelop servers from our current datacenter to our new datacenter.

problems arise it may take longer.


This move will affect:
 * The main MonoDevelop webiste (www.monodevelop.com)
 * The MonoDevelop download pages

the DNS to propagate before you will be able to access these services
again. This could take up to 12 hours.
