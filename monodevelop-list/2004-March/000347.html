I've helped a user with a gtksourceview-sharp issue, presumably in
Mandrake 9.2, but i don't sure if it belongs to programs not bundled
with Mandrake or if it was some user customization, since the source of

fits better in gtksourceview-sharp.

Anyways here's the issue, detected after installing gtksourceview-sharp
successfully.

$ pwd

he deleted things and worked. It would be helpful for gnome newbies to
have this somewhere (FAQ, INSTALL?) in the gtksourceview-sharp
documentation, webpage, etc.
