> You'll need the following packages (order I compile them in):
>     - libgdiplus
>     - mcs (build when building the mono package)
>     - mono
>     - gtk-sharp
>     - gtksourceview-sharp
>     - gtkmozembed-sharp
>     - gmome-sharp
>     - mono-tools
>     - monodoc
>
> All packages can be found on the mono SVN repository.
> Of course, for packages such as gtk-sharp GTK is required and the
> version available in your distribution should be sufficient.
>
> Note: this is how I do it on Ubuntu when compiling everything from
> source, I guess it will be very similar on Fedora.
