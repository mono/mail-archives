someone else's source code. Note that we did not perform an exhaustive 
comparison, just enough to prove the misappropriation of source code.

The meat: it is fine with us that you release the code under GPL. However, 
it is not that you also sell it as a commercial offering. We expect as a 
courteous act from your side that you take it offline immediately, I don't 
actually want to get into the issues of you offering it in the first place.

Closing words: we do not feel like searching your code and telling you 
where you blatantly used ours or someone else's. We expect that you do the 
right thing.
