Nope. Mono has no WPF support at all, because it is much too big to
implement with our current resources. Perhaps Moonlight will
eventually get close enough to WPF.

MD on Windows targetting .NET could have basic WPF support added quite
easily (project subtype, templates, etc) but all of our contributors
seem to be more interested in doing cross-platform stuff :)
