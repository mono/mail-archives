Hello,

In moonlight there is no UDP support, so it is not possible. In 2.0 or
4.0 profile, you can implement UDP transport. But since there is no
standard UDP binding, you will have to do it by yourself.

Also notice that UDP transport channel will be simplex, unlike HTTP and
TCP streaming ones.

HTH,
Atsushi Eno
