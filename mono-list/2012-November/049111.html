Here is a little more info I got via email from support:

We're not offering Mono Tools for Visual Studio for sale at this time, while we reconfigure the offerings to try to make the tools more useful for the kinds of scenarios we're capable of supporting. (For instance, we'll likely introduce better support for Mac development in an upcoming release). Upcoming product offerings will be made available via Xamarin.com, and the mono-tools.com will be deprecated in favor of that.
