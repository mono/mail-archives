Just wanted to follow up and close this thread. Everything is working now.  I
had initially reported the problem with SWIG in Mono2.x or higher. In fact
my mono1.9 build was 32-bit, because I used the mono-installer. Because the
installer wasn't available for 2.0 I had to compile from source, which
resulted in a 64-bit build.  As soon as I compiled my C++ project and swig
bindings in 64-bit, everything ran fine.
