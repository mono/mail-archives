> Are there any pre-compiled Debian (Jessie packages) for Mono with LLVM
> support? I remember that I had to compile it from source 2 or three years
> ago, and it was rather painful, so I'd like to avoid repeating the

my Debian mono-opt packages are using this configuration:
https://build.opensuse.org/package/view_file/home:tpokorra:mono/mono-opt/debian.rules?expand=1
./configure --prefix=/opt/mono --enable-llvm --with-llvm=/opt/mono

see
https://build.opensuse.org/package/show/home:tpokorra:mono/mono-opt
and
http://software.opensuse.org/download.html?project=home%3Atpokorra%3Amono&package=mono-opt

perhaps this helps,
