#WF information can be found at:
     http://www.icsharpcode.net/OpenSource/SharpWT/default.aspx
there is a developer list for #WT at
     http://lists.ximian.com/mailman/listinfo/sd-mono-port

An alternative, that uses a fairly complete multi-platform gui is
wx.Net, which you can find at:
     http://wxnet.sf.net/
the wx.Net is a wrapper for the wxWindows library, which is available on
about every current platform, and is pretty well respected, and may
better suit what your needs are...
