Yeah, it's that time again!

As usual lots and lots of improvements [1] in this new release including:

* complete nullable type support
* special notation for nullable types (T?) and enumerables (T*)
* improved booish behavior with nicer colors (it should work inside
emacs now :))
* 'else' block for 'for' and 'while loops
* generics, generics, generics
* and generics!

With many many kudos to Avishay Lavie, Cédric Vivier and Marcus Griep!

Cédric deserves special mention for working on all fronts and really
pushing for the release. Superb work!
