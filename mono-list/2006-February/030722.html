You also need to inform System.Web about the .htm extension
being now a page:

web.config:

<system.web>
   <httpHandlers>
     <add verb="*" path="*.htm" type="System.Web.UI.PageHandlerFactory,
System.Web, Version=1.0.5000.0, Culture=neutral, 
PublicKeyToken=b03f5f7f11d50a3a" />
...
