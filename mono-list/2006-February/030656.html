Gtk.ComboBoxEntry in Gtk# 2.4+

The GtkCombo entry in GTK+ is deprecated in favor of ComboBox widgets,
and is thus marked obsolete in Gtk#. Do not use them in new
applications.

Cheers,
Aaron
