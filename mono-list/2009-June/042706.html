Hello Mono community.  :)

The Sims 3 makes non-trivial use of Mono.  Reading the blogs around the interwebs, you guys have already figured that out.  Most of the behavior you guys see in relation to object and Sim interactions is written in C#.

Read a bit of  http://www.gamasutra.com/php-bin/news_index.php?story=23980 for an idea of how the first week sales went.  Go Sims 3!  Yay!

Using Mono was an interesting experience.  It can be a hard sell to management for sure, especially when a commercial license is needed, and for me, talking to lawyers is rarely fun.  I will say Mono is in a much better place now than years ago when we first started using it - you guys have already pulled the version number from the last drop of Mono we took, and you'll notice it is rather old.  :P

Good luck in your efforts TheBlueSky!

thx,
bruce
