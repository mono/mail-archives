The March, 2003 issue of the .NET Developers Journal
(http://www.sys-con.com/dotnet) will be focused on implementations of
the CLI other than Microsoft's CLR for .NET.  For this reason, we are
now accepting proposals for any and all articles dealing with:
 
*         Mono
*         Portable .NET
*         Microsoft's Shared Source CLI (a.k.a. "Rotor")
 
The deadline for proposals is January 6, 2003.  To be considered, simply
fill out the form at:
 
            http://editorial.sys-con.com/proposal.cfm
 
(Make sure to choose ".NET Developers Journal" where it asks for
"Journal Title")
 
Email me directly with any questions.  Until then, I look forward to
hearing from you!
