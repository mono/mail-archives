You need to have the directory of csc.exe in your PATH
For example if SYSTEMROOT points to I:\WINNT use:
export =
PATH=3D/cygdrive/I/WINNT/microsoft.net/framework/v1.0.2914:${PATH}

That should fix your problems. I don't think you need to edit any =
makefiles,
at least I do not have to.

- Martin
