existed and was owned by a different user, so mod-mono-server.exe didn't 
have permission to delete it.

has some problems but they're pretty trivial. You have to add 
--background to the start-stop-daemon start line, and it still dumps a 
bunch of stuff to stdout or stderr that I'm not sure how to supress 
within start-stop-daemon (there's no --redirect-stdio-to-dev-null option).
