Call me crazy, but MasterPages, while being something that would be
helpful, can absolutely be emulated in 1.1.

There is no technical reason the site can't be replicated, extended, etc
in mono right now.

More than anything I would say the issue is one of time.

If it was put to a vote, I'd vote for doing the site in ASP.NET,
however, I think before you figure out how to do it, you should evaluate
the skillset of who is doing it :)
