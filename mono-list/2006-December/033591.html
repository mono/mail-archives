> You should pass a --debug argument to mono when running
> mod-mono-server2.exe
>
> For example change /usr/bin/mod-mono-server2.script to:
>
> #!/bin/sh
> exec /usr/bin/mono $MONO_OPTIONS --debug
> "/usr/lib/mono/2.0/mod-mono-server2.exe" "$@"
>
