Over the last few months, as I think I've mentioned before on this list,
I've been setting up a project for my MSc in Computing that examines
some aspects of Mono and compares them with their equivalents in the MS
.NET Framework.

The experimental part of the project is now up and running at:

http://linmono.uwic.ac.uk/starter

It will be available for the next two weeks, and if any members of this
list would like to participate I should be most grateful.

The ASP.NET application at the URL above is a very short data entry
simulation, the instructions for which can be obtained by clicking on
the appropriate link on the first page you see.

The whole exercise, including reading the instructions, should take no
more than five minutes of anyone's time - although you are most welcome
to participate multiple times should you wish to do so.

Please do not enter any personal details.  Enter the details contained
in the instructions.

The results of the experiment will be published on my blog:

http://peredur.blog.co.uk

... probably some time in August (after I've found out whether or not
I've passed).

Thanks in advance, and thanks again for all the help and support I've
had from this list.  Once the dissertation is in, I'll try to be of more
practical help.

Best regards


Peter
