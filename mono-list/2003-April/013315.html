Hi all,

 

I think I got the right list to post this message. I am currently a
part-time student who is doing a master program in Information
Technologies at Harvard Extension. As part of my graduation, I am
required to do a thesis due to be completed by February 2004. To make
the long story short, I was very enthusiastic and excited when I saw
that a native Java compiler for .NET is needed for the Mono project. I
thought I could write my thesis on the java to IL compiler, and
contribute something back to the open source community as well. Before I
begin researching into it and proposing the project to my thesis
advisor, I am wondering if anyone has started working on this project
yet? Please advise. Let me know. Thanks.

 

 

 

Cheers,

 

Sam
