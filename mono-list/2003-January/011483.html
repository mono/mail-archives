Hey guys,

My Danish friend, Kenneth Christiansen, just reported to me that I
forgot to add our new Visual Basic.NET compiler, mbas, to the RPM
package spec file. I'll try to roll a new RPM by the end of the week to
fix this problem.

In the mean time, if you've installed mono-0.19 via RPM and want to use
the mbas compiler, please download these two files and put them in
/usr/bin.

	* http://primates.ximian.com/~duncan/mbas.exe
	* http://primates.ximian.com/~duncan/mbas

Sorry for the inconvenience and thank you to Kenneth for reporting this
bug.
