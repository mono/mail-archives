Um, pertaining to that charset thing. If you want a permanent solution, 
put this in your charset.alias file (fink puts it at 
/sw/etc/charset.alias)

en_US UTF-8

I imagine if you're having similar problems with another language 
(en_CA) or something, you can do a similar thing.
