We are MediaWiki, the same software as the Wikipedia which is the most
used Wiki system in the world, the one with the most development going
on, and one that is well understood by many.

We just wanted to use what in my opinion is the best Wiki engine for the
Mono site, independently of the language it was written on, and focus
ourselves in what we do best: improve Mono.

Also, since every other group in Novell is using MediaWiki that means
that we have a lot of people that we can ask questions about it.
