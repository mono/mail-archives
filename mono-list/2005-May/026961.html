>>> Kirill <kirillkh@gmail.com> 05/11/05 9:53 PM >>>
1. Open this page:
   http://www.mono-project.com/Downloads 

2. Scroll down until you see your architecture in the left column.

3. Identify your distro and click its link.

4. Download the packages.


The packages are available for SuSe (all versions, except Suse 9 on 
S390), Red Hat 9.0, Fedora Core 3, RHEL 3.0 and 4.0 and MacOS X.
No Debian or Mandrake packages, apparently. Hope it helps.
