The Firebird ADO.NET Data Provider 1.7 Beta 2 is available for download.

http://www.firebirdsql.org/index.php?op=files&id=netprovider

* Release notes can be found here:

http://www.firebirdsql.org/index.php?action=view_item&topic=1102169379
