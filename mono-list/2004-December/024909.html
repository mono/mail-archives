<p  style='mso-margin-top-alt:auto;mso-margin-bottom-alt:auto'><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Sun Rayz Products Inc. (<a href='http://www.infoemail-web.com/080E16061110114A101A140B454115420632011C171319171F131B1E5C111D1F0E434A4A43460E460E400E404647414347450E08.aspx'>OTC: SRPI</a>) today announced the first shipment of FLA USA Orange Juice to B&amp;B U-Save Supermarkets.</font></p>
<p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Sun Rayz Products Inc. introduced their 100% Valencia Orange Juice into U-Save Supermarkets this past week and are expecting reorders from the stores throughout Florida. Michael Goeree, President &amp; C.E.O. of Sun Rayz Products, Inc. Stated &quot;We know we have high quality product, Once the consumers tries it they will be back for a repeat purchase. We believe that the FLA USA line of products will be a continued winner for all involved.</font></p>
<p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><strong>About U-Save Supermarkets:</strong><br>
  U-Save Supermarkets is a wholly owned subsidiary of B&amp;B Corporate Holdings, Inc. Beginning in the 1960's, B&amp;B Corporate Holdings, Inc. initiated a program of &quot;Everyday Low Prices&quot; spearheaded by the opening of U-Save Supermarkets. The response to the new U-Save &quot;Everyday Low Prices&quot; was an instant success. Soon, other B&amp;B Supermarkets were converted to U-Save Supermarkets. Today, B&amp;B Corporate Holdings, Inc. has 26 Handy Food Stores, 11 U-Save Supermarkets and employs over 1,000 associates. In addition, B&amp;B the real estate division of B&amp;B Corporate Holdings leases and manages properties throughout central and southern Florida.</font></p>
<p><font size="2" face="Verdana, Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif"><strong>About Sun Rayz Products, Inc:</strong><br>
  Sun Rayz Products (<a href='http://www.infoemail-web.com/080E16061110114A101A140B454115420632011C171319171F131B1E5C111D1F0E434A4A43460E460E400E404640414145400E08.aspx'>http://www.sunrayzproducts.com</a>) is a marketing and distribution company based in Tampa, Florida which holds the exclusive, worldwide rights to use the &quot;FLA-USA&quot; label in connection with the marketing, sale, and distribution of consumer products related to the Florida lifestyle in the United States as well as internationally under its own brand name, as well as the FLA USA brand. The FLA USA logo (<a href='http://www.infoemail-web.com/080E16061110114A101A140B454115420632011C171319171F131B1E5C111D1F0E434A4A43460E460E400E40464741424A4A0E08.aspx'>http://www.flausa.com</a>) is nationally and internationally recognized as the official symbol of Florida, the number one tourist destination in the world. The Florida Tourism Industry Marketing Corporation allocates approximately $60 to $70 Million annually in promoting Florida tourism worldwide under the FLA USA brand. Sun Rayz Products plans to utilize this recognition with a global sales &amp; marketing strategy, synergistic partnerships, and a nationwide roll-out of consumer goods reflecting Florida's positive lifestyle.</font></p>
