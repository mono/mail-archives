Have you checked to make sure the appropriate permissions are set for the
MySql.Data.dll ?  Also when I installed the MySql.Data.dll connector to the
GAC (using gacutil -I MySql.Data.dll) I had to manually create the symbolic
links for use in v2.0 of the framework.

Ex:

$ ln -s
/usr/lib/mono/gac/MySql.Data/5.0.5.0__c5687fc88969c44d/MySql.Data.dll
/usr/lib/mono/2.0/MySql.Data.dll
