Adriaan van Kekem wrote:
> ok, so its not possible to use apache redirect based on virtualhosts in
> combination with SetHandler mono?

Of course you can. It's just a matter of setting the proper
rewrite rule:

http://httpd.apache.org/docs/2.0/misc/rewriteguide.html ;-)

Handling Request.ServerVariables["HTTP_HOST"] in your login.aspx
is far more portable and easier to implement.
