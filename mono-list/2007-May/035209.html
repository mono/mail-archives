> Also is it possible for you to shed some light on the
> Mono.Unix.Process class and how it works.? 

What exactly do you want to know about UnixProcess?  It's a
System.Diagnostics.Process-like wrapper over the near-equivalent POSIX
system calls, and exhibits some of the limitations of POSIX as well
(e.g. you can't list all processes on the system, you can't wait on an
arbitrary process, only child processes, etc.).

Do you have any specific questions about it that aren't answered by the
documentation?
