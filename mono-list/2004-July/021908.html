On my Mandrake 10.0, I was stuck with the same error (with 
--prefix=/opt/mono)

I made a copy of art/art-sharp.dll and rsvg/rsvg-sharp.dll in 
gtk-sharp-1.0/sample/rsvg

Then I modified sample/rsvg/Makefile.

I changed
assemblies = ../../glib/glib-sharp.dll ../../pango/pango-sharp.dll 
../../atk/atk-sharp.dll ../../gdk/gdk-sharp.dll ../../gtk/gtk-sharp.dll 
../../rsvg/rsvg-sharp.dll

into

assemblies = ../../glib/glib-sharp.dll ../../pango/pango-sharp.dll 
../../atk/atk-sharp.dll ../../gdk/gdk-sharp.dll ../../gtk/gtk-sharp.dll 
rsvg-sharp.dll

After, gtk-sharp built successfully.

Pretty ugly but it worked.
