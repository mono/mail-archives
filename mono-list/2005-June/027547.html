<VirtualHost *:80>
    ...
    <AddMonoApplications test2 "test1.selestial.com:/:/var/www/test1"
    <Location />
       MonoSetServerAlias test2
       SetHandler mono
    </Location>
</VirtualHost>

Now works fine. Although I cannot get both working on the same path.
