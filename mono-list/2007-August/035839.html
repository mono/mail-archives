They're not available on any mayor repository that I have checked. You 
can take current rawhide specs and patches [1][2][3][4], they'll build 
on Centos5 (x86_64) with little adjustments.

Hopefully it'll end up on EPEL[5] sooner or later. I have read somewhere 
that someone was already interested on maintaining it. In the mean time 
you'll have to build them yourself though.
