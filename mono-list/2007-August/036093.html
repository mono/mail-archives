In order to compile a new Mono you either need no mono/mcs in PATH at  
all or a working mono that can be used.

I have no idea why your mono spits this error, but it appears to be  
the already installed mono so you need to remove it either completely  
or keep it from being detected.

If you choose to install a precompiled mono it does not matter where  
you download it from, either from your distribution or from mono- 
project.com.
