Are you an open-source or cross-platform .NET developer based in Europe and
always wanted to attend MonkeySpace, but couldn't due to it always being in
the US? Well, you're in luck, because MonkeySpace is coming to Europe this
June!

MonkeySpace Europe edition is being held from Jun 9-11 in Dublin, Ireland.
We have general ticket pricing and also pricing for students.

General - €600
Student - €99*

More information and the speaker lineup can be found on the conference
website: http://monkeyspace.org/

We hope to see you there!

* Student pricing details can be found here:
http://monkeyspace.org/campaign/2014-05-01/
