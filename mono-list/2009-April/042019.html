Hello folks,

    I wanted to share with everyone on the lists a very important
announcement from Telerik, the creators of RadControls for ASP.NET.

    As of today Telerik is making RadControls for ASP.NET available and
supported for Linux users using Mono 2.4 on their systems.   Third-party
controls is one of the most requested features that we hear from our
community and Telerik is the first major vendor to offer their tools for
Mono users.   

    You do need Mono 2.4 as it contains many bug fixes and new APIs that
Telerik consumes that are required to run RadControls with Mono.

    Telerik's announcement is here:

http://www.telerik.com/company/press-center/company-news/telerik-announces-support-for-mono.aspx

    My blog on the subject is here:

	http://bit.ly/1RRK2x

    And some live demos are here:

	http://mono.telerik.com
