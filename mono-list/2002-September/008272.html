Just wanted to make mono hackers aware of a nifty C# windows extension.
Commandbar attaches a full dos box to the lower half of an explorer window,
and keeps the pwd synchronized with the folder navigation. Adds useful
command shortcuts, etc. If this were ported to Nautilus or other Gnome/KDE
filesystem browsers, it would probably be drive quite a bit of interest in
Mono on the desktop.

http://www.codeproject.com/csharp/commandbar.asp
