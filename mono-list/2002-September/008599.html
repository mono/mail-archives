You have this wrong.  You do not install Microsoft's version of SWF to use it 
under Mono.  Rather, Mono is developing it's own version of SWF to use with 
Mono.  This is being done through the judicious use of WineLib.  If you would 
like to try the SWF with Mono, you'll have to download from cvs and install.  
I do not know what the current status is nor the difficulty of installing.
