What kind of key?  Shucks, I should probably be
familiar with the windows registry if I had it.

I guess your key is a kind of compound key.  Two keys
pointing to one data item.  The simple solution is to
merge the two keys into one and use that one key as
the berkeleydb key.  Since the keys are sorted its
easy to search on both of the keys or the first one.
