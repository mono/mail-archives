Encode your hierarchy inside a single key.

It's kind of a bummer, but it does speed up searches. For instance
having

'/LOCAL_SOFTWARE/MegaCoolProject/Name'
'/LOCAL_SOFTWARE/MegaCoolProject/Path'
'/LOCAL_SOFTWARE/MegaCoolProject/DbString'

A single call to retrieve every '/LOCAL_SOFTWARE/MegaCoolProject/*' will
give you all your keys.
