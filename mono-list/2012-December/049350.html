I really recommend against using WCF on mono. I've found it to have
performance and stability issues, not too mention that a large amount of it
is simply not implemented. If you want good cross platform web services
under .NET & mono, use either the older .asmx style services or the third
party, open source ServiceStack.net services. I really recommend the latter
as you have much better support for both SOAP and REST style services right
out of the box with ServiceStack.
