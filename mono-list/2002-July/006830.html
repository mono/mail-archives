Just a random thought: If one motivation for WinForms is using 
SharpDevelop, perhaps it's easier to port that IDE to gtk# than to port 
WinForms to Linux to make SharpDevelop run on that emulation layer.

Concentrating on gtk# could also raise the interest of Linux/Gnome 
hackers and lower the risk to get in trouble with Microsoft.

Finally, if gtk# is really better than WinForms, there's always a chance 
that Windows programmers will pick up that API.
