FYI - changing baseurl to the following worked - thanks to David Ford 
for the info :)

[mono]
name=Mono for fedora-5-i386 (latest)
baseurl=http://go-mono.com/download-stable/fedora-5-i386/
enabled=1
gpgcheck=0
