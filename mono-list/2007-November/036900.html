I've downloaded the sample and ran it without problems (all the menus work as
expected) on svn trunk version of Mono. Casing problems arise from the fact
that Windows is merely case-aware but not case-sensitive, while Unix is both.
To get the sample working without changes, export the MONO_IOMAP=all variable
in your environment before launching xsp2.
