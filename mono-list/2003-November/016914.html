>I'm thinking of writing a GUI library that sits on top of both SWF and
>GTK#, able to use either depending on a config setting or guessing from
>the system running the program.
>
>Is there already anything like this?

#WT is similar like it.

#WT (Sharp Widget Toolkit) is a port of Eclipses SWT (Simple Widget 
Toolkit).

There are some people for using it for SharpDevelop.
There Mailinglist you can find at:
http://lists.ximian.com/mailman/listinfo/sd-mono-port
(in the mailing-list-archive there are some screenshots of #WT and 
code-samples)
I think at http://www.roboto.ch/swt/ are some screenshots.
Here are some examples: http://svn.sharpwt.net/SWT/
Here another #wt-page: http://japj.org/projects/swt/
And here other examples with screenshots: http://sharpwt.cbclass.net/

And then there existing wx.net, .net-Bindings for wxWindows.
http://wxnet.sourceforge.net/
