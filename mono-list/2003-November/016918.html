If you're using NAnt  <http://nant.sourceforge.net/> and editing your NAnt
files by hand, stop right there. Go and download NAntPad
<http://www.nantpad.com/default.aspx>  - a GUI app that lets you visually
configure your build and then spits out the XML file. Simple and easy.
