Hello,

> Is possible to make a stripped mono version ?...
> I want to make a runtime environment with mono, corlib.dll, System.dll 
> and some other needed library.....
> Is this possible ??... what is the minimum files needed ??

Yes, it is possible.

The absolute minimum you need is:

	$prefix/usr/bin/mono
	$prefix/lib/corlib.dll
	$prefix/etc/mono/config
	$prefix/etc/mono/machine.config
	$prefix/usr/lib/libmono.so.0.0.0
	$prefix/usr/lib/libmono.so.0

Include extra dlls at will.

Miguel.
