On 22.01.2012 01:17, Nicholas Frechette wrote:

> Are named mutexes supported under mono?

They are, if you enable shared handles by setting the
MONO_ENABLE_SHM environment variable, e.g.

MONO_ENABLE_SHM=1 mono yourapp.exe
