Hello Monoistas,

We're happy to announce that we're kicking the tires on having a Monospace
Conference around October of this year in Austin, Texas.  While we begin
the planning stages and determine everything that will need to be done,
we've setup a simple form[1] for you to submit your email allowing us to
gauge interest.  After submitting your email, you will be automatically
added to the mailing list to receive updates about the conference.  You
will also receive a response from us with more information and estimated
ticket price.  Another way to keep updated is to follow us on twitter
using our handle @monospace_conf.

We will keep everybody updated on the process of the conference.  Thanks
for your support and our goal is to make this an annual event!  We hope to
see everybody there!

Regards,


The Monospace Organizers
