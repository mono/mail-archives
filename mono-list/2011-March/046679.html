I ran into this "issue" myself: for some reasons ANTLR does not
deploy its SNK. It's 0 byte long in the archive.

You may want to create a new key:

	cd runtime/CSharp3/Sources/Antlr3.Runtime
	rm Key.snk
	sn -k Key.snk
