Hi all-

I have some of the System.Xml.Serialization classes finished (a lot of them are
just property/attribute/collection classes with no real logic). Don't get too
excited, I am just to the point where I will start work on the serializer
itself :) 

I'd like to get these in CVS just to avoid collision with someone else (which
I've had happen before on another project and it sucks).

Also, having some of the classes there will allow people looking to get their
feet wet to start writing tests, if they feel so inclined. My tests are
concentrated around the actual functionality of the serializer as opposed to
the individual testing of every class, although I think individual unit testing
is the way to go. 

Also, a disclaimer: I don't know for sure that these classes will work in Mono
as far as having their dependencies satisfied
(Reflection/Xml/Xml.Schema/Attribute..), all of my work thus far has been on
top of the ms .net framework.

Thanks,
John
