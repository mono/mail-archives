To close this issue a short follow up, mostly relavant to Mono RPM
users, who got their RPMs from mono.baselabs.org.

The pre-built libgc6 RPM didn't contain a reference to libdl. This
caused the segfaulting. I have built two fresh libgc6 RPMs which solve
the problem.

Thanks to lupus and Richard in helping to track down this problem. The
RPMs are just a little more functional again :)

Everybody using Mono RPMs should upgrade the two libgc6 RPMs AND the
Mono RPM. The current version are online at :

http://mono.baselabs.org/software
