mono and depencies (glib2, pkgconfig, etc) for you, builds mono, and
installs it for you.  You can find the mono-build.sh script at the Mono
Downloads page
http://www.go-mono.com/download.html
=20
There is also directions there detailing how to setup and use the
script.
