I have found that the uninstall works great.  However, if no
uninstall...removing the appropriate directories.  Look in
\WinNT\Microsoft.Net.

Also, have found it helpful to "clean" the GAC located at
\WinNT\assembly and it's download cache (append \download to previous)

between) Beta 2, RC0, RC3...and I have never rebuilt.  It has not been
a major problem.  (Just a bit of encouragement ;)
