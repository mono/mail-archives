just a thought, probably nothing to do with your issue, but i found 
this the other day
 
i had some problems with changes not showing when modifying
code behind files directly over a smb share because the clock on 
my laptop was ahead of the server time
 
i was running the server as a virtual machine, which i had suspended,
so its clock was about 4 hours behind when i brought it back up, and 
hadn't sorted itself out yet. 
 
on forcing the clock to catch up everything came good
