In a recent project, I had no troubles working with lockbits on Mono
1.9 on linux.
In the past, using some older version of Mono (probably 1.1.x or 1.2.x) I had
encountered few troubles working with lockbits but they have been resolved in
newer Mono releases.

Another thing that I noticed is that calling SetPixel repeatedly on Microsoft's
.NET is much slower than calling SetPixel repeatedly on Mono (though lockbits
was still faster on both).
