regarding mono and patents; It should adequately answer your questions.

old arguments go back and forth, and no new information is ever
presented. (I.e. it's a bunch of people saying "I am not a lawyer,
but...". Of course, if anyone has spoken to their patent attorney and
has some new insight because of this, then this would be the correct
place to post it.
