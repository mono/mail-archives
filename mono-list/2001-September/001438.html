In order to get ./autogen.sh to work for me I had to downgrade the version
of autoconfig that comes with the recent cygwin releases.

The following versions allowed me to autogen

cygwin  -> latest version
autoconf -> 2.13
automake -> 1.4
libtool -> 1.4

you can find these at the gnu.org or one of its mirrors.
