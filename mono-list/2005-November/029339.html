> No, the only way to restart a mod-mono-server (besides killing and
> starting the process yourself) is through the control panel.
> 
> > My hoster my not allow this because its a security risk.
> 
> In the forthcoming release, the control panel will let you restart
> mod-mono-servers individually (rather than all of them, for a given
> root/virtual host).  Your hosting provider could then password protect
> the appropriate control panel URLs through the usual Apache directives
> for password protection to make sure only the right people can restart
> the right mod-mono-servers.
> 
> This solution is a bit crude, so suggestions are welcome.
