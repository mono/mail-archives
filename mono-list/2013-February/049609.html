WCF services on mono do not work so well.  Even if you can get them to function, I've found the implementation does not perform very well.  If you get away from HTTP bindings, things get worse, and I'm not sure at all about the RabbitMQ bindings.

I use RabbitMQ heavily under mono and the regular driver works very well.  Can you get away from WCF for this?

Also, if you're building services in mono, or want a nice framework that is fully supported on both mono and .NET, I highly recommend using ServiceStack (http://ServiceStack.net). It performs quite well on both mono and .NET, and most people find the design approach to be quite refreshing.  I certainly do.
