At 05:03 PM 11/7/2002 -0500, Bob Smith wrote:
>To add another question to this, Do we know exactly what parts of SWF does
>SharpDevelop use so we could implement those first?

We are not using too many "advanced" controls (toolbar, treeview, listview, 
as well as the property grid), mostly basic stuff such as textboxes, 
dropdowns, etc. Of course there are the standard common dialogs that are 
being used. You can get a good idea of the controls used here:

http://www.icsharpcode.net/OpenSource/SD/Tour/

Thanks to our flexible addin infrastructure, we can remove certain features 
from the Mono version very easily (such as the help browser, which is tied 
to IE at the moment - anyone interested in writing a Mozilla# binding?)
