I have not used WCF under Mono but it seems that nobody else is getting back
to you. Not all of WCF is available under Mono. It seems to be their target
to implement the subset that is used by Silverlight (Moonlight). You might
get better answers if you ask about it on the Moonlight list.

You can see the status of the Mono classes vs. the Microsoft ones here:

http://www.go-mono.com/status/

You will notice that System.Data.Services (OData) is fairly complete.

http://www.go-mono.com/status/status.aspx?reference=4.0&profile=4.0&assembly=System.Data.Services
http://www.go-mono.com/status/status.aspx?reference=4.0&profile=4.0&assembly=System.Data.Services.Client

An alternative is to use Service Stack instead of WCF.

http://www.servicestack.net/
