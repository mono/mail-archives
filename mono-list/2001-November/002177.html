Most of my work thus far has been research. Microsoft's documentation is
not too clear on how they get CLR into the webserver. I've read verious
documents though and am getting a fairly good idea about how to acomplish
it. I've only looked at it from the ASP.NET side of the fence currently so
if you could find any documents describing how Microsoft's web services
relate to ASP.NET or use ASP.NET's runtime that would be very helpfull.

I've started working on classes that relate to the runtime but are
slightly higher level with makes them easier to understand to help get a
better picture of things. Maybe you could do the same thing too. Work on
some of the System.Web.Services stuff that are easier to work with but
have some tieins to lower level stuff. Look for something that implements
or ties in with System.Web.HttpApplication or System.Web.HttpContext.

I'm on irc.gimp.net #mono if you'd like to chat.
