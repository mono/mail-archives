To avoid collision with ECMA and/or MS, we should put extensions like that 
in mono specific namespaces. Our compilers use Mono.Languages.

I suggest for your components: Mono.Web.UI.Utils.

Later if we propose it to ECMA they can rename it to some namespace rooted 
in System, and it is easier for us to change or simply duplicate (to keep 
existing applications running).

If we use the System namespace, we are asking to have trouble not only 
moving our classes but propagating the trouble to those who create 
applications based on Mono.

Miguel:
I think this should be a policy on the project, any nested namespace and any 
classes not 'private' (even 'internal' may bring problems) that was 
naturally going to be added to what exists in ECMA/MS System namespace must 
be in truth rooted on Mono namespace. What do you think?
