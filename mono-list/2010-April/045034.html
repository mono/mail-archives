Maybe try editing /usr/lib/monovs-server/monovs-gui-server.exe.config and put
this line in:

<add key="MonoVS_Server_BindTo" value="10.0.2.15" />

Note: If you're using the GUI, edit the -gui- file, otherwise, edit
monovs-server.exe.config.

I submitted to Bugzilla a similar problem.  Here is the link that to it.

https://bugzilla.novell.com/show_bug.cgi?id=599059

You also may want to check the firewall on the machine hosting the VM.
