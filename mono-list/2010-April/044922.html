Try compiling with 'gmcs' instead of 'mcs'.

Depending on your version of Mono, mcs runs against the 1.1 profile (without
generics) and gmcs runs against the 2.0 profile (with generics).

It is likely that MySql.Data.dll was built against System.Data.dll 2.0 but
you are building against System.Data.dll 1.0 when you use 'mcs'.

If that does not work, what happens when you put MySql.Data.dll in the same
directory as your source file?
