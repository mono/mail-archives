Hi!

When I said "Mono core" I meant to the main mono packages, not a package
named "mono-core" :-) Sorry.

You package is libmono-corlib2.0-cil. I don't remember all packages, but you
should install:

monodevelop
mono-gac
monodevelop-database
monodevelop-debugger-mdb
mono-git
mono-runtime
mono-gmcs
mono-devel
mono-apache-server2
mono-xsp2

You can take a look to
http://packages.ubuntu.com/search?keywords=mono&searchon=names&suite=karmic&section=alland
you will get all packages, required and optionals, for mono
development,
and his dependencies.

Daniel.
