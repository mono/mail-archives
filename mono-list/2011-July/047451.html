Literary nit I've gotta pick. Sorry.

Wherefore means 'why' not 'where' as is commonly mistaken. When Juliet asks this in her famous balcony monologue she's not wondering where Romeo's at. Rather she's expressing her frustration as to why he has to be a member of the enemy's family.
