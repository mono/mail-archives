> mod_mono and Apache are installed in Fedora 11 using yum from standard 
> repositories.
> Trying to invoke asmx web service throws exception below about missing 
> System.ServiceModel

You have an incomplete setup.   You need the System.ServiceModel
assembly.

The mono-test-install flagging the incomplete setup is an initial hint,
you likely need something like "mono-full" or "mono-complete".
