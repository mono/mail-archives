Harsh,

The rpm for xsp 1.0.6 and mod_mono 1.0.6 have dependencies on the other
several other 1.0.6 packages. You can however get by this by using the
--nodeps switch with rpm. So you would do this:

rpm -ivh --nodeps xsp-1.0.6-1.novell.8.1.i586.rpm
mod_mono-1.0.6-1.novell.8.1.i586.rpm

Regards,
