On Tue, 2005-02-15 at 10:29 +0100, Hubert FONGARNAND wrote:
>Is there a way to configure mod_mono not case sensitive.
>(i'm working on a IIS based project, and IIS isn't case sensitive)
>thanks

mod_mono does nothing related to case. It just passes what it gets to
mod-mono-server, which uses classes from the System.Web (among others).
If your underlying filesystem is case sensitive, mod-mono-server et al
will be case sensitive when finding files.
