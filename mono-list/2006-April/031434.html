Yes, there are many things missing from MonoDevelop, and in general
there are many things missing in Mono.

Eventually we will get there, but there are a number of ways in which
you can help us get there faster:

	* Contribute code directly to Mono to implement said features.

	* Provide good bug reports, so we actually spend time fixing the
	  bug and not asking everyone "What is the OS?", "What is the
	  version?", "What platform?", "Where is the source?", "how
	  do you reproduce it?"

	* You could also help by fixing existing bugs listed on 
	  http://www.mono-project.com/Bugs

Indirectly, you can also convince your boss/bosses to purchase SUSE
Enterprise Server or Desktop, as the revenue for those products is what
funds Mono development at Novell today.

Directly, you can pay Novell through its support program (/Kickstart on
the web page) or you can hire a developer to contribute to Mono.

Miguel.
