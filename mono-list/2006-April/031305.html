Hi,

As there doesn't seem to be much in the way of approving a pile of
packages to FC 5 Extras (or FC rawhide Extras) that have been sitting in
the RH bugzilla for a while, I've decided to plonk them onto my home
website

http://www.all-the-johnsons.co.uk/mono/rpms.html

RPMS available

boo
ikvm
xsp
mod_mono
monodevelop
gtksourceview-sharp
monodoc
mono-debugger

(all except boo have the debuginfo stuff available to download as well)

They are all happy on x86 and x86_64. This is NOT a yum repo, so if you
want mod_mono, you need to download xsp as well. I'd just get the lot
and install them and have done!

Please note. None of these packages currently have RH bugzilla entries
and have not been approved or certified or anything like that. Use them
at your own risk and if there is a problem, please contact me directly.

The SRPM url is available for those who want to roll their own.

They may work on non-Fedora Core machines, but again, if they don't and
you knacker something, I'll try and help, but can't do anything more
than that.
