On 03/13/02 Christophe VG wrote:
> While working on the RPMS I (and others) constantly ran into the
> LD_LIBRARY_PATH/libmonowrapper "problem" when running mcs through the
> mcs script. I've constructed a very small patch that takes care of the
> export LD_LIBRARY_PATH stuff in a transparant way.
> 
> Maybe someone could verify and commit this to the CVS ?

If you install a library in /usr/local/lib and you don't
add that dir to your /etc/ld.so.conf or to your LD_LIBRARY_PATH,
it's a problem of your system, not of the script.
Should the script also set the PATH env var to the directory where
mint or mono are installed? No. Same thing for the libs.
