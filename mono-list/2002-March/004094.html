To have it find the libmonowrapper you should :
1) set the LD_LIBRARY_PATH
or
2) add the location of the lib to /etc/ld/so/conf, and rund ldconfig

There are a few references scattered around our site. You can find all
pages mentioning "libmonowrapper" with further explanations and the
solutions above in greater detail starting at :

http://mono.baselabs.org/index.php/search.html?q=libmonowrapper

Hope it helps you,
