Mono's System.Data.SqlClient is based on the implementation of TDS protocol
(namespace Mono.Data.Tds.Protocol). This implementation has serious bugs
which could make effects like you described. 
On 4/21/2003 I posted fixes to 2 bugs (String and Decimal values) but they
are not in CVS yet. You could try apply fixes and try again.
