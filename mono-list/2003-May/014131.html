On 05/25/03 Vivek, Bharath Varma (IE10) wrote:
> Could someone tell me, what's the V0.24 equivalent of the --precompile
> switch which was there in V0.23 of mono?
> Found a --compile-all switch in V0.24, is this the one? 
> I was able to do a --precompile @imagename earlier in 0.23, if I use the new
> --compile-all switch will it do the same?

No, --compile-all is just an option used to debug or benchmark the jit.
The new jit doesn't have a --precompile option and I'm not sure we'll
add it, adding the option would require some work to get correct
semantics and it would produce slower code...
