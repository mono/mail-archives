According to this:
http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/system.data.datarow.aspx Field is an
extension method.  You have to make sure you have a reference to System.Core
to use extension methods.  Also, and it's not clear from the help docs, you
often have to have a "using Blah.Extensions;" clause if the extension
methods are in an unresolved namespace.
