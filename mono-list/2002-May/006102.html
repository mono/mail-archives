I have another suggestion:

Remove MSG_NOSIGNAL from all sources and do 

"signal(SIGPIPE, SIG_IGN);" at the very start of "mono" and "mint".

From some previous discussion it turned out that MSG_NOSIGNAL is not
avaialble in some systems (not only freebsd) and signal() is widely
supported.

If you insist on having MSG_NOSIGNAL, I suggest adding test for it to
configure.in instead of #ifdef __FreeBSD__

What do you think about it ?

Jarek
