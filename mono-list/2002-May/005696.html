Well, I'll answer my own question :)  

The libgc6 rpms from mono.baselabs.org don't work with RedHat 7.3.  I
complied the GC from source
(http://www.go-mono.com/archive/gc6.0.tar.gz) and that fixed the
problem.

-Jared
