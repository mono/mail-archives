Two things:

1) Using a gentoo package, I would suggest filling a bug at
http://bugzilla.gentoo.org first. We (the gentoo developers) appreciate =
this
as it might very well be a bug in our packaging. We (the mono =
developers)
also appreciate this because it allows us to spend time nailing down =
mono
bugs, not downstream packaging bugs.

2) As to the specifics of the actual problem, I am not 100% sure, but I
would make sure /usr/lib/System.dll exists first.
