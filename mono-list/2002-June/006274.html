bugs or unimplemented features in any of the runtimes...
Since it doesn't make sense to develop applications for mono or for
p.net or for the ms CLR, I suggest you try to compile/run your
application with all the runtimes and stick with the one that better
suits your needs (and report the bugs/omissions you encounter to the
respective projects).
