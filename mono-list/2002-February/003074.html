You might want to coordinate with duncan since he wanted to work on
Convert as well. You may join us in the #mono IRC channel on
irc.gimp.org.
You can send the patches to the list, if they are not too big.
To create them, use the cvs server as explained on www.go-mono.com,
change the sources, compile, test and then issue:

	cvs diff -uN

to get the patch.
