	You'll be wanting to use the new libgda stuff for Gnome 2.0, on that
basis you'd want to build the following modules:

	'glib'
	'linc'
	'ORBit2'
	'bonobo-activation'
	'libbonobo'
	'gconf'

	all HEAD versions.
