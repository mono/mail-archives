> I was wondering, what's the status of the garbage collector? Is it in
> yet, or do you have an estimate on when it will be?
> 
> Good work to everybody who's been contributing! Keep it up!

We have no status on the garbage collector yet.

The strategy for deploying the Intel GC is being discussed internally at
Ximian.  It is too early to tell, but it looks like we will have:

	* Intel GC system.

	  Precising, compacting, generational, at-any-point collector
	  for architectures that go through the whole process of
	  implementing all the requirements.

	* Bohem GC system.

	  For performing quick ports of the engine.

Miguel.
