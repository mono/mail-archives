Hi all,

I've been looking around on the net for a non-word version of the ECMA
description of the class libraries and I couldn't find a version which
suited me well online, so I made my own (used wvHtml tool,
http://www.wvware.com/ ) based on www.ecma.ch's version

You can browse it at

http://mono.baselabs.org/ECMA/

or get a tarball (~500KB) containing the 23megs of htmlized ECMA at

http://mono.baselabs.org/ECMA.tar.bz2

I'll try to keep it updated as the EMCA is updated (Microsoft promises
monthly changes).
