Like I said you can keep it (still can't force you ;-) but it IS, even
if used with a single host, vulnerable to attacks (e.g. a man in the
middle attack).

Now I'll shamelessly reuse this thread to plug Gendarme (instead of
MonoOSC ;-) since it can detect such bad/hackish/should-be-temporary
ICertificatePolicy implementation.
