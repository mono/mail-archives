with this sample:

select * from test where int_field = @inf_field and varchar_field = 
'carlosga@telefonica.net' and char_field = 'llsdlk@@@jdj@@' and 
double_field=@double_field

this regex:

@"(('[^']*?\@[^']*')*[^'@]*?)*(?<param>@\w+)*([^'@]*?('[^']*?\@[^']*'))*"

results on 58 matches ( most of them empty )

this:

@"(('[^']*?\@[^']*')*[^'@]*?)*(?<param>@\w+)+([^'@]*?('[^']*?\@[^']*'))*";

results on 2 matches :)
