What do you want to discuss?

The general discussion forum for *using* Mono is mono-list (this mailing
list), which you can subscribe to at:

	http://lists.ximian.com/mailman/listinfo/mono-list

The discussion forum for *developing* Mono is mono-devel-list, which you
can subscribe to at:

	http://lists.ximian.com/mailman/listinfo/mono-devel-list

There are other pieces that get their own mailing lists, such as garbage
collection, documentation, and System.Windows.Forms implementation.  You
can find all of the lists at:

	http://www.go-mono.com/mailing-lists.html
