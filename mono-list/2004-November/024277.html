That sounds pretty specific to me.  That's a really long URI.  8K is
probably more than you wanted.  Perhaps you should have your app POST
rather than GET?  I think Apache will like it better if it gets its
query input from STDIN rather than stripping it from the URI.

I don't know the nGallery code, so I can't say where to make this
change, however.
