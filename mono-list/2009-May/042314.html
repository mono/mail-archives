thanks for all...

3 options were given:
1) To return to Main() method and from there to call return;  => clumsy
options when I in method 3 and more level under the Main() method;
2) To call Application.Quit() is used to exit from the GUI main loop, and
not useful for console/batch programs.

3) The best options is to call System.Environment.Exit(0); which exit
instantly from the program.

Thanks Dan...
