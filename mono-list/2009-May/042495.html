> A Marshal.Sizeof showed me that uint was translated to 4 instead of

uint (System.UInt32) is always 4 bytes, even on 64-bit systems,  
similar to uint32_t.
ulong (System.UInt64) is always 8 bytes, similar to uint64_t.
If you need an adapting size, try UIntPtr.
