I think mcs won't know how to extract assemblies from cabinet files, are you 
sure csc is able to do that?

Whatsoever, extract the assemblies (.dll files) from the cabinet files, to a 
temporary directory and reference them individually, and don´t forget 
/nostdlib to avoid collisions.
