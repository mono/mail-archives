Nedit is another nice editor that many of our software engineers use. 
It's free and runs on a number of platforms.  SlickEdit is a nice tool,
but isn't free.  Eclipse is a nice multi-platform IDE that you may want
to look at as well.

http://www.nedit.org/

http://www.eclipse.org/
