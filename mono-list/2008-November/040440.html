dblinq does not really use too new features of nunit.
It's just that test assemblies built with NUnit 2.4 (that are
what they ship) do not work with 2.2. TestFixtureAttribute and
TestAttribute from 2.4 are ignored in 2.2 (probably because they have
different assembly qualified names).

I think it is common case to whoever crosses both 2.2 and 2.4 roads.

Atsushi Eno
