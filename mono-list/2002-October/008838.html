I have a few questions about your Mono project that don't seem to be
answered in your FAQ or rational pages or are a little confusing.
 
You use the term "Common Language Infrastructure",  which is new to me.
Does this basically equate to the .NET SDK but for Unix/Linux?
 
You also use the term "Virtual Execution System" does this equate to the
"Common Language Runtime"?

>Question 48: Will I be able to compile a Microsoft VB.NET application

converting to C# and recompiling?
