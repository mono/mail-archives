*I SMBR - I Seminary Mono Brazil*

*Presentation *
The Program of Free Software of the Paraná - PSL-PR and the Society of 
the Users of Computer science and Telecommunications of the Paraná - 
SUCESU-PR, is carrying through the I Seminary Mono Brazil - I SMBR.

 This seminary is the landmark of the beginning of the activities of the 
MonoBasic Project in Brazil and the presentation of the projects of the 
Mono Brazil to the National Community of Development of Software.

 It sees details of the Project Mono beginner's all-purpose symbolic 
instruction code here
http://monobasic.sl.org.br


*Date: *
14, 15 and 16 of October of 2004

*Place: *
Mini Audience - CEFET-PR
Av. Seven of September, 3165
 Curitiba - Paraná
*
Target: *
Professionals of computer science, controlling of systems, analyst of 
systems, programmers, integrators, liberal professionals of computer 
science. Registrations
