>>>> You can use the <pkg-references> element for this purpose:
>>>>
>>>> <csc ....>
>>>> <sources>
>>>>     <include name="**/*.cs" />
>>>> </sources>
>>>> <pkg-references>
>>>>     <package name="gtk-sharp-2.0" />
>>>> </pkg-references>
>>>> </csc>
