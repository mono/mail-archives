Hi Andrew,

maybe you have tried this, but here is how i got through this this
afternoon:

download monocharge
uncompress to somewhere
cd monochargedir
cp *.exe /usr/bin
cp net_1_1/*.dll /usr/lib

(update mono and mcs from cvs)

cd mono
./autogen.sh --prefix=3D/usr
make fullbuild

that worked for me. then your build will compile with the assistance of
the mcs and the corlib from monocharge.

hope that helps,

--chris
