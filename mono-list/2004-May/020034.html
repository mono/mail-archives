Community
 
In April, 2004, I joined the MySQL team and the ByteFX.Data provider has

been acquired and is being developed into the official .NET provider for

MySQL!  While some details of the transition are still being worked out,

this change will include a change to the name and namespace of the product.
 
New versions of the provider will no longer be distributed from this
site or the corresponding SourceForge.net site.  Bug reports in the
existing builds can be submitted at http://bugs.mysql.com.
 
Announcements will be made detailing the new name, distribution
channels, mailing list, and licensing.
