Your best bet is to file bug reports about the issues you found with 1.1.x.
Backporting all the fixes and improvements we did in 1.1.x to 1.0 is not
going to happen, because it would be very time consuming: we backported only
a few critical and simple bugfixes.
So, please try to track down what the issues are and file bug reports
against 1.1.x: it's the fastest way to solve your issues.
