(Resending to include list)

> I have problem with my Mono installation. I'm OpenSuSe 10.3 user. When I try
> running my autopano program (it requieres Mono) I get this error:
>
> ** (/usr/local/bin/generatekeys.exe:16894): WARNING **: The following
> assembly referenced from /usr/local/bin/generatekeys.exe could not be
> loaded:
>     Assembly:   gtk-sharp    (assemblyref_index=1)
>     Version:    1.0.0.0
>     Public Key: 35e10195dab3c99f
> The assembly was not found in the Global Assembly Cache, a path listed
> in the MONO_PATH environment variable, or in the location of the
> executing assembly (/usr/local/bin).

I had a similar problem with this app recently.  I solved it by
modifying the build scripts to use gtk-sharp-2.0, and glade-sharp-2.0,
then rebuilding the app.  I believe I did this by finding all of the
Makefile files in all subfolders of src, and then looking for lines
like "/pkg:gtk-sharp /pkg:glade-sharp" and modifying them.  Hope this
helps.
