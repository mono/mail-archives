didn't file a bug on bugzilla.gnome.org (yet); however, I reported it

Why didn't that come out before? Or am I missing something? I 
configured mono as

  ./configure \
   --with-gnu-ld\
   --enable-minimal=profiler,reflection_emit,large_code,com,ssa\
   --with-xen_opt=no\
   --with-moonlight=no\
   --with-x=no\
   --with-sigaltstack=no
