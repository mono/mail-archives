mandag 17 September 2007 skrev B R:
> Any tips for someone who'd like to develop on a Windows platform, for Linux? 
> Like any pitfalls to avoid, required dependencies and whatnot?

You will want to setup a vmware image, that runs a recent version of mono, and 
e.g. CruiseControl to check your builds when you update your repository.

You will also want to use unit tests as much as possible, to test all those 
little things.
