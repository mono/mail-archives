Yes, but Ubuntu does not update mono packages until a new release appears.
For example, Mono 1.2.4 will be not available in Ubuntu until 7.10 (Gutsy
Gibbons). Look at packages.ubuntu.com to view that mono packages are
available only in Gutsy Gibbons. The current version of Ubuntu, Feisty Fawn,
does not update mono 1.2.3 to 1.2.4.
