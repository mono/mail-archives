> SslClientStream for most of the servers I've tried breaks connection
> exactly after 5 minutes, no matter if connection is idle or loaded.

Possibly but a network trace would be required to confirm that.

> Has anyone experienced simmilar behaviour?

Some previous versions of Mono could timeout after 5 minutes of network
inactivity. This is fixed in SVN but I don't recall if it was in 1.1.8.x
or not. However this shouldn't affect a connection that has (even a
single byte) activity in 5 minutes. E.g. tests has been done for
submitting very large files (e.g. > 4Gb) without problems (and took more
than 5 minutes).

JD contributed a much revamped async support for SSL (which may solve
this and other problems too). You're welcome to try his patch or wait
for me to do some testing and release a newer binary for
Mono.Security.dll.
