Hello,

> Has anyone ever tried to compile an application that has a reference to
> Microsoft's Microsoft.SharePoint.dll (for use with Sharepoint Portal
> Server)?

You need to have any referenced assemblies installed in Mono's GAC.

That being said, I have my doubts that just copying
Microsoft.SharePoint.dll to Linux will work as I doubt it has even been
tested on Linux (and then you should look at the license for the
library, it might not allow it to be used on non-Windows operating
systems).
