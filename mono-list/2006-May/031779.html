Hello,

> We're trying to bundle our App with the mono-framework using mkbundle. 

You do not have the LD_LIBRARY_PATH variable pointing to the
installation directory with /lib.   I recommend that you do that.

Alternatively, use packages to install Mono into your system instead of
the installer.
