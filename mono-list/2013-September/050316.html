Hi,

there is a horrible bug till years in the drawimage-method of the
graphics-class in System.Drawing! When you tile an image into nine segments,
you have 8 border-segments and one center. If you draw and stretch these
tiles to another image using the graphics-class, not all tiles are drawn and
the areas keep blank/transparent!
