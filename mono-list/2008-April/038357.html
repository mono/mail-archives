If you're interested in packaging it yourself then you will want to read
these Documents:

http://en.opensuse.org/Build_Service
http://en.opensuse.org/Build_Service/Tutorial

Once you have a build service account (same as your Novell Bugzilla
account but you have to get access to Build Service, see Tutorial) try
to get your package building in your home project.  Once it's building
there email me privately and I will add you to the Mono:Community
project and we can copy your package in.

If you were just hoping we'd package it for you I can add it to the list
and one of us should get it done eventually.
