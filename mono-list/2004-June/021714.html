I had the same problem until I set MONO_PATH to gtk-sharp and gecko-sharp
directories:

export MONO_PATH=/usr/lib/mono/gtk-sharp:/usr/lib/mono/gecko-sharp (or
wherever the libs are installed)

Hope this helps.
