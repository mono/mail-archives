On Tue, 2003-02-18 at 02:54, Nick Drochak wrote:
> Hi.
> 
> I'd like to suggest changing the subject line of the bugs-list to
> include the status of the bug.  Usually I am scanning for resolved bugs
> to test out, etc.  For my purposes I care less about the priority.

The headers already contain product/component, status, priority, etc
info.  It should be a simple task to filter and highlight
"X-Bugzilla-Status: RESOLVED" messages.

For example, in evolution its just a matter of:

Tools -> Filters -> Add (or edit your existing one)
Add criterion [Specific header] "X-Bugzilla-Status" [contains] "RESOLVED"

Then in the actions, you can filter to a specific folder or colour the
details red or something.

> 
> Perhaps status can be added to the subject, or even replace the priority
> if that field isn't so much of a...well... priority.
> 

The subject line is already rather crowded, and we'd have to get all the
users of the Ximian bugzilla to accept a change.

- Dick
