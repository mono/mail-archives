Mono's ASP.NET support isn't really ready for widespread testing. The
incompleteness of System.Security and security-related features would
also make a secure multi-user test server setup difficult to achieve.
That said, such a service may well be provided by enthusiasts at some
point in the future. Until then, you can set up XSP or mod_mono for
yourself, following the instructions on go-mono.com
