    I have a special request for the packaging of glade3... I would like
you to package the gnome catalog seperately.

This includes:
    libgladegnome.so (the gnome support module)
    gnome.xml, bonobo.xml & canvas.xml (the gnome catalog files)
    gnome*.png and bonobo*.png (pixmaps for the gnome component)

In contrast with glade2; who is offering "gnome" builds and "non gnome"
builds,
we offer the base glade-3 core library and tool without any gnome
dependancies
and offer an optional gnome plugin.

And ofcourse... I suppose you will be packaging the .pc files, header files
etc
in a seperate "glade-dev" package...
