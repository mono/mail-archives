Since there are going to be others programs linking against libgladeui
it will be also necessary to package gtk+ catalog separately

IMHO i would do something like this:

glade-3		metapackage glade-3-core libgladegtk
glade-3-core	glade-3 executable
	depends on libgladegtk
	recommends libgladegnome

libgladeui	depends on libgtk2.0-0, etc
libgladegtk	depends on libgladeui
libgladegnome	depends on libgladegtk

About the last two, I am not sure about their names maybe something like
glade-catalog-gtk and glade-catalog-gnome would fit better.

So...
for example anjuta-2's glade plugin will depend on libgladegtk and that
will work flawlessly, heh this remind me why I like debian so much :)
