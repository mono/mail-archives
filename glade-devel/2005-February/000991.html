Hi all,
    The LIBGLADE_INTEGRATION branch in now *closed*, for those
of you who dont know about the LIBGLADE_INTEGRATION branch,
you can forget about it :-)

LIBGLADE_INTEGRATION was branched with the intention of code-sharing
the glade-file parser with libglade (and save/load glade files through
libglade).

This turned out to be quite complex and a nightmare to coordinate both
projects together, so abandon ship! now we have load/save support in
glade-3; Thanks Naba for taking the time to kill this branch :)

Cheers,
