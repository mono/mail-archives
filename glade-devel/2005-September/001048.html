Using glade I quickly realize that menu bar accelerators do not work
when focus is on another window other than GladeProjectWindow

The first hack i made was to add the GtkUIManager accel group to the
corresponding windows:
  * palette_window and editor_window: in glade_project_window_new()
  * newly created windows: in on_palette_button_clicked()
  * windows from a saved project: in glade_app_add_project()
