Hi,

Using the old patch I gave you months ago with a little bit mods, I can play
the 2.5.1 without problem so far. Here's my binaries:

http://www.ivanwong.info/gtk/gtk+-2.4.tar.bz2

No setup required, all gtk+-2.4/win32 related stuffs are included properly.
I assume files is extracted under c:/, otherwise please run bin/reconfig.bat
after extraction. Glade should run by just clicking glade-2.exe (not
glade-loader.exe, it's supposed to be removed as suggested). I have recently
improved the gtk+/glade/libglade all-in-one installer under win32 a lot. May
be I will release a beta version of 2.4 as well.
