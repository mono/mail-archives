I believe that it isn't just a matter of size, but the efficiencies of
parsing an un-compiled source and pre-complied binary. For development, we
of course like to use "ready to edit" text .glade files. But for production
release, I think I do welcome the pre-compiled form of .glade files which
improves the efficiency of repated loading of some frequently used forms
(which I also don't want them to be just invisible and reside in the memory
when unused).
