Hi,

  For the amd64 problem, you can probably work around it by building mono
with --with-sgen=no. For the
x86 problem, it seems to be a C compiler problem:

CC	libmonoruntimesgen_la-reflection.lo
make[3]: *** [libmonoruntimesgen_la-reflection.lo] Error 130

Since there are no error messages from the compiler, it just fails.
