SWF is System.Windows.Forms.

We currently have two implementations of this namespace. The first one
lives in class/System.Windows.Forms that requires Wine. This effort has
been discontinued. The second one lives in Managed.Windows.Forms and it
is our ongoing effort to implement System.Windows.Forms natively in C#.
It's still under heavy development.

There is a winforms list where we can follow our progress.
