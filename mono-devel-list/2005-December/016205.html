    Hello,

    MonoBASIC (Mono's VB compiler) is indeed being worked by five
developers in Brazil and this team is now the official maintainers of
the VB compiler.

    We have been working on an effort to get 100% approval on automated
tests and so far we have less than 30 known bugs on the compiler, which
means that it is mor or less usable by now.

    The ASP.NET VB binding is buggy though, it will be worked after
getting 100% positive test approval.
