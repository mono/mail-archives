The reason is the german (non “en-US” VS2005 installation. So you have to
use instead of “Tools”  the String “Extras” (german word in the IDE for
“Tools”) in the member connect.cs of

 Monoaddin for the string “toolsMenuName”.  Recompile and copy the
monoaddin.dll. should also work with other languages
