I would like to hear about MacOSX and mono and the future of the UI stack 
in that space. Some thoughts:

- WindowsForms roadmap to running natively on MacOSX (not in X Windows).

through the Carbon API's.
- Will moonlight, in the future, run on the desktop for MacOSX like it

tried to build it on the Mac). Will these be changed? Is this a priority?
