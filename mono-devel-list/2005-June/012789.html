I think that the gaming platforms is all about hype. That they use the 
capability of floating point calculation as the power of the platform is an 
example of the supervisialness of the whole marketing campaign.

A good while after they are released there should be decent tests, which 
will show exactly which of the platforms is best (for a purpose).

Even though linux will run on the PS3 I wonder whether it will be very 
customizable. And I also wonder whether Microsoft will ever be able to stop 
people from cracking their protections. It may be harder and take more time, 
but if their marketing campaign is right they provide one of the cheapest 
amount of processing power per dollar.

Time will tell.
