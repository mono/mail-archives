I can't speak entirely for moonlight, but I know that Silverlight does
not allow P/Invoke. I would expect that moonlight is the same, since
that would mean any moonlight web app application would have full access
to all things that your user account has access to. Moonlight implements
a sandbox to prevent this from happening.

For tutorials, 3d and audio support, see silverlight.net.
