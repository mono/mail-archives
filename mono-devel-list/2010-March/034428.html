AF_SNA, AF_DECnet and SOCK_RDM are not part of POSIX, and Haiku does not
supply them.
Check for their presence before using them, as already done for AF_IPX.

	* socket-io.c: Don't depend on AF_SNA, AF_DECnet,
	SOCK_RDM.
