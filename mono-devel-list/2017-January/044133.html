little-endian 32-bit MIPS ought to work - if it doesn't it's a bug. It's
not *actively* maintained due to a lack of CI infrastructure, but e.g.
https://buildd.debian.org/status/logs.php?pkg=mono&arch=mipsel

To cross compile, use appropriate --target= and --host= flags to
configure, and pass --disable-mcs-build to configure too (build the
classlib on x86, you can't cross-compile it)
