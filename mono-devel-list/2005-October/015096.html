Hi Mono Hackers,

Mono 1.1.9.1 does not compile on AMD64 for 2 reasons on Debian, one is a
binutils problem which is already patched [0] by us, but after that the
compilation segfaulted around WebServices.
After that I got another patch [1], this time through the Debian BTS [2]
for Mono 1.1.9.1 which fixes problems for 64bit architectures (like
AMD64) and indeed the segfaultes we had are gone and Mono 1.1.9.1 works
now for AMD64 on Debian. I checked SVN HEAD of Mono and it looks like
it's not yet fixed there, so I am posting this patch [1] here now.

[0]
http://svn.debian.org/wsvn/pkg-mono/mono/trunk/debian/patches/amd64_compile_fix_r50553.dpatch?op=file&rev=0&sc=0
[1]
http://svn.debian.org/wsvn/pkg-mono/mono/trunk/debian/patches/64bit_implicit_pointer_cast_fix.dpatch?op=file&rev=0&sc=0
[2]
http://bugs.debian.org/cgi-bin/bugreport.cgi?bug=330982

PS: here a lib linker patch, I am not sure if it is required, but please
check this one too [3]

[3]

PPS: Mono 1.1.9.1 is now in Debian/Unstable and working for x86,
powerpc, amd64 and ia64
