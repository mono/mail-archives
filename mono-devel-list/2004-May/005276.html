RPMs for Mono 0.31.99 and Gtk# 0.91 are available here:

        http://primates.ximian.com/~duncan/0.31.99/take-3/

They are available to Red Hat 9 and SuSE 9 users. SuSE 9.1 and Fedora
Core 1 will come next.

There is a known bug in this set of mono packages -- it should depend on
the serial-5 ICU packages, but it's not doing that right now. Those will
be fixed in the next build.

Please reply to this message if you find any problems with the packages.
I'm especially looking for people with existing GNOME (distribution or
XD2) and Mono installations. It'll be of great help if some of you can
test the upgrade path. If any weird packages are required to be swapped
out, anyone out of the ordinary, please report back.
