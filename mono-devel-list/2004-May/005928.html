Sunday 23 May 2004 21:57, Met wrote:
> What do I need to do to the Makefile(s) that come with Nemerle to make
> it install correctly (GAC) for Mono on Linux?

You have to download the latest snapshot from
http://www.nemerle.org/download/snapshots/

(introducement of GAC into mono changed way of loading libraries - and as I 
remember it is still a hack in our compiler, we take GAC path to corlib.dll, 
than replace mono/gac with 1.0/ to find out where symlinks are located)

We do not support installing into GAC yet - GAC design is still unstable in 
mono BTW.

I hope we can make new release together with mono 0.91Beta2 and make sure 
everything works together.
