There is gnunit (module nunit-gtk in cvs).

Just uptdated from cvs, rebuilt and executed. It's working well. Just beware 
that make install does not create an wrapper script as is normal with other 
mono tools.

I ' make install'-ed to prefix /usr and had to call it like

mono /usr/bin/gnunit.exe

Also I had to recompile my test assemblies, for them to refer to assemblies 
that changed version numbers (GAC/Versioning is now working)

Good testing,
