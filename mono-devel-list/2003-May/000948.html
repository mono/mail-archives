> There is also a question about whether it is legal to mix malloc/free with the

In theory, you should not mix g_malloc/free;  malloc/g_free;  But with
Glib 2, it is possible to force g_malloc/g_free to always be
malloc/free, so this issue could be ignored (it is also the default).
