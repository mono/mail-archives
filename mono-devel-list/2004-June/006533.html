Just a quick note to the list;

I've notice a fair number of MAC/OSX/PPC users still using the external 
boehm GC.

As of B2 mono has synced the necessary PPC/OSX changed of boehm into 
the internal GC and the preferred way of compiling/running Mono on 
those platforms is with the internal GC.  It makes for much easier 
problem replication and debugging to somewhat standardize.
