Hi,
i had problems with saving in Monodoc (beta2) and wrote a little patch 
for monodoc/browser/editing.cs.

Then i realized that Miguel already fixed in cvs, though i think my fix 
is better.

It first creates backup of your contributions before serializing 
changes. So in case that serialization goes wrong you will not loose all 
your work. Patch also prevents creating empty changeset.xml file, that 
made monodoc unusable.

Cheers Radek.

PS: it's my first patch, i hope i did all well.
