Stubbing classes helps out a lot.  They have to be manually coded by hand
though.  They can not be copy/pasted from MSDN.  Anything not implemented,
you can put a [MonoTODO] attribute above the class's methods or properties.
Also, you can throw a NotImplementedException in Mono too.
