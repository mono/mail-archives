Hi,

I have never tried csc to build olive stuff before, but the same
trick as our mcs tree has (actually the trick in in build/rules.make)
worked: make CSCCOMPILE="csc -d:NET_2_0 -d:NET_1_1"

If you want to try our nunit tests under .NET framework, you can
run "make run-test-ondotnet". It just runs nunit-console.exe without
mono runtime.

For above, you don't have to change anything in your source tree ;-)

Atsushi Eno
