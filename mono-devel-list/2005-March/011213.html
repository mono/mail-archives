monodoc works fine with the latest gtk#.

You just have to make monodoc think gtk# 1.0 is installed:

    cd $MONO_HOME/lib/pkgconfig
    for m in gtk gtkhtml glade; 
         do sudo ln -s $module-sharp-2.0.pc $module-sharp.pc
    done
