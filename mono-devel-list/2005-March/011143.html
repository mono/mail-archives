Last night while I was messing I need GtkSpell support. I knew Tomboy
had it but I didn't want to go pulling it apart. So I generated new
bindings with GAPI and bam. GtkSpell support for the rest of us :-). Its
still buggy and I already know of one or two issues in the
Makefile/configure files but it should work right now, and it works on
both WIN32 and Linux.
 
If anyone has seen gaim or tomboy knows about GtkSpell. It basically
creates the Word, or Evolution type marks on your misspelled words, and
with a quick right mouse click you can fix your mistake. It only takes
one line of code and you have spell check on any of your TextViews :-) 

http://www.polystimulus.com/gtkspell-sharp.tar.bz2

have fun!
