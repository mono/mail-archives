Hi Adrian,

>  AddHandler mono .aspx .ashx .asmx .ascx .asax .config .ascx

That wasn't necessary: I solved using the Alias directive, like you
suggested in a previous post; however I had to remove
MonoDocumentRootDir - that did it. My mod_mono.conf now looks like
this:

LoadModule mono_module /usr/lib/httpd/modules/mod_mono.so
Alias /fmarini "/var/www/html/aspnet/fmarini"
Alias /eschedule "/var/www/html/aspnet/eschedule"
#MonoApplications
"/fmarini:/var/www/html/aspnet/fmarini,/eschedule:/var/www/html/aspnet/eschedule"
MonoApplicationsConfigFile "/var/www/html/aspnet/mod_mono.webapp"
<Directory /var/www/html/aspnet>
        SetHandler mono
</Directory>

either MonoApplications or MonoApplicationsConfigFile will work.
