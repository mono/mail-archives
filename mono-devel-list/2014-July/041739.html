There's no harm in posting to the mono-devel list, but technically this should be on the mono-discuss list (or mono-list, whatever it's called.)  
The mono-devel list is generally for developing mono.
The mono-list list is generally for developing *on* mono.

But there's a lot of cross chatter between the two lists.  And sometimes people try the other list when they didn't get the response they were looking for on the first list.

Aside from that ... You didn't post enough about your problem for anyone to very likely know what to say to you.  Like what do you try, what do you expect, and what error message or other failure do you get instead....
