There was a little bug in the wsdl generation. It's now fixed in CVS. If
you can't update, try disabling the support for HttpPost from the web
services section in machine.config and/or web.config (look for the
configuration/system.web/webServices/protocols element, and remove the
<add name="HttpPost"/> entry).
