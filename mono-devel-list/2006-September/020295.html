Hi Chicão,

Blogger exports ATOM feeds and Monologue wants one of the RSS-named
formats, That is why I had to abandon my Blogger blog and use another
one for Monologue. The correct thing to do, I think, would be to make
monologue support ATOM, but AFAIK no one tried to add such support, or
if someone did no advertise it properly.

Feedburner may be a way to have someone translate ATOM to RSS in lieu
of Monologue.
