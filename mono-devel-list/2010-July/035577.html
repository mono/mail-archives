     As of now, we request that developers no longer maintain the contents
of ChangeLog files by hand.

     The ChangeLog files are now generated at distribution time from the Git
commit history.

     For details see:

http://mono-project.com/Coding_Guidelines#Source_Code_Control

And in particular:

http://mono-project.com/Coding_Guidelines#Git_Workflow_Changes
