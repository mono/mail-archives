Your questions are Mono / .NET questions, not MonoDevelop (IDE) related.

Maybe you could do something like this:

System.IO.MemoryStream ms = new System.IO.MemoryStream(data); // "data"
being your byte array
System.Drawing.Bitmap image = new System.Drawing.Bitmap(ms); // Don't forget
to Dispose your objects...

(Prefixing types with the full namespace to make it easier for you.)
