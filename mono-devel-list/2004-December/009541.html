On Thu, 2004-12-23 at 11:39 +0900, Atsushi Eno wrote:
> Our svn service has been inaccessible for a while (because of some
> kind of DDoS attack). Our Ximian ops are working on the issue, but it
> is not likely to be fixed soon. We are not sure how long it will be.

In the meantime, if you want some read-only access, use the `svn switch'
command to switch your repo over to the anon version. This just makes
you read from the anon server, you can switch back with the same
command. Look at http://svnbook.red-bean.com/en/1.1/re27.html for an
example of the usage of the command (note that you want the --relocate
flag).

If you have patches you want on the repo, you can send them to the
Ximian monkey responsible for the area. Some people are going to be out
on vacation, so, you probably want to post to mono-devel-list.
