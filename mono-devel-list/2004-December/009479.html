g_mutex_init is not a new but an old private function which got removed
somewhere between glib 2.2 and 2.4. I don't know what's really causing
the error on your system but I'd suspect that your glib installation is
somehow broken (perhaps mixing parts of glib 2.0/2.2 and 2.4/2.6?). What
distribution do you use or have you built glib from source?
