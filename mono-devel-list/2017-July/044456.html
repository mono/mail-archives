Due to our reliance on mono & our need to ensure a tested version is used
with our product, we are bundling mono in our package for install. With
this setup, we have a separate registry, configuration, directory of
assemblies, etc., so install/uninstall/update of system mono won't affect
us. However, it appears that we are sharing the mono certificates with the
system mono. For the registry/config, we use the MONO_CFG_DIR setting, &
MONO_PATH for the assemblies. But I can't find a similar setting for the
certificate store. (We're running a service as root, so the path is
/use/share/.mono/certs.) Is there any way to separate our certificate store
as with the registry, config, assemblies, etc.?

Thanks,
