Hey Guys,
    Good news! After months of prodding, the Novell marketing dept has 
changed their policy on automatically sending out the "validate e-mail 
address" e-mail when a new  Bugzilla account is created.

Accordingly, the Novell Bugzilla team has already updated the website to 
automatically send out this "validate e-mail address" e-mail.

I've updated our Novell Bugzilla Faq with the new information:
http://mono-project.com/FAQ:_Novell_Bugzilla

This was one of the biggest complaints our users have had with Novell 
Bugzilla, so please help spread the word that this is now fixed!

Thanks,
Thomas
