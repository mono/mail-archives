> 	Is there a way to turn the JIT off in mono? I'm working with it on a
> Mac and wanted to see if I could get XSP working. 

Rather than starting XSP like this:
mono xsp.exe

Start it with mint:
mint xsp.exe

That will turn off the JIT compiler, at least for XSP itself.
