Hello Matthijs,

Here are the best sources of samples for Mono.Security.dll, most classes are
documented in Monodoc.

(a) Mono.Security is used by to implement all security tools under
/mcs/tools/security/
this includes: sn, secutil, certmgr, makecert, signcode, chkltrust ...

(b) There is a copy of most of the classes of Mono.Security in corlib
(dependency issue) as corlib's uses many classes to implement it's (mostly)
cryptographic classes.

(c) My blog, http://pages.infinit.net/ctech/poupou.html, also contains some
samples (or fragments).

Finally I you have any doubt about "can Mono.Security do X or Y ?" then send
them to the mailing list and I'll answer them.

Sebastien Pouliot
