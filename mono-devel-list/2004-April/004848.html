How the very error message says, the host key has changed and you have to 
correct your known_hosts file. Mine I just killed it (it is automatically 
recreated next time you use ssh, by means of cvs in our case) because ximian 
and sourceforge are my only trusted sites, anyway...

>Add correct host key in /home/Administrator/.ssh/known_hosts to get rid of 
>this message.
>Offending key in /home/Administrator/.ssh/known_hosts:1
>RSA host key for mono-cvs.ximian.com has changed and you have requested 
>strict checking.

Hope it helps,
