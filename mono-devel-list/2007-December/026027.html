On 09/12/2007, Pete Erickson <petee at mindtouch.com> wrote:
> I tried submitting this to bugzilla but verifying my email address is
> apparently broken.

I don't know if you ran into the same problem as I did, but I found
that it's not actually broken, just very confusing.  After you first
sign up, it implies that an email will be sent that you should use for
verification... but no email arrives.  *HOWEVER*, if you then try to
log in, it gives you a useless screen where you can request a
verification email, which is then sent immediately.

After clicking on the link in that email, there are some suspicious
browser caching and/or cookie issues, but if you log all the way out
and back in, it will work.

I mostly stumbled on this by accident, or I would have thought for
sure that it was broken.
