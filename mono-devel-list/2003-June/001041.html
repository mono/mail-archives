I disabled it in cvs, until it's properly tested and propagated to the
anon cvs server.
You can comment out the use of libgc in configure.in by changing the line
AC_CONFIG_SUBDIRS(libgc)
to:
dnl AC_CONFIG_SUBDIRS(libgc)
or wait for the anon cvs server to sync thet same change I just
committed (also you need to remove the lines that mention libgc i
in the toplevel Makefile.am).
