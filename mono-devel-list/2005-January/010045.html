> As seen in Mono svn, the ByteFx.Data is ~7 weeks old.
> On ByteFx homepage it is stated that in April 1, 2004 MySQL hired that
> guy and now ByteFx.Data isn't developed anymore.
> So my question is: Can't Mono switch to this MySQL official connector
> (which basically is the old ByteFx.Data)?
> http://dev.mysql.com/downloads/connector/net/1.0.html

No, for licensing reasons.  The ByteFX.Data that Mono has is LGPL, while
the new ByteFx is GPL.  Mono does not want to ship GPL assemblies.
