It's not ideal, but you can grab daily precompiled assemblies from here:
http://mono.ximian.com/daily/ , and place them in your Mono's GAC:
C:\Program Files\Mono-2.2\lib\mono\gac.  (Or use Mono's gacutil to 
register them.)
