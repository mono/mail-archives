>I would recommend Pro C# 2008 and .NET 3.5 platform from APress

That's a fine book but much of it won't work in Mono yet. Perhaps the
free DotNetBookZero is a good place to start.

http://www.charlespetzold.com/dotnet/DotNetBookZero11.pdf

Or the Soft Steel Tutorial:

http://www.softsteel.co.uk/tutorials/cSharp/cIndex.html

Or the nice Twisted OOP tutorial:

http://www.geocities.com/jeff_louie/OOP/oop.htm

Programming C#, 4th Edition, by Jesse Liberty is pretty good.
