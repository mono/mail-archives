Hi,

  There is a method called a managed-to-native wrapper between the test
method and the
 C function, this method is generated by the runtime, in marshal.c, and it
does the marshalling of parameters, in this case, it should convert the
'true' value to a 0xffff.
Also, I can't reproduce this failure on either ppc or ppc64 (debian).

                                                    Zoltan
