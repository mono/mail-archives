I have mono from source (svn) on ubuntu, you need to checkout this 3 items:
  svn co svn://anonsvn.mono-project.com/source/trunk/mono  svn co svn://anonsvn.mono-project.com/source/trunk/mcs  svn co svn://anonsvn.mono-project.com/source/trunk/libgdiplusSome days ago, i add reference to Compiling [1] on IncompletePackage [2] wiki page beacause i was having the same issue.
[1] http://www.mono-project.com/Compiling_Mono
[2] http://www.mono-project.com/IncompletePackage
