Get rid of the sun packages and install GNU tools:

gtar
gsed
gcc
gmake
autoconf
automake
libtool

alias make=gmake
alias cc=gcc

You can obtain them all from blastwave.org

Once you're there... cross your fingers!!! I'm still trying to build on 
Solaris 10 x86. It seems there's a problem with big/little endian or 
something like that. I'm working on it with Geoff and Miguel.
