Mono already works to a certain degree on OSX.  There is a bug (51590) 
that is currently preventing a clean build from CVS.  Check out the 
report for a workaround.  The interpreter (mini) is completely 
functional and thanks to Paolo Molaro, the JIT (mono) is well on its 
way to being complete on PPC.

The only catch right now on BSD is the GC is broken.  You must compile 
with --with-gc=none.
