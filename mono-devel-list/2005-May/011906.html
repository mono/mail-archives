Hey everyone,

There are far too many patches on bugzilla that have not gotten reviewed
and checked in. Most of these patches happen to be in the Class
Libraries component, however they do exit elsewhere.

I have added a [Patches] query to the bugs page:

        http://www.mono-project.com/Bugs
        
This looks for the `patch' keyword on the bug.

I ask two things:
      * Please take a look at this query in any areas that your hack on.
      * If you create a patch, or notice that another person has, please
        mark it with the `patch' keyword. Hopefully this will give it
        the attention it deserves.

-- Ben
