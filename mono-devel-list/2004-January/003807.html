Heya Everyone

    As MonoDevelop picks up speed the anon-svn is getting slow to use. 
For people who don't want to wait hours to get their hands on a copy of 
the source I'm making hourly source snapshots available. The url is

http://mono.enter-the-dragon.com/

At the moment there is just a raw directory listing of tarballs, in the 
format MonoDevelop-Date-Hour.tar.bz2 If in doubt you want the one with 
the highest number. These are just pure compressed tarballs of my 
monodevelop tree, as checked out from anonsvn.

At some point soon I'll sort out daily patches so you don't have to 
download a full tarball every time, and some pretty html. In the 
meantime you can either download tarballs regurlarly or download a 
tarball as a seed and then keep it updated from the svn.
