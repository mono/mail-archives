[1]

This works on MS.NET, as long "c:\tmp\foo" is pointing to a subdirectory
of the application's directory:

<configuration>
    <runtime>
       <assemblyBinding xmlns="urn:schemas-microsoft-com:asm.v1">
          <probing privatePath="c:\tmp\foo"/>
       </assemblyBinding>
    </runtime>
</configuration>
