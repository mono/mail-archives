Fixed.

The arm-iwmmxt-linux-gnueabi-g++ compiler was in my toolchain but it wasn't being picked up during configure stage. Defining CXX path as below fixed issue.

CXX=/xx/xx/xx/xx/bin/arm-iwmmxt-linux-gnueabi-g++
