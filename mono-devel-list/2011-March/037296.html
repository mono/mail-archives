> I am balbeer from india,currently doing masters in Software engineering.
> Some one please tell me how i should apply in Mono Project. i mean , i have
> interest in some of the projects suggested by Mono project and also i have a
> new idea that i can suggest..
> Now what should i do? weather i should give preference to my project while
> applying or to the existing project in mono project site.

We're always interested in new ideas! You should post your idea on the
mailing list and ask for feedback. Don't just say "I want to work on
X", be more specific, tell us what you want to do, think about how
you'd do it, sell your idea to us and get us excited about it!

That doesn't mean you have to decide now which one to do. We've
already had several different proposals submitted by one student, it's
not a problem. We evaluate them and talk to you to figure out which
one is best.


> For Applying in the same organization for different projects,i have to use
> different application forms or just one application..?
> waiting for a quick response...

It's always one application per project. A soc proposal is for
describing a project so we can evaluate it, so don't make it multiple
choice :)
