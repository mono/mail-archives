  This is caused by a bug in the cygwin win32 headers. See this:

http://cygwin.com/ml/cygwin/2003-09/msg01771.html

Hopefully it will be fixed soon. In the meantime, try changing

mono/metadata/file-io.c. I will check this workaround in
until cygwin
is fixed.
