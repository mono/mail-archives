One thing to keep in mind is that the exact format and specifications for 
MSBuild are still subject to change. Many items are not (fully) documented 
yet. Also, some of the older documentation and MSBuild sample files are not 
compatible with the version of MSBuild being distributed now (such as in the 
VS Express Beta).

Apparently, I'm competing against the Mono effort in developing an MSBuild 
work-alike.
