Also it has not been mentioned, that Microsoft is releasing the C++/CLI
spec to ECMA...
 
http://msdn.microsoft.com/visualc/homepageheadlines/ecma/

It is not published yet to my knowledge, but it does lay the groundwork
for the addition of a C++ front end to an IL compiler. Maybe eventually
GCC will have a flag to add this functionality (even Borland is working
on the spec).

final until Whidby ships.
