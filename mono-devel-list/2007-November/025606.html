This Saturday, the 10th of November, at 10:00am MST, we will be moving
the Mono servers from our current datacenter to our new datacenter.
We're hoping that this move will be completed within 5 hours, but if
problems arise it may take longer.


This move will affect:
 * The main Mono webiste (mono-project.com)
 * The Mono download pages
 * Monologue
 * The Mono documentation


Svn and the mailing lists should not be affected.

This transition will involve DNS changes, so you will have to wait for
the DNS to propagate before you will be able to access these services
again. This could take up to 12 hours.
