Oups. I was looking for the solution during 2 hours without success. But
I just found it.

gecko is using the environment variable MOZ_PLUGIN_PATH. Just setting
this:

export MOZ_PLUGIN_PATH=/usr/lib/mozilla/plugins/

and it works.
