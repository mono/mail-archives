Hi All,

There's an Alpha of 2.4 out and I'm hoping to get lots of input from folks
on this list.

We're (finally) building and testing on Mono (both profiles) on several
different platforms. That's the good news. The bad news is that we have a
number of NUnit test failures when running under Linux. We _believe_ that
most of those are due to platform assumptions in our tests, rather than in
NUnit itself.

To build for Mono, just grab the source zip from sourceforge and use 
   nant mono build
   nant mono release build
   nant mono-2.0 build
   etc.
