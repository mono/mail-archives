The proper way to do this is to dynamically lookup and invoke the method, i.e:

if (windows) {
    MethodInfo mi = typeof (FOO).getMethod ("BAR");
    mi.Invoke (....);
}

The MethodInfo could be cached somewhere of course to speed this up.
