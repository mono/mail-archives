On hppa, the stack grows upward towards higher addresses. This patch
introduces an architecture compile flag (MONO_ARCH_STACK_GROWS_UP) and
fixes two places in the generic exception code where the stack direction
is expected to grow downwards. There may be other cases that I haven't
found yet.

Please apply.
