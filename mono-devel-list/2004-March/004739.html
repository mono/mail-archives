Hello:

  It's a bug, try with (psi.UseShellExecute = false;). Example:

ProcessStartInfo psi = new ProcessStartInfo ("mcs", "-t:library
test.cs");
psi.UseShellExecute = false;
System.Diagnostics.Process p;
p = System.Diagnostics.Process.Start(psi);
