> We have a pretty big set of .NET solutions/projects that we would like

> of C#, VB.NET, and one small C++ project that does some native Windows

You don't need to compile your solution with Mono to get it running
on Linux/OSX. We are building our Assemblies on .NET and they are
running under Mono just fine. 

To get it running you must ensure that your assemblies are fully
managed (no C++) and compiled as Release.
