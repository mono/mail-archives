Just try it. :)
If you download MoMA, you'll have the latest Mono definitions (ie: 2.4), so
your app will be tested against Mono 2.4. You can download older definition
files from the MoMA downloads, too, in case you'd like to know if your app
may work with older Mono versions.

Note that MoMA telling you your app will work or not is not sufficient, only
an indication, you need to actually test it.
