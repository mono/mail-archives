Well, our VB compiler is in Alpha stage, and didn't recognize the syntax, 
generated by ASP.Net from your page.

Specifically I think we still doesn't handle all the variants of the 
AddressOf operator that is used in the line printed. That line is generated 
to properly render some control.

Currently C# is the best option for doing aspx pages in Mono, because mcs 
(our C# compiler) is very mature. We are working to make mbas (our vb.net 
compiler) as solid and useful as mcs but we probably will expend some 
months, to arrive there.
