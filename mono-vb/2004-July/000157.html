Probably you may hit some licensing restrictions to use Crystal Report.NET 
assemblies outside windows/.NET, even if technically possible (it may not, 
because they may have unmanaged dependencies in their solution).

If you generate PDFs in Windows and just offer them for download through 
mono asp.net implementation it would be fine. Also you can try to generate 
PDFs using free technologies, instead.
