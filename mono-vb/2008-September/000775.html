Hi,

 

Third Party Controls (OCX controls) are native windows binaries which cannot
execute on any other platform.

 

This means that Mono/VB can’t do it on Linux either.

You can use MoMA (www.mono-project.com/MoMA) to get an idea if your
application will work with Mono or not.
