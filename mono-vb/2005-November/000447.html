It's a namespace mismatch. The codebehind class you are compiling is
WebForm1 inside no namespace, either change HelloVB.aspx.vb to have the
namespace/end namespace pair or change the HelloVB.aspx to have
something like this at the end of the last line.

 Inherits="WebForm1"%>

Either way you'll still find the mbas bug around calling subs without
the empty parenthesis pair, stops your page from working. We are working
on that bug, but we don't know when it will be gone.
