OK, I found it. 

There is a separate dll for the Gdk. I was not aware of this. 

So we need to compile like:

vbnc -r:/usr/lib/mono/gtk-sharp-2.0/gtk-sharp.dll
-r:/usr/lib/mono/gtk-sharp-2.0/gdk-sharp.dll application.vb
