Hi All,
Today I have added few more tests for mbas.

Also, I tried to compile a document for bugs found (attached) in mbas
by running these test cases.
The idea for compiling this bug list is, now VB is in such a unstable
state that filing bug in bugzilla may not be a very good option. I had a
chat with Miguel regarding this and he suggested me to go this way.
This list is mainly to help the VB community who wants to keep track of
problems and to have a common understanding among each other. This
should also be helpful for those who are wiling to start contributing to
VB.
For the time being I will be glad to maintain the list.

Please feel free to send your comments on this and add bugs to the list
as and when you find.

Thanks.
Anirban.
