On Tue, 2003-02-11 at 16:23, John Hunter wrote:
> 
> I have a toolbar with 6 or seven widgets.  If I increase the number of
> elements in the toolbar properties, it gives me a new space at the end
> of the toolbar.  If I want to add the new tool somewhere in the middle
> of the toolbar, the only way I know how to do it is to cut and paste
> the individual widgets 1 by 1 to reposition them.
> 
> Is there a better way to add a widget in a toolbar at a desired
> position?

Yes, you can use the context-sensitive menus.

Move the mouse over a toolbar button, press the right mouse button, and
then select 'Insert Item Before' or 'Insert Item After, in the toolbar
submenu.
