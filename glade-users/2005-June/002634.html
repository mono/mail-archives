On Sun, 2005-06-26 at 14:05 -0700, Fred J. Stephens wrote:
> Can't figure out how to use/install them. gnomedb site says they are
> included with recent versions, but I never see them. I have noticed that
>  a grayed out group for them is visible on the pallet, until I open a
> new  project-either GTK+ or Gnome. Does that mean they aren't installed?

You have to compile Glade with support for gnomedb enabled (with
the '--enable-gnome-db' configure option). I think some/most Linux
distros don't do that, so you don't get gnome-db support in the
Glade package.

You'll have to compile Glade yourself, from the tarball.
