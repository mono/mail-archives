ok, I think I've got it.

I used a non-homogeneous GtkBox (horizontal) with two fields. I put a button in each field. Then I clicked on the first (left) button and in the Packing field I set Pack Type to "Start." I clicked on the second (right) button and in the Packing field I set Pack Type to "End." I used the defaults for Fill and Expand for the buttons, which were "Yes" and "No", respectively.

That's pretty neat and tidy.  Glade is great.
