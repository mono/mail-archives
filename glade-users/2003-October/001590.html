'Lo Paulo,

I've written a tutorial for Glade2.  it's at www.kplug.org/tutorials,  
along with a Glade1 tutorial.  I'm afraid the examples in both are in  
C.  There is no manual per se, yet.  I am currently writing something  
more complete than the tutorial though.

The Deprecated widgets should be around for another 4 years or so.  If  
you want your code to last longer than that, then use the ListView and  
model.  You can find out more on the gtk site, where there are  
tutorials.  Try www.gtk.org.
