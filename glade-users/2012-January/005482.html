>> How do I get the GtkWindow pointer from the GtkWidget I get after the
>> builder?
>
> With the standard type casting macros:
>
> GtkWidget *widget;
> GtkWindow *window;
>
> widget = gtk_builder_get_object (builder, "window");
> window = GTK_WINDOW (widget);

GTK+ documentation.
