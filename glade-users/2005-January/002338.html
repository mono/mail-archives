On Mon, 2005-01-03 at 18:29, - tomahawk - wrote:
> I currently have an app made with glade 2.0.1 and I am planning on upgrading 
> to GTK 2.4 and glade 2.6.  I was wondering if there are any major issues 
> that I might encounter.

It should be fine. Let me know if it isn't.

The only major thing that has been changed is the toolbars, since they
were completely rewritten in GTK+ 2.4. But Glade should automatically
update your toolbars to the new style. Of course your app will only work
with GTK+ 2.4 then.
