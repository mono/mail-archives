I think the tarballs for glib, gtk+ and glade are all cross-platform, so
if you want to compile them yourself on win32 you can.

But the packages on http://gladewin32.sourceforge.net/ are compiled,
easy-to-install packages, just for win32. You could email the maintainer
and ask when the next release will be.
