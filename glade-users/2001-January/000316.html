Dear List Members:

As you may have heard, Helix Code, Inc. has changed its name to Ximian
(ZIM-ee-un), Inc.  We are now in the process of moving all of our
mailing lists, web pages, and addresses to the ximian.com
domain. We're doing our best to make this as painless as possible, so
we're going to keep using the Helix Code message format until February
9, 2001.

Beginning February 9, the name change will affect mailing lists in the
following ways:

1: Lists with [HC listname] in the subject header will now have [Ximian
listname] instead.

2: You will need to update any email filters dependent on the "Helix
Code" name or the helixcode.com domain.

3: You can still send mail to your list at the helixcode.com address,
although we encourage you to begin using ximian.com now.

4: The name change will NOT interrupt your list subscription and you
do NOT need to resubscribe.

Nothing else about this list or our company has changed.  If you have
any questions, please visit our web page at http://www.ximian.com/,
where we have a full announcement and a FAQ.

Sincerely,
The Ximian, Inc. Network Team
