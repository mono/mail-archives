> I want to set a label frame inside of Glade-3 but it doesn't seem to be

Label of GtkFrame is widget on it's own and is not limited to
GtkLabel. But for users convenience, Glade packs GtkLabel and
GtkAlignment. They are both accessible from widget tree.
