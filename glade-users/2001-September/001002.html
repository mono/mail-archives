I'm having some problems with two dialogs that I've done with glade, and
that are not rendered by libglade in exactly the same fashion that I see
them with glade.

In the first of them (lists.glade) I have a vbox embeded in a GtkDialog.
The vbox has 3 items inside, and I've put a "spacing" of 8.  This
"spacing" is not respected by libglade (which seems to render the dialog
as if this spacing was 0).

In the second of them (gnome-lists.glade) I have 4 buttons in a
gnome-dialog (from left to right: Cancel, Ok, Customize, Restore).
Cancel and Ok are stock buttons, and are rendered right, but Customize &
Restore both have a custom icon (the "undo" icon for Restore and the
"properties" icon for Customize) and this icon is not rendered by
libglade.

Attached are the two files and 4 little screenshots showing the relevant
parts of the two dialogs rendered by glade and by libglade.
