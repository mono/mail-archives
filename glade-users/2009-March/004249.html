Is there a really bad bug in Glade 3.6 that makes it unusable for you ?
(please let me know; I just now released it), otherwise, is there a reason
why Glade 3.4 does not work for you ?

For your question, no you cannot place a widget into a drawing area
(a drawing area is not a container) but you can place one into the tab
of a notebook by first removing the label (you can then add an hbox
and alignment if you want).
