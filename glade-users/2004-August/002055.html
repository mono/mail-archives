On Mon, 2004-08-30 at 18:49, Lucas Smithen wrote:
> Where does Glade get it fonts from when starting a project?  I am using 
> KDE 3.3.

Glade uses the GNOME/GTK+ libraries rather than the KDE ones.

GTK+ uses fontconfig to locate fonts. See the fonts.conf file, probably
in /etc/fonts. I think the <dir> options specify where to look for the
fonts.
