Hi,

>You need to find out where the rpm installed the libgnomeui-2.0.pc file
>and add that path to your PKG_CONFIG_PATH environment variable.  
>Configure should run fine after you've done that.

The problem was that
    libgnomeui-2.0.5-lpt.1.i686.rpm
was not installing any libgnomeui-2.0.pc file.

I googled and googled and finally learnt that
I had to install:
    libgnomeui-devel-2.0.5-lpt.1.i686.rpm
also. That solved my problem.

Many thanks to Franz, Rikke Giles, and Seth Remington 
for their inputs.
