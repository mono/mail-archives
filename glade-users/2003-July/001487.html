Gunther,

You'll have to direct the output into a file.

Basically the command would be:

libglade-convert filename > newfilename

Then tell glade to open the newfilename.  Basically I made a new 
subdirectory /Projects2 for my glade2 projects.  I then put the 
converted files into their own subdirectorys in that directory, just 
like glade1 did it.

There are several tutorials on the net for glade, one of them is mine 
(I have a tutorial for glade1 and another for glade2).  You can also 
find tutorials listed on the glade homepage at glade.gnome.org.

My tutorials are linked at www.kplug.org/tutorials.shtml

Cheers,

Rikke
