  If you look how a button or a widget is added to a toolbar, you will see that
the widget added will be added to a GtkToolbarChild. The label and the icon is
actually part of this structure (GtkToolbarChild). The widget itself has no
bearing on the icon or label.

 Take a lookd at gtktoolbar.c

  Hope this helps.
