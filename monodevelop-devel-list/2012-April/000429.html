libgnomeui-2.24.5-3.1.2.x86_64 on my system.

 From a quick look at the dependencies then my guess is that 
gnome-sharp2-complete or gnome-sharp2 is badly packaged and should pull 
in libgnomeui as a dependency but doesn't define it. Try installing it 
and see if that fixes the problem.
