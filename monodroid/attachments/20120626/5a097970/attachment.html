<blockquote class="gmail_quote" style="margin:0 0 0 .8ex;border-left:1px #ccc solid;padding-left:1ex">I am using nant to build and package my apk. Here is my build target:<br>
<br>
 <target name="build"><br>
<br>
    <msbuild project="${projectfile}" target="SignAndroidPackage"<br>
verbose="${verbose}"><br>
      <property name="Configuration" value="${configuration}"<br>
verbose="${verbose}" /><br>
    </msbuild><br>
<br>
<br>
    <delete file="bin\OCTech.Mobile.Applications.TouchScan.apk"<br>
failonerror="true" verbose="${verbose}"/><br>
<br>
<br>
    <call target="zipalign" verbose="${verbose}"/><br>
<br>
<br>
    <delete file="bin\OCTech.Mobile.Applications.TouchScan-Signed.apk"<br>
failonerror="true" verbose="${verbose}"/><br>
<br>
    <call target="release" verbose="${verbose}"/><br>
  </target><br>
<br>
  <target name="zipalign"><br>
<br>
    <exec program="${androidZipAlign}" verbose="${verbose}"><br>
      <arg value="-v"/><br>
      <arg value="4"/><br>
      <arg file="bin\OCTech.Mobile.Applications.TouchScan-Signed.apk"/><br>
      <arg file="bin\OCTech.Mobile.Applications.TouchScan.apk"/><br>
    </exec><br>
  </target><br>
<br>
<br>
A couple of notes:<br>
<br>
1) You'll need to set the ${androidZipAlign} property to the zipalign exe in<br>
the Android sdk<br>
2) My release target just copies the apk to another directory.<br>
