On Dec 19, 2012, at 6:53 AM, John Murray <john at murray.gb.com> wrote:
> can anyone tell me the steps I need to take to de install 4.4.41 and go back to whatever was current last August?

1. Go to: https://store.xamarin.com/account/Products
2. For a given license/product, click the "Show Recent Releases" link.

The Show Recent Releases link gives download links to the .msi installer for a specific release. I currently see links going back to 4.2.2 from May 24 2012; presumably you want the 4.2.4 release.
