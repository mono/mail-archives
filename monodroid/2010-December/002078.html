http://monodroid.net/Documentation/Guides/Using_Android_Assets

Assets provide a way to include arbitrary files like text, xml, fonts, 
music, and video in your
application. If you try to include these files as "resources", Android 
will process them into its
resource system and you will not be able to get the raw data. If you 
want to access data
untouched, Assets are one way to do it.

Clear it depends on the use.
