Based on my experience with MT, I would also point out that using Mono* does not magically save you from having to know about the underlying platform.  

For iOS, understanding the native API, UI guidelines and conventions, and quirks of the platform are still key to getting anything done.  MT helps reduce friction by [mostly] removing Obj-C/Cocoa from the mix, and by providing you with a familiar language and a lot of great framework features that you otherwise would do without.  But it's not a magic bullet.


I don't have much experience with MDroid yet, but I'd guess the same line of thought applies there too.
