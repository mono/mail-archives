Microsoft will also have to show some love to his “early adopters” and constantly update the OS on the old devices too. I am very curious who will win:  Microsoft or hardware OEM’s ? For Apple is simple they make the hardware, they make the OS so they decide if to update or not a device and till now their strategy wins (the owners know they will be able to update at least at the next major version of the OS so they have an updated phone for at least 2 years). The downside of Android is that most of the hardware OEM’s won’t update “old” devices to the next major version so as a user you are “out” in less than 6 months. In this moment it costs less to buy an iPhone that lasts 2 years than the equivalent of 4 Android phones. I really hope Microsoft won’t screw things up.
