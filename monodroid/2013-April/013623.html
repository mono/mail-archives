On Apr 17, 2013, at 9:21 AM, krish <kickrish at gmail.com> wrote:
> hi,
>    m suffering from deployment problem...i tried one thing...i copied the .apk signed file(E:\MonoAddModifyDeleteView\MonoAddModifyDeleteView\bin\Debug) to sd card of tablet and installed it ...it is installed on the tablet but giving error AllControls.AllControls has stopped unexpectedly (Force Close)...

That won't work, because Debug builds are not "stand-alone"; they have other dependencies which the IDE should be installing for you.

The question is, why isn't the IDE able to install the app?

Please enable device deployment logging:

	http://docs.xamarin.com/guides/android/deployment,_testing,_and_metrics/diagnostics#Device_Deployment_Logs

You may need to restart Visual Studio after enabling the deployment logging. This will write a `monodroid.log` file to your Desktop. Please provide the `monodroid.log` file.

You may also need to provide your devices `adb logcat` output:

	http://docs.xamarin.com/guides/android/deployment%2C_testing%2C_and_metrics/android_debug_log
