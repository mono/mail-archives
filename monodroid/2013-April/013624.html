I think what may be happening is that when you deploy directly (that is 
device connected to computer and tell the compiler to send it to a 
device), the runtime etc is copied onto the device followed by the app. 
If you just copy the apk, the runtime isn't copied so that may be the 
cause of what you're seeing. With the non-debug, you don't get the 
problem as the runtime is shipped with the app (IIRC)

I know on my Nexus I deploy both debug and non-debug without any issues 
at all.
