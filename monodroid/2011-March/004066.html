Open up your Project Properties, go to the Build tab, and in the Treat warnings as errors section, select All.

Then you will know the true meaning of "warning free code." :-)

You can also view Warnings in the Error list, but there's a checkbox there to hide Warnings so they might not currently be visible for you.
