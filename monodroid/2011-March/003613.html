On Mar 12, 2011, at 6:25 AM, Amir Waldman wrote:
> I'm trying to create a RelativeLayout programmatically but can't find
> constants for LEFT_OF, BELOW, ABOVE, etc...

Many constants in Mono for Android are moved into enumerations, which is the case here. You want the Android.Widget.LayoutRules [0] enum, e.g. LayoutRules.LeftOf.

 - Jon

[0] http://docs.monodroid.net/index.aspx?link=T%3aAndroid.Widget.LayoutRules
