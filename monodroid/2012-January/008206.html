My opinion is that errors should be caught as early as possible. The normal 
order of error detection is compiler, build, deployment, run time. There is a 
large increase of the cost and difficulty of error correction as you go down 
this sequence. As they say: "More computing sins are committed in the name of  
efficiency (without necessarily achieving it) than for any other single  reason 
- including blind stupidity."

 Bruce Rosner
