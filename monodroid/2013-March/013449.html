Service: If you want to do something in background , this will be running
always in background even if the application closed. You can create this in
separate process and also you can give your service to other app if you
want. Downloading any content or Music is good example

Broadcast Reciever: Usually system will send some info which can be recieved
by your app if you would wish to ,by registering. And you can do something
what you want when that thing happens by using onReceive method.
