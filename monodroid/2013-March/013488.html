Your original question was regarding why it wasn't present as global::Android.Resource.Styleable. The answer is that we parse android.jar to generate our bindings, and as demonstrated earlier in this thread, the Android SDK android.jar does not contain an android.R.styleable class.

Note that the android.jar provided with the Android SDK is _not_ the android.jar on the device, so it is not at all surprising that your hand binding works.
