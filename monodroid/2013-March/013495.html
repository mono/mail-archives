the main page of the article
http://docs.xamarin.com/guides/android/user_interface/working_with_listviews_and_adapters
contains the link to the ZIP
http://docs.xamarin.com/static/guides/android/user_interface/working_with_listviews_and_adapters/index/Resources/SimpleCursorTableAdapter.zip
which will give you a complete working example (that works for me).

looking closer at your screenshot, it seems you're missing these lines from
the HomeScreen Activity OnCreate method:

            SetContentView(Resource.Layout.HomeScreen);
            listView = FindViewById<ListView>(Resource.Id.List);

if you were just following the cursor sample, you may have missed this
code, since it is actually introduced on a difference page of the article
http://docs.xamarin.com/guides/android/user_interface/working_with_listviews_and_adapters/part_2_-_populating_a_listview_with_data


anyway, i hope that helps!
