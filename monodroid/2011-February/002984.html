No, its because the emulator has a virtual firewall and it can only see your
host machine on 10.0.2.2. If you're trying to access a service hosted on
another machine somewhere on the internet or LAN it can see it as normal,
its only when trying to connect to services on your development machine that
you have to do this (as far as my experience goes)
