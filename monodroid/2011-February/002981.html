For anyone having the same problem when starting MonoDroid development, this
is how I got it working;

Add your service reference.
In code, after you've instantiated your service reference, change the Url;

serviceRef.Url = "http://10.0.2.2/someService.svc"
call methods as normal.

Thanks to all that led me in the right direction ;)
