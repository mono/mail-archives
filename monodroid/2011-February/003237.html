I bought a Nook Color a few months ago and had a heck of a time getting the laptop to install the right drivers and get ADB going, but a few nights ago I finally made a break through and I'm now pushing Monodroid apps to it. I would recommend you guys get one if you want a fun generic Android device for the home for a good price. I think the hardware you get for the price just can't be beat right now. Plus, there's some guys on XDA developing HoneyComb for the Nook Color -- I ran it off an SD Card for a few days and it was so cool, cant wait for the hard version not running off an SD Card...it will be super impressive. Anyways just wanted to let you guys know about Nook Color, if you didn't already. Have a good one
