Hi John,

A lot of the Java-isms have been ported to .NET-isms where appropriate. In
your case the Activity.getIntent() and Activity.setIntent() methods have
been exposed as the Activity.Intent property.

In general, you'll find info here
http://monodroid.net/Documentation/API_Design in the Design Principles
section.

Dan
