On Feb 25, 2011, at 7:23 PM, Paul Camacho wrote:
> Can anyone give an idea of how I can try my luck at MDr with the honeycomb preview?

That depends on how you define "with Honeycomb." :-)

If you mean "run on Honeycomb," it should work now without any difficulty -- I've installed MonoDroid apps within the Honeycomb simulator and they've Just Worked. (They took up 1/6 of the screen...but they did work.)

If you mean "use Honeycomb-specific APIs," the answer is "not yet." There is no current timeframe, other than "as soon as possible after Google bothers to release 11.xml at [0]." (Which, frankly, I would have thought would have happened by now...)

Note, though, that widgets appear to be a pre-existing feature with added use now (as the screen is bigger and can thus fit more/larger widgets), and you can create widgets with MonoDroid; see [1].

 - Jon

[0] http://android.git.kernel.org/?p=platform/frameworks/base.git;a=tree;f=api;hb=HEAD

[1] https://github.com/mono/monodroid-samples/tree/master/SimpleWidget
