> So I've been messing with animations quite a bit. Everything seems to
> work pretty darn well, but randomly I run into the following
> exception: 
> 
> 
> E/mono    ( 3184): [0xafd4a828:] EXCEPTION handling:
> System.MissingMethodException: No constructor found for
> Android.Views.Animations.Animation
> +IAnimationListenerAdapter::.ctor(System.IntPtr)
> E/mono    ( 3184): [0xafd4a828:] EXCEPTION handling:
> System.NotSupportedException: Unable to activate instance of type
> Android.Views.Animations.Animation+IAnimationListenerAdapter from
> native handle 454dc1e8

>From looking at this with no source code to verify my thoughts, I would
say you are most likely seeing a known GC related issue that we believe
to be fixed in p12 and will be released shortly.

I can also guess that you are implementing the Listener interface
without subclassing Java.Lang.Object based on the presence of an adapter
in the activation problem.  We recommend that you derive your custom
listeners from Java.Lang.Object.
