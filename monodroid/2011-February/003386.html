On Feb 28, 2011, at 3:33 PM, Chris Erickson wrote:
> The only thing I changed was that I replaced the WCF proxies with
> generated soap proxies...and that assembly build just fine.

Run `ildasm` on the generated SOAP proxies, and see what assemblies they reference. In particular, which mscorlib.dll version do they reference? If they're referencing 2.0.0.0, that's the problem, as the linker can't find mscorlib.dll v2.0.0.0, as MonoDroid includes mscorlib.dll 2.0.5.0.
