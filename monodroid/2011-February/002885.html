Hallo Wally

yesterday we found this solution for us:

We changed a section in the MSBuild file
"%ProgramFiles%\MSBuid\Novell\Novell.MonoDroid.Common.targets":

The original section 

<Target Name="SignAndroidPackage"
	Inputs="$(MSBuildAllProjects);$(ApkFileIntermediate)"
	Outputs="$(ApkFileSigned)"
	DependsOnTargets="Build;_ResolveAndroidSigningKey">

becomes

<Target Name="SignAndroidPackage"
	Inputs="$(MSBuildAllProjects);$(ApkFileIntermediate)"
	Outputs="$(ApkFileSigned)"
	DependsOnTargets="PackageForAndroid;_ResolveAndroidSigningKey">

Herbert
