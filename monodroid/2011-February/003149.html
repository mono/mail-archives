The short version is that I know of no MonoDroid-specific changes to System.Threading that would impact the thread pool, so we should be using ye normal Mono .NET 4.0 profile behaviors.

When in doubt, check the mono/mcs/class/corlib sources and grep for MONODROID or MOBILE.

Keep in mind that the machine.config we ship with MonoDroid has no threading-specific settings, and System.Configuration.dll isn't present (so you can't provide your own .config either).

 - Jon
