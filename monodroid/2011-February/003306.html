>     MOTODEV Studio allows you to specify settings from their Eclipse
>     plugin. However, you can start the emulator from the command line:
>
>     [path-to-sdk]\tools\emulator -avd XOOM -scale 0.75
>
>     where XOOM is the name of the device image.
>
