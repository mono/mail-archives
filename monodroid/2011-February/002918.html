That's not generally the approach you should take in Android apps. Rather
than thinking about navigating between views, you should be thinking in
terms of navigating between activities, each of which corresponds to a view.

It's worth reading through the docs a bit to get a feel for how the platform
is set up: http://developer.android.com/guide/topics/fundamentals.html
