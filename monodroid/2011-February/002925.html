"That's not generally the approach you should take in Android apps. Rather
than thinking about navigating between views, you should be thinking in
terms of navigating between activities, each of which corresponds to a
view."

Is this really true? It seems with objects like the ViewFlipper, it is at
least somewhat intended that you could navigate between views as well as
between Activities. I like to do animations between different views, and I
haven't yet seen a way to do that between activities. That may be due to my
ignorance though.
