On Tue, Feb 1, 2011 at 10:22 PM, Joseph Hill <jhill at novell.com> wrote:
> Hi all
> Over the next few previews there will be a number of changes
> reflecting decisions we've made for the 1.0 product release:
>
> * MonoDroid 1.0 will not offer a shared runtime option for the
> Android Market.
> * We will be moving Mono.Android.dll to the development
> shared runtime.
>
> We feel this provides a number of benefits:
>
> * Developers will have a much quicker build/deploy/debug cycle.
> * The end-user experience is nicer, in that users will not have to
> download an additional package.
>
> While we may provide a shared-runtime suitable for the
> Android Market at some point after our 1.0 launch, we believe
> that the potential for frequent updates to the runtime during
> the first several months would likely just generate frustration
> for users and developers.
>
> We're very interested in your feedback on these product plans.
> Please feel free to contact the team with your feedback and
> suggestions via monodroid at novell.com
>
> Alternatively, please feel free to email me directly.
