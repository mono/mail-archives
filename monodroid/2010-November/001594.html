On Wed, 2010-11-10 at 19:55 -0800, Koushik Dutta wrote:
> That is not possible, because to participate in the Android VM, you
> must be inherited from Java.Lang.Object

Thus the "workaround" of an internal Java.Lang.Object subclass which
overrides Object.hashCode(), Object.equals(), and Object.toString(),
delegating them to a wrapped System.Object.

It's not perfect for all scenarios, but for simple collections it
appears to be workable.
