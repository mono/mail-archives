On Nov 28, 2010, at 12:24 PM, Daniel Lyonnais wrote:
> ...there seems to be several different methods to read and write text files.  So my question - Is there a preferred method for Monodroid?  FileInputStream ? FileReader?  InputStreamReader? BufferedReader?

I would suggest sticking to the .NET types if at all possible (System.IO.FileStream, etc.), for two reasons:

 1. This will be more portable to other platforms (MonoTouch, WP7, etc.)
 2. I suspect (but have not tested) that this will be faster.

I suspect that System.IO.FileStream will be faster because there will be fewer JNI transitions involved when reading and writing data, but we've done ~no profiling to see how much overhead JNI involves.

 - Jon
