Some troubleshooting things to try:

- Are you waiting for the device to be fully booted?
- When you say you have to "close it down, then restart", are you 
restarting the emulator or VS?
- We get our data from Android's adb, see if it shows up with "adb devices".
- See if killing the adb.exe process(es) in Task Manager fixes it.
