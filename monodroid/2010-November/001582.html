That's right, you no longer need to use ActivityFlags.NewTask (as of P6, I
think).  You can just do:

StartActivity(typeof(SettingsActivity));

You can see it in the context in a demo app I used in my talk last weekend,
if it helps:
https://github.com/gshackles/Sample-Projects/blob/master/MonoDroid/BrowserDemo/BrowserDemo/BrowserActivity.cs#L134
