Today a couple of questions came up:

* Are we working on Mono for Blackberry?
* Are we working on Mono for Symbian?

The answer to both is no.   We are currently focused on delivering a great
experience for Mono on Android.   It will be a top-notch solution, and this
requires work, polish, time and to manually groom our work until it can
graduate from nice to fabulous.

Working on any other platform at this point would prevent us from graduating
to fabulous.   We would merely be passable.

Once both MonoTouch and MonoDroid are both fabulous and we feel they are
both perfect, we can evaluate new platforms.

Miguel
