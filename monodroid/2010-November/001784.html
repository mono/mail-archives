> The issue is there is no inherent link between an Activity and a Layout,
> so there's not really any way for us to know what layout to generate
> strongly typed members for.
>
> ie: Does your HomeActivity use mylist.xml or mytree.xml for its layout?
>
> One suggestion I've seen would be for MonoDroid to generate an activity
> for each layout that you could then derive from.
>
> ie: We would generate a MyListActivity and MyTreeActivity, and you would
> do this:
>
> HomeActivity : MyListActivity
>
> There are several potential issues with this like naming and you
> couldn't have your own Activity subclass if you wanted all your
> Activities to have the same subclass.
>
> Another potential solution would be an attribute that would cause
> MonoDroid to generate a partial class with strongly typed members:
>
> [Layout (Resources.Layout.mylist)]
> HomeActivity : Activity
