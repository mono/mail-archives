This often helps too:

ServicePointManager.ServerCertificateValidationCallback = (sender,
cert, chain, ssl) =>  true;

It makes it ignore cert errors. I dont think it'll ever drop back to
non-SSL, but if it's self-signed, it helps a lot.

Not a great idea for production tho!
