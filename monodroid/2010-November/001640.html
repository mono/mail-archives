On Tue, 2010-11-16 at 09:42 +0000, Mike Warriner wrote:
> Is it likely that Blackberry support will be forthcoming in within the
> next 6 months?

No, and BlackBerry will never be supported until they change their SDK.
Specifically, they don't allow native code execution of any form [0],
nor do they expose the Java mechanism for loading native code, as they
do not provide System.loadLibrary(String) [1].

I suppose if someone has lots of free time they could explore a
Grasshopper-like [2] solution of translating IL into Java bytecode
(assuming that Blackberry actually supports bytecode), but that would be
a far cry from embedding Mono as is done with MonoDroid and MonoTouch.
