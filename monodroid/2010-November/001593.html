On Thu, 2010-11-11 at 12:37 +0800, Deryk Robosson wrote:
> Does such a creature exist?

Kinda-sorta.  There's the Mono.Android.xml XML documentation comment
file, which can be found at e.g.:

C:\Program Files (x86)\Reference Assemblies\Microsoft\Framework\MonoDroid\v2.0\Mono.Android.xml

You may have it in another location, but you *should* have it (as it's
used by VS to show type/member documentation), and most of what's at
docs.monodroid.net are in Mono.Android.xml (and OpenTK.xml contains the
rest of what's currently at docs.monodroid.net).

This might not be the best way to _view_ the docs, but they exist.
