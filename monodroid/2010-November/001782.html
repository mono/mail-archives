Ah I hadn't seen the SetClass and SetClassName methods, I like it.

Personally I have been using your second method, but without strings...

StartActivity(new Intent(this.BaseContext, new MyActivityClass().Class));

or

StartActivity(new Intent(this.BaseContext, new
MyActivityClass().Class).AddFlags(ActivityFlags.NewTask));

> i.AddFlags(ActivityFlags.NewTask);
> StartActivity(i);
>

> i.SetClassName(this, "uicontrols.radiocheckboxes");
