Adding back mailing list dropped before...

I may agree with you. But in reality, large of our business logic is custom
HTTP video streaming which specifically been developed to accomodate
Silverlight MediaEmement by implementing abstract MediaStreamSource. It is
not GUI, but core Silverlight video rendering technology. Re-implementing it
with different video rendering technology is huge draw back for us. I may
understand why MediaElement/MediaStreamSource are not part of MonoDroid
though. It is probably too difficult to implement as a wrapper.

Alex
