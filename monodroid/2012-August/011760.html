On Aug 10, 2012, at 4:16 AM, MysteriX <wolf.benjamin at arc-greenlab.de> wrote:
> i try to place a second layout in my project.

I would suggest _not_ doing what you're doing. Instead of calling SetContentView() within your Click handler, you should instead call StartActivity() to a new Activity subclass which has its own SetContentView() invocation.

This is more consistent with Android in general, and allows you to sanely handle screen rotation/etc.
