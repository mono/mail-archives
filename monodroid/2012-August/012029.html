Tabs in android are at the top of the screen. My suggestion is to follow
the native format as users are used to this. You should also look at the
fragmenttabspager demo
https://github.com/xamarin/monodroid-samples/blob/master/Support4/Support4/Activities/FragmentTabsPager.cs.
I believe tabs at bottom is custom
