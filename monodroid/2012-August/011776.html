 From the names I think those types are obfuscated (the type names are like
'a', 'b', ...) and prevents bindings sanity.
You should filter them out by describing "removal" in Metadata.xml in XPath
for obj/Debug/api.xml like:

<metadata>
   <remove-node
api="/api/package[@name='<the_package_name>']/class[string-length(@name)
= 1]" />
   <remove-node
api="/api/package[@name='<the_package_name>']/interface[string-length(@name)
= 1]" />
</metadata>

Atsushi Eno
