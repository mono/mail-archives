You can just upload the file to a web server, and e-mail the link to your
users. When the users click on the link from their device, it will install.

Or, you can attach the APK to an e-mail, and have the users open the APK on
their device. They will see a button to install the APK on the device.

Of course, your users will need to enable the installation of non-market
apps for these last two to work.

If you're testing zubhium looks promising (think TestFlight for Android).
