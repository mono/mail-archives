On Jul 22, 2012, at 11:23 AM, al23883 <alex.mccurry at gmail.com> wrote:
> I have created five folders in the resource folder...
> 
> Drawable
> Drawable-hdpi
> Drawable-mdpi
> Drawable-ldpi
...
> Everytime I load the app on the emulator, it always selects the images in the Drawable-mdpi folder no matter what size dimensions my emulator is. 

You're using the wrong qualifiers:

	http://developer.android.com/guide/practices/screens_support.html#qualifiers

The ?dpi qualifiers are for display _density_, not display size. Changing your display size will not impact it unless you also change the display density -- the "Abstracted LCD Density" hardware property in your AVD configuration.

If you want different images/layouts depending on display size, you need to use the size configuration modifiers: small, normal, large, xlarge.
