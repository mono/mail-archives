On Jul 10, 2012, at 5:35 PM, al23883 wrote:
> Where can I get the Mono for Android 4.2.4 beta release from?

Use the automatic updater.

Within Visual Studio:

1. Click Tools > Options
2. In the left-hand tree, click Mono for Android
3. In the right-hand side, under the Mono for Android Extension header, select the [ ] Notify of updates of quality [Beta] drop-down.
4. Click the Check Now link.

Within MonoDevelop/OS X:

1. Click MonoDevelop > Check for Updates
2. In the Update Channel drop-down, select Beta
3. Click the Switch Channel button.
