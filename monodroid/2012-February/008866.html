Do you have ARMv5 support enabled in your release build? Or just ARMv7?

You can check by `unzip`ing your .apk. Do you have a lib/armeabi directory? If you have just a lib/armeabi-v7a directory and not lib/armeabi, then this will fail on devices which do not support ARMv7, and your HTC Magic is one such device (it's ARMv6).
