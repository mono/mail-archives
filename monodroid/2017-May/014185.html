This is what I want so badly. My friends and  I love "playing"  hearthstone  together...  that is,  spectating each others games and chatting about it. But once you have 3 or more  people trying to  discuss one game,  things start to get a  bit ugly. A "party  mode" or something  would be such a fun implement!

Hearthstone has great potential to become a social game. That is evidenced by its  extreme success on streaming  sites such as Twitch  where over  25,000 people  can be  seen just watching  and  chatting about casual Hearthstone games  at any time  of  day... Streamers like Trump or Kripp can attract more viewers  by just sitting around  and playing arena  matches than some other game's tournaments do. It could  really do well by moving at least some of  this traffic into the game itself,  allowing  people to watch and socialize (and even play) while actually in the game!
