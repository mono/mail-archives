Due to the recent changes in the Android repository layout you will need
to go to http://xamarin.com/trial and download the installer again After
launching it, the version in the lower-left corner of the installer
window must say at least 1.0.2.2. Note that you might be required to
uninstall the _installer_ from Control Panel -> Programs and Features
and - ClickOnce sometimes fails to "upgrade" the installer. Hope that helps,
