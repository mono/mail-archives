I have a Galaxy Tab and it works fine in Windows 7.

You must sign up for the Samsung Developers site (free registration) and
download the USB Drivers.

http://innovator.samsungmobile.com/down/cnts/toolSDK.detail.view.do?platformId=1&cntsId=9480


Be sure you put the GalaxyTab in debug mode from Settings-> Applications->
Developers
