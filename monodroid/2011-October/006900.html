This works for me in 1.9.2, but I have to long-press the button to view the context menu.

So this is either a bug fixed in 1.9.x, or you're not long-pressing the button. Just clicking the button will trigger the Button.Click event, and not show the context menu; you need to long-press to get the context menu.
