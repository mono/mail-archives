On Apr 10, 2011, at 6:55 AM, Karl Heinz Brehme Arredondo wrote:
> There is some way to avoid destruction of the database?

At present, with current tooling? No.

At present, manually? Yes. You can use `adb` manually to uninstall and reinstall the app; I'm not sure which sequence of incantations you'd need, but `adb` lists:

	adb install -r FILENAME
		('-r' means reinstall the app, keeping its data)
	adb uninstall -k PACKAGE
		('-k' means keep the data and cache directories)

So in theory, you could manually run `adb uninstall -k PACKAGE` before installing from VS, and that could keep your database. Alternatively, you could use Build->Package to create the .apk and use `adb install -r path/to/package.apk` to preserve the database.

You may also consider filing an enhancement request to allow the IDE to do this automatically.
