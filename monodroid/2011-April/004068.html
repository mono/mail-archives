On Apr 1, 2011, at 8:44 AM, Brian Long wrote:
> In the interim, what is the smart way to turn a Java FileInputStream
> into a .NET Stream?

To turn a FIleInputStream into a .NET Stream, use Android.Runtime.InputStreamInvoker:

	http://docs.mono-android.net/index.aspx?link=T%3aAndroid.Runtime.InputStreamInvoker

Similarly, to convert a .NET Stream into a Java.IO.InputStream, use Android.Runtime.InputStreamAdapter:

	http://docs.mono-android.net/index.aspx?link=T%3aAndroid.Runtime.InputStreamAdapter
