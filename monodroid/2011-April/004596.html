As Craig Dunn mentioned, in Release builds the Linker is active, and the linker WILL remove "unused" types and members that the linker determines aren't used. Unfortunately, the linker doesn't trace Reflection code, so code such as `type.GetProperty(...)` may result in errors, as the linker may remove the property if it isn't otherwise statically referenced.

The fix is the same: either access the member statically (w/o Reflection), or mark the member with the [Preserve] attribute.
