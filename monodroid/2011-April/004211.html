On Apr 9, 2011, at 7:34 PM, <www at gencode.com> <www at gencode.com> wrote:
> I was following an example, but when I tried it in my project I am getting the following
>  
> (_CompileAndroidPackage target) ->
>   monodroid : error 1: System.InvalidOperationException: The type 'MyApp.CustomList' needs to have a public default constructor.

As Dennis Quebe noted, you didn't provide CustomList, you provided CustomListAdapter.

That said, is MyApp.CustomList in your main assembly or some library assembly? If it's in a library assembly, the linker may be linking its contents (and thus removing the constructor). If this is the case, use the [Preserve] attribute:

	http://mono-android.net/Documentation/Linking#PreserveAttribute
