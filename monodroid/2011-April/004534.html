On Apr 26, 2011, at 10:59 PM, Jeff Alstadt wrote:
> I can still not get the emulator to appear in the selected device.
> The emulator can boot up fine in 2.1 but the monodroid is not able to recognize it.

Can `adb` find it? Information on where the `adb` program is located at:

	http://mono-android.net/Documentation/Guides/Android_Debug_Log#Accessing_from_the_Command_Line

Then, from CMD.EXE, run:

	adb devices

Does it display your emulator? If it does, then there's a Mono for Android bug (and we would like additional details ;-).

If `adb devices` doesn't show your emulator, you need to kick things:

	adb kill-server
	adb start-server

After that, `adb devices` should show your emulator, and then Mono for Android should be able to deploy.
