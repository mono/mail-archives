On Apr 9, 2011, at 5:07 AM, Jesús Bosch Ayguadé wrote:
> My question is: 
> - Can I port an existing flash app to Android using mono droid?

Mono for Android is for using C# (and eventually other managed languages). Flash uses ActionScript (and a completely different runtime environment and class library...). I'm not sure why you would consider Mono for Android for porting a Flash app.

That said, Adobe has released an AIR runtime for Android, so presumably you could port your Flash code to use Adobe AIR and use that for Android development...

> - If so, would the android market accept that kind of app?

Yes, the Android Market will accept Adobe AIR apps (though you may need to do some special packaging; I have no idea what the workflow is). I believe that Adobe AIR apps also require that the user separately install the Adobe AIR runtime, so this does slightly complicate the app deployment story for end users.
