First of all, thank you guys at Mono to deliver this great product for us -
.NET developers specialized for line of bussines mobile apps.
I' ve been reading this mailing list since early begining of the first
monodroid preview.
In a meantime our monodroid guy has managed to port our compact framework
windows mobile app completely to mono for android in a stable beta version,
ready for workload tests and production.
In general, we are very satisfied with overall performance of an aplication
and monodorid platform and tools.

Finally, we easy decide to buy a licensed version of tools and platform for
production - in our case Enterprize edition licence, because our app is
primarily for direct to device, non-marketplace deployment.

On a Novell store I found that the price for Enterprize edition licence for
european developers is significantly higher than $999 per licence - price
officialy claimed on the hompage of mono for android.

For us European based developers the price set on Novell store is 830,00 EUR
per licence, which is more than 999 USD (on xe.com or google: 999 USD = 689
EUR which is far less than 830,00 on Novell).

Is it just me, or there is some reasonable explanation for this?
If 140EUR is shipping price for European based developers?
