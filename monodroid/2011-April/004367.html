On Apr 16, 2011, at 10:21 AM, warren burch wrote:
> Getting a bunch of these in the log *just* before the debugging bridge
> detaches.
> 
> D/SntpClient(   63): request time failed: java.net.SocketException: Address
> family not supported by protocol

Look at the process id (pid), which is the value between the parenthesis (63 in this case). I highly doubt that your app has pid 63.

To find the PID for your process:

	http://mono-android.net/Documentation/Guides/Android_Debug_Log#Interesting_Messages

In short, those are entirely ignorable (coming from system_server, at least on my N1).
