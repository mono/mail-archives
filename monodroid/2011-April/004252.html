The HTC Desire HD has a dodgy linux kernel that likes to kill processes that generate more than 10 signals, and as it happens Mono's sdb uses signals for stepping:

	http://baulig.blogspot.com/2011/04/limiting-page-faults-on-htc-desire-hd.html

You will need to wait for a future release for a workaround. :-(
