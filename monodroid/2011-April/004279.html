Yes, this is normal.  For debugging deployment, we just use the default 
certificate which has a six month expiration.

When you sign your package for real for the marketplace, it walks you 
through creating a key that doesn't expire until at least 2038.

http://mono-android.net/Documentation/Guides/Preparing_Package_for_Android_Marketplace
