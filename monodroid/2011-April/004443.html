HI guys. I got accessing a WCF Service working. It was a few releases back
so I'm not sure wots changed since then, but the trick was accessing the
service on 10.0.2.2 rather than localhost or any other url. It has something
to do with the firewall on the debugger needing to go through that port to
hit anything outside of itself. I think you may also need to host it in IIS.
So if your service us called service1.svc hosted in the virtual directory
AndroidService, you'll access it on
http://10.0.2.2/AndroidService/service1.svc.

Hope that helps.
