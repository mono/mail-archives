On Jun 25, 2013, at 10:35 AM, Paul Johnson <paul at all-the-johnsons.co.uk> wrote:
> The original source has this line in it
> 
> compName = new ComponentName(this, MyAdmin.class);

`MyAdmin.class` is a way to get a java.lang.Class from a class name; it's the moral equivalent of `typeof(MyAdmin)` in C#.

In Xamarin.Android, use Java.Lang.Class.FromType(Type):

	compName = new ComponentName(this, Java.Lang.Class.FromType(typeof(MyAdmin)));
