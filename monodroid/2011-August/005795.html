found the addin with mono 2.6..was using 2.4..and fixed the exception
when selecting the addin-under the options node by copying
Mono.Posix.dll from C:\Program Files\Mono-2.10.2\lib\mono\4.0\...to
AppData\Local\MonoDevelop-2.6\Addins\MonoDevelop.MonoAndroid.2.6.4\...thanks
for your help!!!
