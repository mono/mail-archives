Hi Steve,

Xamarin is honouring downloads and support for all valid Mono for Android
and MonoTouch licenses purchased from Novell. In other words, you don't need
to do anything to transfer and  the license you purchased from Novell is
valid with Xamarin.

As you purchased from Novell, you can download the latest version from here
http://activation.mono-android.net/ - this is a Xamarin controlled site and
is up-to-date with the latest releases.

Xamarin are providing support for your purchase.

I don't work for Xamarin, but as I saw this message from Jonathon Pryor of
Xamarin yesterday, I thought it would be worth saving people some time:

"Your license is still good, and you can go to
http://activation.mono-android.net/ to download updated releases.

Thanks,
 - Jon"
