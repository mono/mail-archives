On Aug 9, 2011, at 7:40 PM, riteshsahu wrote:
> Looks like none (at least most) of the assemblies shipped with Mono for
> Android are not strongly named. It stops us from strongly naming our own
> assemblies as the whole chain needs to be strongly named. 
> Any chance of getting this resolved in the next update?

I doubt it'll happen for the next update, nor am I sure what timeframe it would be in.

Is there any reason you need this? In the current design there is no GAC, and all assemblies are embedded within the .apk (a .zip container) in the 'assemblies' directory. Consequently, I'm not entirely sure what strong naming would actually gain you: for all intents and purposes, every Release Mono for Android application is an "xcopy deply" app, with all dependencies bundled with the app.
