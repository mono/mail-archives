Hello Igor

Can someone point me to the detailed definition of "enterprise deployment"
license usage? I've searched but found no "formal" definition of this.

Terms for deployment are defined in the Software License Agreement that is
presented when you install the software.  I know this wording needs to be
placed more clearly on the site, though.

Enterprise deployments are large deployments to end users that do not go
through a public app store (like Apple App Store, Android Market, or Amazon
Appstore).

On iOS, this refers explicitly to Apple's in-house deployment for members of
their Enterprise Developer program:
http://developer.apple.com/programs/ios/enterprise/

On Android, we define "large" to be > 100. This is meant to mirror the
In-house deployment option Apple offers developers in their Enterprise
Developer Program.


 One of our planned distribution channels is "OEM" license, with application
preinstalled on a hardware. This is for sure not an "enterprise users" -
since these user could in same way buy the app in android market. But this
also is not a pure "market" distribution.

For OEM deployments, you would need an OEM license.  You can contact us to
discuss options: contact at xamarin.com
