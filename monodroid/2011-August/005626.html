You can't have more than one <application/> element within
AndroidManifest.xml, at least I don't believe that it worked when I last
tried it. The docs also imply that there's only one:

	
http://developer.android.com/guide/topics/manifest/manifest-element.html

	"[The <manifest/> element] must contain an <application> element."

Notice it doesn't say "one or more <application/> elements."

In short, don't have more than one 1 <application/> elements, and you should
be fine.
