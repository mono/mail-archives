You can't save these downloading images into the resource folder. The
images in the drawable folder are compiled into the APK and cannot be
modified. See the Android documentation on building an APK:
http://developer.android.com/tools/building/index.html

To load an image from disk, your best best bet is to probably use
BitmapFactory.DecodeFile(string fileName).
