The 1.9 shared runtime is incompatible with the 1.2.0 shared runtime. Thus, to run your apps against 1.9, you need to rebuild them (load the project, Clean, Build, Reinstall).

(This is technically true for all our releases, but frequently you can "get away" with not rebuilding pre-existing installed apps. In this release, lots of fundamental things changed to enable shrinking .apk sizes, so now things break immediately, as you see.)
