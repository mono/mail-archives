On Sep 7, 2011, at 12:04 PM, Miljenko Cvjetko wrote:
> Even google admits it that emulator is slow.

Indeed. They also state a way to "speed up" the emulator: make the screen size as small as possible (e.g. QVGA).

The reason why is that there's no hardware video acceleration, so the entire screen is rendered in software...in (emulated!) ARM. The smaller the screen, the less that needs to be rendered, the faster the UI updates.
