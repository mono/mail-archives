> Does anyone have a sample of this that works.

I don't have a sample, but I think I know the problem you're seeing. You cannot currently use DataContractJsonSerializer with types inheriting (directly or indirectly) from Java.Lang.Object, because DataContractJsonSerializer doesn't invoke the Java.Lang.Object constructor. (Consequently, the Java-side object is never allocated, and things go "weird" quickly.)
