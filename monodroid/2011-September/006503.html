On Sep 28, 2011, at 11:24 AM, sound wrote:
> Why System.Net.HttpUtility is internal class?

Because Mono for Android follows the .NET framework assembly/namespace pattern, not the Silverlight assembly/namespace pattern. Thus, we have a public System.Web.HttpUtility in System.Web.Services.dll (which really should be System.Web.dll, but c'est la vie).

We should probably add a System.Net.HttpUtility for WP7 compatibility.
