On May 8, 2012, at 3:15 PM, Jonathan Pryor wrote:
> Oops; mandroid.exe is a winexe, and thus can't write to the terminal. `mandroid --version` will work on OS X...but that won't help on Windows.

What will work on Windows is redirecting output to a file:

	> mandroid --version
	# no output

	> mandroid --version > out.txt
	> notepad out.txt
	# out.txt contains version information

You learn something new every day...
