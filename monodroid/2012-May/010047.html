It was something that was unsupported until 4.1.

It's not trying to deploy your winforms app to android, it's trying to 
deploy your android app to android, even though you aren't trying to 
debug it.

The VS model allows for multiple projects to be deployed and started 
when you hit F5.  For all VS knows, the android project is a remote 
server than needs to be deployed and running for your winforms app to 
communicate with.

With 4.1, you are able to tell VS that it does not need to deploy your 
android app in order to run your winforms app.

Hope this helps!
Jonathan
