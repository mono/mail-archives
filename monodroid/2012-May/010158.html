Hi,

        I also hit this error when I installed 4.2 alpha and was able
to correct the error by Updating the

C:\Program Files (x86)\MSBuild\Novell\Novell.MonoDroid.Common.targets.

        I just removed the LinkDescriptions attribute from <Target
Name="_LinkAssemblies" ...

        and the error disappeared.

        I believe it is related to some installation problem and hence
re-installation will also update the MSBuild files.

        I also hit the error that System.Drawing.xxx is defined in
Mono.Android and OpenTK-1.0 and had to use the Namespace [extern alias
newVer;] redirect syntax to use both Mono.Android and OpenTK-1.0
