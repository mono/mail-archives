> Why does 4.2alpha require a restart when it installs? None of the previous versions did...

I'm told that it depends on whether or not VS was closed before installation (as the installer requests). If VS is closed, a restart shouldn't be needed; otherwise VS will have some of the files locked, which necessitates replacing them during reboot.
