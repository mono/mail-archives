It generally means the app running on the device crashed for some 
reason, so it breaks the socket the debugger was using to talk to the app.

Generally, there will be a stack trace in the device logcat.  It 
probably won't be pulled automatically into the IDE because the 
connection was lost, so you may need to one of the other ways to view 
logcat:

http://docs.xamarin.com/android/advanced_topics/android_debug_log
