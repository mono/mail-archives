Debug builds have the INTERNET permission automatically added to them so 
the debugger can function.

Either add this to your code:
[assembly: UsesPermission (Android.Manifest.Permission.Internet)]

Or add the INTERNET permission in the Android Manifest tab of the 
project properties.

Hope this helps!
Jonathan
