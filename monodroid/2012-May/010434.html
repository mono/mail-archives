On May 17, 2012, at 4:58 PM, kuma wrote:
> can you help me? what is Java.Lang.Object? 

Java.Lang.Object is the C# binding for Java's java.lang.Object type, and is the base class for all bound types.

It is also the type you should inherit from whenever implementing Android interfaces and the Android.Runtime.IJavaObject interface.
