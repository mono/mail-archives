On 8/26/2010 4:57 PM, MrUnleaded wrote:
> Am I missing something or is there no way to see unhandled exceptions.
> My app is crashing but I am not sure how to debug it other then stepping
> line by line through the code.

In C:\android-sdk-windows\tools, open up a command prompt and type:

adb logcat

This will dump all the logging and errors from your emulator/device.
