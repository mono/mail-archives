In essence you are OK so long as your comments are sympathetic to fact that
Monodroid is very much pre-release software.

Writing a blog in that case, which focuses on the bugs apparent in Monodroid
(as a result of it being pre-release) would be unfair and in no one's best
interests.

Writing about your experience in so far as the features which set it aside
to any other development scenario (which makes MT/MD unique and an amazing
tool) might on the hand be favorable.

Miguel has (OTR I think) mentioned they are happy for us to chat about it,
just don't use any click bate, misguided/uneducated apprehension or
premature criticism - all fair and good IMO
