Some things i've noted along with concerns:

- 28mb runtime installed
- Basic application size is ~4mb before running
- Initial application startup is ridiculously long ( JIT? )
- After first run, app adds 12mb of data ( is this also data from the JIT? )
- Despite application being installed to SD card, the deflated data seems to
occupy internal memory.

I understand that it's going to change and these are early days, but i'd
worry that the initial startup time and app data being installed to internal
memory may potentially harm the product a lot. Even cut down to 6 or 7mb,
live wallpapers and widgets get rated down heavily for being > 2mb.

Sadly that's my biggest gripe about android ( love it otherwise ), storage
space makes me constantly uninstall apps from my nexus one, despite the
install on SD support in FroYo.

Cheers,
David Goemans
