I've found that always leaving the emulator running helps a bit - you
don't always get the penalty of starting up the emulator each time.
That said, it seems that it does take an application a *very* long
time to start up - even for a Java app.

I have noticed that Monodroid doesn't always seem to do anything when
I leave the emulator always running.  By this I mean that I will hit
'F5' to start, and the app will compile with no problems, Monodroid
will tell me it's uninstalling the old app and installing the new app,
and then nothing happens.  The app doesn't start up in the emulator.
Or, maybe it's just taking to long to start up?
