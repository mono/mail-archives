Well, this is just the first preview  ;-)

That said, similar to MonoTouch, you can certainly use one of the many
Android designers to begin exploring UI options.  I like DroidDraw:
http://www.droiddraw.org/ 

Based on the opinions that have been echoed here regarding the true value
of a designer, it seems likely that the 1/2 point we reached with Interface 
Builder in MonoTouch is probably good enough (so perhaps we'll just 
support 3rd party designers).  I agree that Intellisense support for the 
Android's XML format would be a big win, too.

> with that. But if Novell are going after the...VB developer...then the  
> designer is a must. 
We're not going after the VB developer yet.  I personally have a lot of interest
in seeing us do that, and have done a lot of experimenting with VB on MonoTouch
in my spare time, but so far, the demand just hasn't materialized to complete
productization of VB support.  Not sure how much that fact should effect our 
decision on supporting a designer, though...

--Joseph
