In order to get ReSharper to play nicely with MonoDroid projects, open 
your .csproj up in Wordpad or something, and add:

<Reference Include="mscorlib" />

with the rest of your references.

Reload your project and enjoy!

I've added this to the default template in SVN, so this will be 
automatic in new projects created with future releases.
