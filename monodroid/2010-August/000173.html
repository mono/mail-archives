On 8/19/2010 10:01 PM, Sergio Pereira wrote:
Since many of us probably have already been programming for Android, in
Eclipse or otherwise, chances are that our Android SDK isn't located at
the mandatory c:\android-sdk-windows for the preview to work.

So this is just a reminder that, instead of relocating the SDK or
duplicating the whole thing, we can use NTFS features to create symlinks
or junctions:

(Win7)
mklink /D "C:\android-sdk-windows"
"c:\your\current\install\path\android-sdk"

(Win2008 or Vista)
junction "C:\android-sdk-windows" "c:\your\current\install\path\android-sdk"

I've used that in my Win2008x64 and it seems to be working just fine.
