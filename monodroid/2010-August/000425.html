i believe the XML is processed as part of the build right?

if that is the case then this isnt possible

and i'm not 100% you'd want to - the diff between android and wpf would be
that the user might not necessarily have the requisite connection to the
Internet to accomplish this - what happens then? good night Vienna?

as k-9 mentioned - downloading components or skinning the app at runtime is
probably the way to go and we do this sort of stuff in festival star -
though we've learned to eager load on sync rather than mix that with lazy
loading (we were lazilliy loading certain resources to cut the time down
needed for sync but the issue here is the same as above - your app can
download 90% of the data and when it comes to downloading resources lazilly
- the user may not have a connection)

unless this is an ent app in a controlled corp environment - i'd have a
think about this.
