So far, my experience is that copying/debugging with the emulator has a few...hiccups. A few times I have to close both down and just start again. It would appear that I get more joy if I always close the application in the emulator and then hit stop in VS. That way the app isn't running when I try to deploy next time. It hasn't had a hiccup since I've followed that practice.
