On Thu, 2010-08-26 at 10:51 +0100, Matthew Baxter-Reynolds wrote:
> Is this list a forum for things that work in one of the device Mono's
> but not in the other?

Theoretically no, but why not... ;-)

At the outset, I can say that MonoDroid and MonoTouch WILL behave
differently in many ways, but only because of platform-specific
restrictions.  For example, System.Reflection.Emit can't work in
MonoTouch (AOT-everything) but CAN work in MonoDroid.  Ditto generic
methods on interfaces and/or virtual generic methods (fail on MonoTouch,
should work in MonoDroid).

However, I don't see anything in your ToXmlDocument() example which
should cause things to fail in MonoTouch.  Sounds like a bug.
