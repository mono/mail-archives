I think when getting into something new, a Designer is a good thing.
However, once a developer understands how things work, coding everything out
by hand becomes not only faster but much more efficient and controllable.

I do agree with Paulio that Intellisense is something Visual Studio
Developers are not only used to but insist upon these days. If anything it
helps one to learn something new much faster.

For me personally, I enjoy the use of a Designer to visually sketch things
up but often times the XML/HTML/Code output by such a Designers is less than
spectacular so I tend to hand code it to save my sanity :)

However, I do think NOT having a Designer introduces a good opportunity for
someone to create one :-)

-Kevin
