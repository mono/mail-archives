On Thu, 2010-08-19 at 14:41 -0400, Mike Akers wrote:
> Is MonoDroid doing AOT compilation of application code? Or did you
> port the mono vm to android?

The Mono VM was ported to Android's libc/pthreads/etc. libraries.  No
AOT compilation is performed, it's the normal JIT.

This has the added benefit that full LINQ, System.Linq.Expressions,
DynamicMethod, etc., are supported (unlike with MonoTouch).
