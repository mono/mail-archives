I did not have any issues with CodeRush or for that matter, having the
entire DX Universal suite installed [latest].  It worked just fine.  As for
JDK x64/x86, I am running Win 7/64 and only installed the 32-bit version.  I
did have to modify my PATH environment variable, however.. to include
C:\Program..(x86)\Java?\bin directory, and that did the trick.  I restarted
VS.. fired up the Android SDK to launch the VM, and was able to run my
sample application without issue.
