How are you launching the emulator, from a command-line or from the IDE?

Launching from the IDE should provide enough storage space for packages to install, but if you launch the emulator yourself[0] you need to provide the -partition-size parameter:

	emulator -avd ImageName -partition-size 512

If you are launching from the emulator, what's the output of [1]:

	adb shell df
