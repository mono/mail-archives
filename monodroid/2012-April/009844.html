On Apr 19, 2012, at 3:50 PM, percramer wrote:
> At first i would just like to know if the service is already running when the app restarts / becomes active again.

Our favorite oracle [0] states that there's no API to do this, but you can work around it by using in-process shared state [1, 2], e.g. a static variable. Since your Service and Activity will (normally) be in the same process, your Activity will be able to access the same static variables that your Service can.

> In the second place i would like to communicate with the service ofcourse. 

If the static variable holds your Service, you "suddenly" have access to the Service instance, and can do whatever you please. :-)

 - Jon
