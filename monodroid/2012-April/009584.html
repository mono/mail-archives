On Apr 3, 2012, at 2:45 PM, Paul Johnson wrote:
> Thanks - the idea is to be able to write to somewhere with a downloaded file. Is there an alternative to the SDCard that can be used?

The only directory you can rely on is /data/data/Your.Package.Name, e.g. System.Environment.GetFolderPath(System.Environment.SpecialFolder.MyDocuments) (which is /data/data/Your.Package.Name/files).

The problem here is that the /data partition is frequently small (e.g. on my N1 it's ~128MB in size), so you can't download large amounts of data there.
