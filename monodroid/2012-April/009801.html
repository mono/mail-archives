> This generally means you are trying to use an assembly that is not compiled for the MFA profile.
>
> In the .NET Framework 3.5, System.Func is in System.Core.dll  In MFA (and .NET 4.0), System.Func is in mscorlib.dll.
>
> You will need to recompile your assembly using the MFA profile.
>
> Jonathan
