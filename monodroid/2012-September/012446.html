Why do you need to put and explicit "End" button in your app?  for some
sort of cleanup?

User's don't really quit/end/kill an application - Android does it for
them. It's a best practice to let Android kill the app after users have
stopped using it (i.e. they move on to another application):

http://stackoverflow.com/questions/2033914/quitting-an-application-is-that-frowned-upon
.
