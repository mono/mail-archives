I ran in to this, too, but in android. What version are you targeting? If
you can make it to 2.3 (i think) you can use .commit and get a bool if it
saved. Where as .save does it "eventually" and you app might be closing
before it saves (so it partially writes) and you lose a file. Not sure if
it's your issue but that's what fixed the same thing for me (in java)
