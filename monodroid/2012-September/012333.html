Just because it's present doesn't mean that it's bindable, for a variety of reasons (frequently due to tooling limitations...).

What would be help would be to enable diagnostic build output, clean + rebuild your Java Bindings Library project, and provide the diagnostic output. Specifically, you're looking for:

	Tool .*generator.* execution started with arguments:

Following that are frequently warnings about types that are being skipped and why they're being skipped.
