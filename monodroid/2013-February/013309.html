Sounds like you are using the Designer, correct?  In that case I would
suggest that you check out the documentation on the Designer:
http://docs.xamarin.com/Android/Guides/User_Interface/Designer_Overview,
particularily the section on Alternate Layouts (
http://docs.xamarin.com/Android/Guides/User_Interface/Designer_Overview/Part_3_-_Alternative_Layout_Views
).

Another option is to just edit the layouts by hand and not use the designer.

HTH.
