Hi,

Yes, definitely a regression. To workaround this issue while we
(re)fix it, uninstall the app from the emulator, disable Fast
Deployment and deploy the app to the emulator, running it at least
once. After that, you should be able to re-enable Fast Deployment and
deploy as usual, as the directories in question should now have the
proper permissions.

andreia
