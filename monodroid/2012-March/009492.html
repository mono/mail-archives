> I'm doing some testing out and would like to force a view to get focus.  I realize that this is a bad thing in Android, but I thought I would at least give it a try. I have setup the view's .FocusChange property to process the OnFocusChange event and I see the method called twice.  The first is when the view gets focus and then when the view loses focus.
