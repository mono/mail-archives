Currently, you will have to copy the entries into your 
AndroidManifest.xml, and manually remove the LAUNCHER intent.  For the 
next preview, this has been completely reworked so you can use an 
[Activity] attribute to specify what you want to get generated for your 
activities:

http://monodroid.net/Tutorials/Working_with_AndroidManifest.xml

For the next preview, I have also removed the application icon field in 
lieu of specifying icons at the [Activity] level.

Jonathan
