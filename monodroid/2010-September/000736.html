WCF Data Services are not expected to work even under our full WCF
profile (there used to be plan to have someone working on Data Services
stuff, but it haven't happened).

Importing .NET's WSDL either. I have never tried that, but WsdlImporter
would be expected to handle some policy elements in the WSDL specially
to create REST-oriented client endpoint. It is also not done at all (as
it's part of above work).

Classic SOAP-based WCF would work. WebHttpBinding may even work, but no
one AFAIR has examined it so far.
