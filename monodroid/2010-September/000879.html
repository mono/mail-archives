In standard Android the lib seems to prefer a RESTFul Service.  (POST, GET etc). 
We had to use it locally (though Im brand new to MONODROID). 
 
Here is a quick (already working) Blogging Service written in REST. 
http://www.developer.com/net/article.php/10916_3695436_3/Creating-RESTful-Web-Services-with-Windows-Communication-Foundation.htm
