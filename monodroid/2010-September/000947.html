I have to admit that I find it extremely impressive that you chose to go the
route in which enabled to greatest number of devs to gain benefit from this
project rather than the route that most of the early adopters (I run Windows
via VMware Fusion from my Mac, so I'm certainly one who would prefer Mac,
but am happy with Windows just as well) would likely have preferred. Not
that this comes as any real surprise: Pragmatism has always trumped idealism
in the land of Mono. It's just impressive to see it time after time after
time from a group of legendary Unix/Linux-centric devs who continue to
impress with each passing year.
