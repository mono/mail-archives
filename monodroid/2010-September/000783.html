On Mon, 2010-09-13 at 14:53 +0200, Jonathan Dickinson wrote:
> Just remember than any DLR languages won't work (Ruby, Python,
> Javascript) because those need IL emit.

The problem isn't lack of System.Reflection.Emit; that is supported.
The problem is that in order for Android to invoke managed code, there
needs to be a Android Callable Wrapper, and these are only generated at
build time, not runtime.  See:

        http://monodroid.net/Documentation/Limitations
        http://monodroid.net/Documentation/Architecture/Android_Callable_Wrappers
