On Wed, Sep 15, 2010 at 1:46 PM, Jonathan Pobst <monkey at jpobst.com> wrote:

> On 9/15/2010 12:11 PM, Chris Small wrote:
> > The attributes for the AndroidManifest.xml look good (one pascal casing
> > google does like), however wouldn't this be a bit confusing for people
> > who are use to naming their application via the project properties and
> > AssemblyInfo?
>
> The issue is that an "application" isn't what shows up in the Android
> launcher menu.  You have to pick the "activity" that you want to be your
> startup activity that shows up in the launcher menu.
>
> You can in fact have multiple activities that show up in the launcher
> within one application.  So the names for the launcher menu have to come
> from the activity level and not the application level.
>
> We contemplating building a GUI in the project properties that took all
> the activities in your project into account, but it becomes so large and
> complex that we think the [Activity] attribute will be easier.
