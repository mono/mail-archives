>  > I am going to guess that the activity names that you copied over do not
>  > match what MonoDroid wants to call them. For example, let's say you
>  > call your activity:
>  >
>  > <activity android:name="MyActivity" />
>  >
>  > and your activity class is:
>  >
>  > public class Activity1 : Activity
>  >
>  > There is no way for MonoDroid to know that MyActivity refers to the
>  > Activity1 class.
>  >
>  > We highly recommend you do not manually edit your AndroidManifest, and
>  > instead use the [Activity] attribute so it all gets generated properly
>  > for you:
>  >
>  > http://monodroid.net/Tutorials/Working_with_AndroidManifest.xml
