On Wed, 2010-09-08 at 15:37 -0600, Tom Opgenorth wrote:
> Thanks for the info and the heads up that this will change.
> 
> If I may ask to satisfy my curiousity, why get rid of the Java-esque
> I***Listener interfaces?  Is it because EventHandler<> is more C#?

Yeah, this is the eternal debate.  I've argued both sides of it.  :-)

The event handler approach is the .net way, and since we are providing
a .net binding of android, leaving the Listeners around when they aren't
specifically needed introduces ambiguity, a bigger installed footprint,
and a larger maintenance burden.

While having them there might facilitate a more direct porting path from
existing java code, the transformation from listeners to events is just
one of many paradigm shifts a java developer is going to have to make
with monodroid.

Oh, and event handlers are sexier.
