I want to make sure I understand what "first time" means. 

 

If I simply move away and come back to the application I continue where I
was before and still see the click count. I can, in fact, come back after
running apps. But eventually that seems to expire (I don't know the exact
rules) and it needs relaunch the application and things become painfully
slow again (with 12 seconds being an underestimate by far - I'm still
waiting after turning the screen back on ... and eventually it did start.)

they share the same unpacked runtime?
