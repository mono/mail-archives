When you start the emulator, there is a flag that tells it how much 
"memory" to have:

-partition-size 512

If you start your image from the MonoDroid "Select Device" dialog, we 
pass this flag so you get plenty of memory.  If you are starting your 
image yourself, it gets started with ~44 MB.
