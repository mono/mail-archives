As a guess, you need to include com.google.android.maps.jar in your
project.  You can presumably do this by providing -I=path/to/maps.jar in
your monodroid.exe invocation.

I have no idea how to do that via the VS GUI.  Pobst?

Additionally, at present monodroid doesn't include android.jar if any -I
is provided.  (This is either a feature or a bug, depending on whether
or not you want to provide your own android.jar for some reason...)

This means you'd need to do:

        monodroid.exe -I=path\to\android.jar -I=path\to\maps.jar ...
