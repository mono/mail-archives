I do understand your position, but I can also see things from the side of
the fence that many of us are sitting on in which it doesn't become
immediately obvious until you are forced to go digging into the first few
emails tagged as [Monodroid] to discover the username and password are
embedded in what appears to be nothing more than a Domo-generated "you've
been subscribed to yet another mailing list" form letter.  I'm not
complaining so much as I am suggesting that I can both understand your
position while at the same time find the idea of spending a few hours
creating a preconfigured VM to host the OpenID authentication piece a much
preferred scenario that can then be used in place of that same simple layer
thats currently in place.  Everyone wins :-)
