Very weird. :-)

It looks like javac is crashing while reading one of the .jar files
we're providing to 'javac -classpath'.  This may suggest an installation
error of some form; are your android.jar and mono.android.jar correct? A
sane way to check this would be to run:

        jar tf path\to\android.jar

for both android.jar and mono.android.jar.  If that works without
failure...  I'm out of ideas.

What might also be helpful is providing the javac command line (should
be in your MSBuild output prior to the monodroid.exe stack trace you
printed) and the obj/Debug/android/src directory tree.

Thanks,
 - Jon
