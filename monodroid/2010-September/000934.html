I vote that in the next phase of either this or any other private beta that
a simple OpenID server is setup and used requiring that the URI for that
OpenID be provided as part of the sign up process. This removes a layer of
responsiblity, keeps the private layer in place using nothing more than
white list of openid URIs, and on our end allows us to manage the pieces
that matter most: The authentication related to the white listed OpenID URI.

Of course, could it get any easier to implement than:
http://www.dotnetopenauth.net/ ?
