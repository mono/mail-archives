make a call to Environment.getExternalStorageDirectory()<http://developer.android.com/intl/fr/reference/android/os/Environment.html#getExternalStorageDirectory%28%29>
to
get the directory:

File sdDir = Environment.getExternalStorageDirectory();


give your app the correct permission to write to the SD Card by adding the
line below to your Manifest:

<uses-permission android:name="android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE" />
