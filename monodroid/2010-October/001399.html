Have you guys considered publishing nightly builds?  I feel like it would
help a lot with getting more testing in.  I know for me at least my
MonoDroid development cycle has been something like this for the last month
or so:

0) New preview comes out
1) Download latest preview
2) Continue to attempt to port a Silverlight project I have to MonoDroid
3) Encounter a bug(s) that prevents me from continuing
4) Report it
5) Wait a couple weeks for the next preview.

Nightly builds could turn step 5 into a couple days in a lot of cases.
