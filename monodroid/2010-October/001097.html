> On Fri, 2010-10-01 at 13:54 +0400, Alexander Bykin wrote:
> > Can anyone tell me:
> > - can i create multilanguage applications, like it possible at
> > silverlight ?
>
> Maybe.  Preview 4 does not contain the DLR, so any dynamic languages are
> out.  Consequently, if you have a language which can be made to depend
> only on the assemblies shipped with MonoDroid [0], that language should
> work.
>
> Thus, it *should* be possible (in theory [1]) to get Visual Basic.NET
> working, as long as you remove all dependencies on
> Microsoft.VisualBasic.dll.
>
> (Nemerle is slightly more problematic, as iirc they can't easily make
> their compiler target profile assemblies that differ from the profile
> the compiler was built against.  F# could plausibly work...if you can
> (again) remove all dependencies on the FSharp*.dll assemblies OR
> recompile those assemblies and bundle them with your app [1].)
>
> In short, Try It And See.  If the language can target Silverlight,
> there's a high likelihood that it'll work on MonoDroid (though
> Silverlight includes Microsoft.VisualBasic.dll while MonoDroid doesn't,
> so see above advice on VB support).
>
>  - Jon
>
> [0] http://monodroid.net/Documentation/Assemblies
>
> [1] Untested by me, but should be possible in theory.  Of course, in
> theory reality and theory are the same; in practice, they're not.
>
