On Sat, 2010-10-16 at 14:49 -0400, Matthew Groves wrote:
> That sounds like the -best- times I've gotten, but I've gotten
> significantly longer times as well (5-10 minutes or more).  What might
> be causing such delays?

The only thing I can think of is that the adb server has decided to go
on vacation.  As suggested in other threads, if things are taking awhile
(defined as "several minutes"), hit a shell and run `adb kill-server`
and `adb start-server`, then try again.
