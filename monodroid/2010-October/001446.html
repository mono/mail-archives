You can actually do it with or without the Mono runtime.  In the 
"without" scenario, all that stuff is put into your application .apk, so 
it gets much larger.

Currently, we advocate using the shared runtime, as it makes your 
change/deploy/test cycle much faster than having to redeploy everything 
with each change.

Jonathan
