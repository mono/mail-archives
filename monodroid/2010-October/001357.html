On Wed, 2010-10-13 at 21:37 -0500, Keagan Williams wrote:
> I was wondering how to you used logcat with monodroid :o 

Preview 5 adds a VS Window:

        http://monodroid.net/Releases/Previews/Preview_5#Logcat_Viewer

Otherwise, you need to go into your Android SDK installation directory
and run `tools\adb logcat`.

> From what I could catch this is what LogCat states:
>
> W/dalvikvm(30765): threadid=1: thread exiting with uncaught exception (group=0x4001aad8)
> E/AndroidRuntime(30765): Uncaught handler: thread main exiting due to uncaught exception
> E/AndroidRuntime(30765): java.lang.UnsatisfiedLinkError: n_onCreate
> E/AndroidRuntime(30765):        at happy_Birthday.Activity1.n_onCreate(Native Method)
> E/AndroidRuntime(30765):        at happy_Birthday.Activity1.onCreate(Activity1.java:23)

That's...bad.  Very bad.  It means that the runtime wasn't initialized,
and thus your assembly was loaded and "hooked up to" the native Java
methods declared in the proxy.

I'd suggest looking earlier in the log to see why initialization failed.
Alternatively, there's something wrong with your Activity proxy
-- Activity.onCreate() has mono initialization code "injected" into it
before delegating to the managed code.
