A theoretical workaround would be to use the MonoDroidExtraArgs option
within your .csproj:

        <PropertyGroup>
                <MonoDroidExtraArgs>-A=raw\resources
                \dir</MonoDroidExtraArgs>
        </PropertyGroup>

I think this has been filed as:

https://bugzilla.novell.com/show_bug.cgi?id=640619
