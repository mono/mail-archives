I've never done this sort of thing,  but I would try JavaCast<>() here

        if (JavaCast<Java.Lang.Type>(!GetType ().Equals(typeof
(MediaInfo))))

and if that doesn't work exactly, break your line up into separate
statements and try different combinations of JavaCast on the intermediate
values.
