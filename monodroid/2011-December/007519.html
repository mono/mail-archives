Andreia,

Thanks for the clarification. I rechecked it and you are correct. You do get
an OnLoad event after the pause, however you do not get an onResume event
and the graphics context is null. For some reason this only occurs on
rotation and sleep, if the activity is paused because another activity is
becoming active then the context is recreated successfully upon resuming the
activity hosting the AndroidGameView.
