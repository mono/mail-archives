I get this happening from time to time but I'd wager it's more of a VS2010
issue than anything.  Why do I say that?

Because I see it when trying to debug some WCF services that I attach to on
a plain-jane VS2010 install (i.e. no M4A). The only relief I seem to get is
(in this order): restarting VS2010, then starting and stopping my WCF
service if still sluggish, then rebooting. I suspect that it's partially
related, in my case, to the fact that this client makes us run VS2010 on
Windows XP.  VS2010 just seems to play nicer with Win7 IMHO.
