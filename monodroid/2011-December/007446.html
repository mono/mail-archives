Hello,

I have nothing to share any alternatives approach for market licensing 
with you, so let me talk only about LVL-based solution.
We would also like to hear if there are other stories :)

We receive a couple of requests to support LVL and in-app billing, and 
we are working on them. There are couple of things we need to discuss 
and things to be done in Android API binding level (such as decent 
Parcelable support) and the actual libraries.
I hope to get some prototype working in the near future, but it might 
not be very soon. Hopefully we can introduce them in some releases in 
early next year, though not in the first of them.

Hope this helps.
