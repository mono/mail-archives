On Dec 14, 2011, at 11:37 AM, Tom Opgenorth wrote:
> Just curious, with the the fact that devices are getting more and more memory, how many people still see the APK size as an issue going forward?

You need to care somewhat, as there is a maximum .apk size of 50MB for deployment via the Android Marketplace:

	http://android-developers.blogspot.com/2010/12/android-market-client-update.html

Many devices also have small internal storage sizes (e.g. my N1 only has 196MB of total space available on /data), and downloads via 3G may have lower limits (e.g. on iOS apps can be of any size, but must be < 20MB to download via 3G).
