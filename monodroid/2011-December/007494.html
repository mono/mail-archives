Changes needed to get the samples working with the new release were done on the release-2-0 branch, which was just merged to master ~10 minutes ago. Please update your repo and see if that fixes it. :-)

> Also there has been some changes in the ItemizedOverlay now having the
> CreateItem method registered, but it needs to return a
> Java.Lang.Object, does that mean that my custom OverlayItem has to
> inherit from Java.Lang.Object? I thought OverlayItem was already a
> Java.Lang.Object?

OverlayItem inherits Java.Lang.Object, so your class inheriting from OverlayItem will also inherit Java.Lang.Object, just not directly.

Subclassing OverlayItem is fine (and required, iirc).
