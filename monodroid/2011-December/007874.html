Try using context.StartActivity() instead of
context.ApplicationContext.StartActivity().

Another important question is this: what platform are you running on?
Honeycomb changed things so that apps won't get the BOOT_COMPLETED
notification unless the user has launched the app at least once, so if
you just install your app then reboot the device, you won't get the
notification.
