Running a company do have expenses, besides the running expenses like renting a office etc you need to pay staff and developers do have big salaries because of our advance careers. If you also go do research u can see how expensive dev tools r in general.. Unity 3D is $1500 for 1 user license, $399 per plugin for iOS/Android, Delphi tools are the same,  don't let me mention Visual Studio.Net tools or other software like MS Office 2011. 

Am sure that the MonoDroid/MonoTouch pricing model is justified and if somebody post in this group complaining about the pricing then go use the FREE tools like JAVA that does exist. 

Am sure this pricing model was decided after proper market research, budgeting, cater for company and dev tool growth etc and not just decided in a second to screw us.

The debate about money have been in this group from the start bla bla bla and I guest those that complain will only be happy if these tools are made FREE like the Mono compiler/MonoDevelop...

In the end its your choice on what product u wanna use, nobody is holding a gun to ur head... But don't hold one and expect everything to be FREE since u will not do work for FREE so don't expect somebody else to.
