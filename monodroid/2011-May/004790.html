We announced the two options for the discount programs on the 
newsfeed on the home of both sites, but to clarify:

If you already owned MonoTouch prior to the the Mono for Android 
launch, your MT activation code will work as a coupon code for 50%
off of Mono for Android if you purchase the same license type.  

If you did not own MonoTouch when Mono for Android launched, 
you can purchase the two together by adding MonoTouch to your 
cart from:
http://monotouch.net/Store

Adding MonoTouch to your cart adds Mono for Android to your 
cart for 50% off automatically.  

If you already purchased Mono for Android at full price, or you 
purchased MonoTouch after the launch of Mono for Android, 
you'll probably need to return your purchase to shop.novell 
and re-order using one of the options outlined above.  The
offer expires May 31st, and shop.novell has a 30 day refund
policy.

Hope that helps
