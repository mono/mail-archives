Man, I am surprised.  I expected to see a lot more discussion about this
today.  Maybe it is just me but I am personally freaking out about it all
since I read the slashdot story last night.  Sure mono and this project may
stay around but will they stay up to date? supported as well as they have
been?  get updated with the 3.0 objects I am going to so badly need.  As a
tiny company I am worried that the last 4-5 weeks and 5-6k lines of code to
R&D a proof of concept to pitch to a client of ours next week may end up
being a dead end.  I've got a really long week ahead of me and find my self
staring at the screen this morning wondering if it will all be a waste
soon.  I started down the native code route and decided to switch to mono to
help ipad/android share more code as I am sure most of you enjoy.  Guess
this linux geek, mono lover, will just have to cross his fingers and hope
for the best.  Too late to turn back now....

Jon
