On May 13, 2011, at 2:08 PM, Jose M. Rivera Ortiz wrote:
> How can I get the package name so I can use it when creating connection to
> sqlite db like "Data Source=/data/data/" + GetPackageName() +"/databases/" +
> DB_NAME;

How do you know it's "databases"? Are you creating it, or using  something else?

You could use Path.Combine(Context.FilesDir.FullPath, "../databases") [0] or you could use Context.GetDatabasePath(DB_NAME) [1].
