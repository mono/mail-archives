Hi Neal

Not sure how you are sending your email but if you are using an Intent 
to send it via an email app here is how to do it:

Intent sendIntent = new Intent (Intent.ActionSend);
sendIntent.SetType ("application/zip");
sendIntent.PutExtra (Intent.ExtraStream, Android.Net.Uri.Parse 
("file://pathtofile.zip"));
StartActivity (Intent.CreateChooser (sendIntent, "Send Email"));

In the SetType you need to set the mimetype of your attachment (mine 
being a Zip file).
In the putextra I have used an Android Uri.
