Wally,

Sorry to hear that your efforts have been disrupted. I can understand that
you would not want to release the book into such uncertainty and that your
audience is not going to be clamouring like it was before.

I think you CAN guess when it would be sensible to return though. Miguel has
stated that he expects new products to be in beta in 3-4 months with .NET
for Android trailing .NET for iOS by about a month. As an author, I am
pretty sure that Xamarin will be willing to give you pretty solid
information on the timeline as those dates grow closer.

So, I guess it depends on where in the life cycle of the new product you
want to launch a book. Do you want to be there when the product launches? Do
you want to gauge it's popularity first? Do you want to wait until it
matures a bit?

It is a lot harder for me to guess when you will release your book than it
is for me to guess when .NET for Android will be available.

I admit though that these are just guesses. There are a lot of variables
that could impact things between now and then.
