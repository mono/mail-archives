> We discovered (Yesterday evening, I believe) that MonoDevelop improperly attempts to register Mono for Android.
>
> Oops.
>
> So, the workaround is to open CMD.EXE, navigate to whichever directory contains mandroid.exe (e.g. `C:\Program Files (x86)\MSBuild\Novell`), then run:
>
> 	mandroid.exe --activate --name "NAME" --company "COMPANY" --email "EMAIL" --phone "PHONE" --code "ACTIVATION CODE"
>
> Replace the values as appropriate. :-)
>
> To see if this worked, you can run:
>
> 	mandroid.exe --activated&&  echo "activated"
>
