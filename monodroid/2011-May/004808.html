What if you make some changes in your app and make sure to rebuild it 
for debugging?

The debug log says your deployment target somehow misses platform API-9 
support package, which might happen after certain sequence of build, 
debug and runs without debugging. Making sure to have it installed would 
be the solution.

I'm not sure if my way works, just a possibility; I experienced 
something similar.
