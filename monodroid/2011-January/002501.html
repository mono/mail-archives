_usable_.

By default, stdout and stderr are dropped in the bit bucket:

	http://developer.android.com/guide/developing/tools/adb.html#stdout

In theory, stdout will be shown within the MonoDevelop Application Output
window, but I haven't had a lot of success testing this.  I have tried this
with Visual Studio, and it worked _then_ (but I haven't retested recently),
so...

I'd suggest just avoiding Console.WriteLine() if at all possible.
