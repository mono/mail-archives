> Are there any multiplatform options for Mono?

You can do things manually, like building and linking Cairo, but there
is no cross platform solution at this point.

System.Drawing is something that we have considered, but it has not
been a top priority, as behind the simple facade that System.Drawing
exposes there is a world of dependencies that are not easy to port.
