Personally, I believe that the more the framework costs, the more I'll
expect from it.  The differences between Java and C# (for me anyway)
are fairly negligible, but the other bits surrounding the development
process (IDE differences, debugging differences, etc.) is where I
would hope to gain some time.  I wouldn't have a problem paying for
something at whatever price point the developers choose, but like I
said, the more expensive it is, the more I would expect it to do.

$10-$50 per developer license isn't bad at all, and I could see a fee
well above that if it provided benefits equal to the cost.
