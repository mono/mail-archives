We did not push MonoDevelop 2.4.2 to the updater yet, because we usually wait a few days after a new release to see whether there are any unexpected issues "in the wild" before we make the updater prompt all users to upgrade.

The MonoDroid Mac P11 upgrade instructions specifically ask users to download 2.4.2, since most of the fixes in 2.4.2 are for MonoDroid support. The update will be pushed to all users soon.
