To me the biggest contributing factor is how easy it is to develop and test before I've made any monetary commitment. If I can write an Android app and test it on the emulator for anything less than 50 dollars it would be enough for me to try it out. If I feel like my project is successful, even if I only want to publish it as a free or open source app, I would be willing to pay a ~500 dollar license. I just don't want to pay it before I can adequately gauge how my first product and the framework are, and I feel that letting you test on the emulator and a single test device are important in making that call.
