Games.  Whilst you do get books specifically covering game development on mobile platforms, given that this is the first MonoDroid book it would be really useful to have at least one chapter that had a decent game example.  Doesn't have to be complicated but should be something more than the standard 'Rotating cube' that most other books seem to offer. Would be good to show how to integrate an existing c# library that offers something like physics.
Given that the vast majority of apps that actually make money on mobile devices are games, its fair to say that this is the area that a lot of developers are interested in and I think it would generate a lot of interest in your book.
