Yes, that is normal.

If you aren't using the property getters anywhere in your app then they
will only be used by the serializer and the linker doesn't know that so
it removed the property getters. 

Or the inverse can be true and you might use the property getters in
your app, but never the property setters because they are used by the
serializer on deserialization. Then the linker will remove the setters.

Its just like MonoTouch in this regard.
