I was facing the same problem when I offered to host a webforum for a
community in Denmark for webshops. What I did was that I installed vBulletin
and changed some code in it to sent email on all submits so all will recieve
the email with a link. Then its easy for them to click on the link and
autologin and write the answer.

I prefer webforum for email because I recieve so many mails every day (and
its not spam).
