I second (third? fourth? nineteenth?) the Google Groups suggestion.  Mailing
lists are convenient for timeliness but nearly useless for search and
reference purposes as compared to a well-moderated forum, and they clutter
the heck out of one's inbox with irrelevant and unnecessary topics.

Google Groups brings the best of both worlds -- push notification of new
replies to topics you're interested in, and an easily searchable archive of
topics, messages, &cet.

My $0.02, MSRP.
