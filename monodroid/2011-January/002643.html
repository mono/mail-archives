Worked fine for me on Windows 7 x64 using the Kies Mini linked from the t-mo forum post.

Make sure you activate Kies mode when the phone prompts you to choose USB mode.

Also, the adb driver for Vibrant seems buggy, sometimes it only shows "offline" after connecting the device to a USB port. Unplugging it and plugging it back in worked.
