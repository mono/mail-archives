Maybe separate licences, with separate conditions of releasing?
Like, a Free version what will force you to make the app free (though
donation, ads, are allowed) for 10-25$, a Production version for 50-200$
with any licence for your app, and an OpenSource version what is free, but
enforces you to publish the source code aswell (ads, donation, still
allowed).

As a C# developer, I would really like to see this on every device, but
making it too expensive for non-US developers, developers who do not earn
too much from their job, is just a way to loose customers (even if they
don't pay too much). I myself, if I could, would pay approx 25-50$ max for
this, or if I had more money, even more. But, I can not afford it. And, by
the way, all my apps are free, and mostly open source (except special
libraries which I do not wish to commit into public).
