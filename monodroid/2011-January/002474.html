MonoDroid will install fine on the Galaxy S I9000 (and its variants like the
Captivate, Vibrant, etc.), however there is a bug with its kernel in Android
2.1 which I believe can cause MonoDroid apps to crash unexpectedly.  This is
fixed in 2.2 and above.  You might get away with doing some work on 2.1, but
you will probably encounter random crashes.  I think that's the issue.
