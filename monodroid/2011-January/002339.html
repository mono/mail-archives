Jay Wren recommended a great book to me on this topic.  If you're a software
developer and have anything to do with pricing discussions, it is really a 
must-read:
http://dontjustrollthedice.com 

It's a free pdf and very short.  I read it last year in the time it took to watch a 
another argument over MonoTouch pricing play out in IRC.
