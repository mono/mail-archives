You may be able to get more granular reads by reading the process's
stdout/stderr streams directly from threads. Beware that the threading
and cancellation support is easy to mess up. Here's an example that
works, though it's more complicated than it needs to be because it
includes workarounds for long-ago-fixed Mono bugs:
