I rebuilt it with MonoDevelop but had to change the cocoasharp.csproj  
file:

- Copied cocoa-sharp.snk into /src/Cocoa folder.
- Changed RootNamespace and AssemblyName from "cocoasharp" to "cocoa- 
sharp".
- Took out  <Compile Include="Interop\Mach.cs" /> because it would  
not compile with it per this bug reported in 2007:

     http://lists.ximian.com/pipermail/mono-osx/2007-July.txt
