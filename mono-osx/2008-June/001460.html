Again, MacPorts is not necessary to compile Mono. I am happily  
building Mono SVN on 10.5 ppc. All you need is the pkg-config tool and  
some libraries, e.g., from your current official Mono.framework  
installation. Robert Jordan has supplied instructions how to do this  
years ago, they still apply - search lists.ximian.com for the details  
(it mostly involves ACLOCAL_FLAGS and PKG_CONFIG_PATH) and please stop  
complaining about MacPorts. There have been a number of reports  
already that the ensemble of MacPorts and official Mono brings up all  
kinds of issues, it is not really recommendable.

If you choose to use MacPorts this is not the right place to complain.  
Otherwise, please provide details of what is not working with  
compilation and we may be able to help.
