Hi.

I had the same problem, and I solved it by deleting the files
/usr/lib/libglib*
/usr/lib/libgobject*

I guess that I had these files because I at one time had tried to build
those libraries to get an earlier version of MonoDevelop to work.

Good luck

/Pete
