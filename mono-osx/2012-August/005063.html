I managed to find bmac.exe (thank you Raymond). I had to build it myself from
source.
Here's how..
1) Download https://github.com/mono/monomac and
https://github.com/mono/maccore
2) Copy them into your monodevelop projects  and rename the two folders
maccore and monomac
3) Start terminal and set the directory to monomac
4) type "make" to build everything.

You should now find a bmac.exe in the folder monomac/src

Getting it to work is another problem.
