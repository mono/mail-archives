Matteot,

  You have a few problems here:

#1 You cannot mix and match gdi+ versions with mono versions.
#2 You cannot use DYLD_LIBRARY_PATH because of symbol conflicts, you
must use DYLD_FALLBACK_LIBRARY_PATH.  Note its completely unsupported
due to #1 tho

-g
