Allan has helped me with this problem before (one of the advantages  
of having lived in the same undergraduate dorm as him).

Basically, the mono runtime starts in the 1.0 profile unless  
explicitly given a 2.0 assembly.  If you call

[DBMonoEnvironment defaultEnvironmentWithName:"/Library/Frameworks/ 
Mono.framework/Versions/Current/lib/mono/2.0/mscorlib.dll"]

then everything should work fine.


Patrick
