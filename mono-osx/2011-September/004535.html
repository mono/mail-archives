Having upgraded Mono from 2.10.2 to 2.10.5 on Mac OS X 10.5.8, I was not able
to open and use Portable KeePass 2.15 or 2.16. I decided to downgrade to
Mono 2.10.4, and to 2.10.3, without success. Before each downgrade, I made
an uninstallation with the script recommended at this address : 
http://mono-project.com/Mono:OSX#Uninstalling_Mono_on_Mac_OS_X
http://mono-project.com/Mono:OSX#Uninstalling_Mono_on_Mac_OS_X 

I finally decided to go back to Mono 2.10.2 as before and KeePass started to
work again !
