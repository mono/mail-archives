My first question would be, does it have to be a Gtk application?  You might
want to look at:
http://www.mono-project.com/MonoMac

https://github.com/mono/monomac/tree/master/samples/WhereIsMyMac
https://github.com/mono/monomac/tree/master/samples/SkinnableApp

I know... not exactly what you asked but unless you absolutely have to go
Gtk MonoMac is probably a better solution.
