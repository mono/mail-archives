I am NOT working on anything i can talk about yet, but i can tell you that i
am developing a complete .NET framework, although primarily targeted at the
windows market, on .MAC with MONO 1.1.9. Debugging is a pain etc, there are
some little quirks, but overall i rather develop this on a Mac than on the
PC :)

I had rather disappointing results using UI (happily my framework does not
need that), so if you want to develop cocoa based apps, you might need
additional feedback (and i would love to hear that as well) from people on
the list.
