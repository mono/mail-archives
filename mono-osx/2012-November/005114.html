I am providing NAnt installation packages (
http://www.monobjc.net/index.php?page=nant-archives). I have quickly tested

I have hard time while building NAnt under Mono 3.0.1; it has required some
heavy changes in NAnt configuration. It also seems that some 2.0 binaries
are missing from Mono 3.0.1 (like "lib/mono/2.0/gmcs.exe") that prevents
