When you navigate to http://www.kepeskronika.net/en.htm then click on the
moonlight area and then select browsing you will be presented with many pages
of a medieval codex. You can select any page to magnify it and hear it being
read translated to english. This browsing function works with microsoft
silverlight and worked in early 2.99 versions of Moonlight, however since a
certain 2.99 version (I can't remember which one) Moonlight lost its abilty to
browse this page and after upgrading to 3.99 it's still incapable of doing so.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Navigate to http://www.kepeskronika.net/en.htm
2. Click onto the moonlight area of the page
3. Select browsing
Actual Results:  
You will be presented with an empty "browsing windows"

Expected Results:  
Ypou shold be able to browse amongst codex pages
