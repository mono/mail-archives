Now you're definitively getting closer, but the signal gdb stopped on is a
normal signal, not the bad one.

So, before executing "run", execute this:

handle SIGPWR nostop noprint
handle SIG35 nostop noprint
handle SIG33 nostop noprint
handle SIGXCPU nostop noprint
handle SIGCONT nostop noprint
handle SIGPIPE nostop noprint

and then gdb will hopefully only stop at the bad signal.
