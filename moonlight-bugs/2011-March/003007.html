--- Comment #30 from Rolf Bjarne Kvinge <rkvinge at novell.com> 2011-03-15 02:07:20 CET ---
Thanks for the help, I've now tracked down the problem: your cpu is old and
doesn't support the sse2 instruction set.

Unfortunately the codecs currently do require sse2 support (this wasn't
intentional, but fixing it involves a considerable amount of work) - and given
that performance on such old cpus will be *very* bad anyway, we'll likely not
prioritize fixing this bug.

I will however disable the codecs if we detect that sse2 support isn't
available, instead of crashing the browser.
