Note that the repro steps below are after I have turned off Hardware
Acceleration in the Advanced Tab of the Moonlight Settings. Failure to do so
causes the app to be unresponsive.

1) Load the example application from
http://samples.msdn.microsoft.com/Silverlight/SampleBrowser/ClientBin/RichNotepadSnippets.html.
2) When the app has loaded put the cursor after the last period ("euismod."),
then hit the backspace key.

The period is deleted as expected but the cursor moves to the wrong location
(between the m and the o of "euismod" instead of being after the d). Also, the
cursor is seen at another location (probably the beginning of the Run) before
taking its final position.
