Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Login to vod.com
2. set silverlight preference at http://home.vod.com/silverlight.php (causes
moonlight to downloads codecs)
3. visit the player page -
http://www.vod.com/video/36080/The-Childhood-Years-Wash-Behind-Your-Ears-Disc-2/?offer=1342

Actual Results:  
Firefox crashes.

Expected Results:  
Codecs dialog should be clickable and the video should begin to play (as it
does in Silverlight). 

Here is a link to a video that will demonstrate the problem.  A free account is
required (email and password only) to play the video (20 minutes are included
free with the offer code given in the link).

http://www.vod.com/video/36080/The-Childhood-Years-Wash-Behind-Your-Ears-Disc-2/?video_id=36080&video_page_style=embedded&ct=5935&offer=1342

A fair bit of warning, vod.com is related to Hotmovies.com (which has adult
content, not work safe).  The link above is work safe, assuming you don't stray
too far away from the selected video.  An account on hotmovies.com can be used
on vod.com, and vice-versa.  I can privately provide a limitted account for
testing purposes if required.
