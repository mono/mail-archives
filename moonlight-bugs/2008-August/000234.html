The browser bridges are loaded as .so (without and versioning) but because the
bridges are versioned (.0.0.0) the .so is a symlink.  This creates a problem
for packaging as we have to package all of them or move them around to fix. 
SuSE policies also complain about having a symlink .so, which it considers a
devel package, in a non-devel package.
