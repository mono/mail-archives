PROBLEM STATEMENT:
when you set LDFLAGS="-Wl,--as-needed -Wl,--no-undefined" before you configure
the program,
then make will be failed

REPRO:
1. download and untar the mono-1.0b1
2. cd mono-1.0b1
3. export LDFLAGS="-Wl,--as-needed -Wl,--no-undefined"
4. ./configure
5. make
