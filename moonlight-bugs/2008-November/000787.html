I suspect the decoder has issues with fast motion video at 720p. With the
Alexander_Trailer_720p.wmv [1] video from the Microsoft HD Content Showcase
[2], playback stops for 0.5 to 1 second every 3-4 seconds. 

It feels like a decoding/buffering issue and not a rendering issue, especially
considering the massive performance improvements with rendering and the latest
nvidia drivers.

http://download.microsoft.com/download/4/3/d/43dc9661-f69b-4735-9797-b3c98199085e/Alexander_Trailer_720p.exe
