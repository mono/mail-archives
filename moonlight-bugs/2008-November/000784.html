http://www.microsoft.com/silverlight/halo3.aspx

This site basically does the following:

someImageElement.Source = "some_image.png";
canvas.children.add (elements);

when you page thru the pages (left and right arrows in the top) of _already
downloaded_ pages.  Because of how we async process the .Source call and create
a downloader, this causes some flickering for us.

Steps to replicate:

1. Click Right to navigate to Game Controls
2. Click Left to navigate back to main

You shouldn't see the game controls background flicker under the Main Menu.
