Another bug exposed by http://grb.sonoma.edu:81/sl/TestTestPage.html

The TextBox in that case has TextWrapping=Wrap, but we were not wrapping
because ScrollContentPresenter's CanHorizontallyScroll was true. With Alan's
help, we discovered that when TextWrapping is set to Wrap, TextBox disables its
content's hori scroll bars.
