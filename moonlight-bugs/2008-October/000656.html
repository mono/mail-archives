Set limits to video width/height

There's no validation of the Width and Height of a video stream. This can
produce HUGE amount of memory being allocated (and potentially crash) and
potential integer overflow (more crash) later.

The patch fix this by setting an upper limit of 2048 (for both) but I'm not
sure it's the best place (or the best size) for the check.
