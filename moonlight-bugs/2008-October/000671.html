So it looks like this is a weird browser-specific issue.

IE doesn't show the tooltips (and mouseleave events aren't generated) when you
mouse over the textboxes.

silverlight+firefox shows the tooltips (and mouseleave events aren't generated)
when you mouse over the textboxes.

moonlight doesn't show the tooltips (and mouseleave events aren't generated)
when you mouse over the textboxes.

going to close as invalid since silverlight's behavior isn't even consistent
across the browser.  I'd say we're as close to IE's behavior as we can be -
there's nothing we can do to not get the leave event when the pointer gets over
the textboxes.
