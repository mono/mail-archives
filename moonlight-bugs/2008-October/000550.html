ITV Catchup (www.itv.com/catchup) is a high profile site from the UK's second
biggest broadcaster, allowing streaming Silverlight-based access to shows after
they air, similar to BBC's Flash-based iPlayer.

The site uses a nasty mix of Flash and Silverlight, though the actual player
itself is Silverlight-based (confirmed on Windows).

Please see http://retro.apebox.org/moon/moon-itvcatchup.ogg to see how the site
currently behaves. Obviously, there should be some video playing at the final
section.

The Moonlight plugin is definitely aware of at least part of the streaming
video (Right Click -> Sources includes an ashx file pointing to a WMV file over
HTTP), but the XAML itself seems not to include any MediaElement.

I may be able to provide access to a UK-based proxy, to defeat the
location-based lockout, though I'd suggest that people ask Novell UK to provide
remote access to a machine which can be used for SSH-based SOCKS5 proxying
(with ssh -D) or a proper Squid proxy.
