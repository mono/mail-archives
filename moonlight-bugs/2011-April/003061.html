I have developed an app in Silverlight 4.0. It works properly on Windows. I
have installed Moonlight 4.0 on Ubuntu. Browsers used are Mozilla 3.6.13 and
Chrome.

Problem: When I open the app in browser, the screen remains blank until I visit
some other tab or some other window. After visiting some other windows or tab,
the data is displayed properly. The screen uses a simple data grid to display a
list of records.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Open the app in either Mozilla 3.6.13 or Chrome
2. Nothing gets displayed
3. Navigate to some other screen or tab. Come back to the original tab in
browser. Data is displayed


Expected Results:  
Data should have been displayed on load of the page
