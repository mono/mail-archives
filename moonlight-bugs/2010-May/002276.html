Description of Problem:
I tried to look at some maps at Västtrafik, public transport company for
western Sweden area.  It encouraged installation of Silverlight, which in my
case means Moonlight.  But after the installation, my browser started to crash.

Steps to reproduce the problem:
1. Go to http://www.vasttrafik.se/Startsida/linjenats--hallplatskartor/
2. Under "Karta", select "Linjekarta regiontrafiken runt Göteborg"
3. Click at least once on the map
4. Move the mouse outside the map to the right, and use the scroll wheel

Actual Results:
Firefox crashes

Expected Results:
The whole window (not just the map) should scroll

How often does this happen? 
ALMOST every time.  There seems to be some detail in the necessary requirements
I haven't understood.

Additional Information:
I'm using a Fedora system.  In particular, I'm using
firefox-3.6.3-2.fc13.x86_64.
