Description of Problem:
Moonlight Codecs Installer causes crash in Firefox 3.0.5

Steps to reproduce the problem:
1. Install moonlight codec from
http://www.go-mono.com/archive/moonlight-plugins/latest/novell-moonlight-1.0-x86_64.xpi

http://www.microsoft.com/silverlight/resources/install.aspx?mode=sysreq&reason=unsupportedplatform&v=2.0#sysreq
