the browser.

Reproducible: Always

1. Just press Use Webclient several times (confirm messages)
2. When completed event doesn't appears, browser process it's stucked.
3. When stucked refreshing the page causes browser hang.

Just press both button several times until you miss a "Completed" or "done"
message.

Keep downloading the file
