Test Case

User-Agent:       Mozilla/5.0 (X11; U; Linux x86_64; en-US) AppleWebKit/534.10
(KHTML, like Gecko) Chrome/8.0.552.224 Safari/534.10

When navigating through a usercontrol that has a Telerik RadComboBox, when
passing the focus onto the combobox, it will not display the focus UI hint.
This happens if you are pressing tab to iterate through the controls in the
usercontrol. If on the other hand, you actually click the combobox, then it
will display the focus hint not to hide it again after the combobox looses the
focus.

Well, better than reading this explanation is trying out the test-case I am
attaching.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Run TestPage.html
2. Ensure the first textbox has focus.
3. Iterate through the controls by pressing tab.
4. Notice that the combobox does not display a visual hint when it has the
focus.
5. Click the combobox and notice that now it does display the focus hint.
6. Cycle through the controls again, either through tabs or by clicking and
notice that now the opposite has happened and that the combo never stops
displaying the focus hint even when it does not have the focus.
Actual Results:  
Focus hint in the telerik combobox is broken.

Expected Results:  
Focus hint in the telerik combobox working just like every other control.
