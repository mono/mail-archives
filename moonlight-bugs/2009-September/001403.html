NOTE: I'm actually using 1.99.3, but the closest version available in bugzilla
is 1.99.1.

At http://simon.nureality.ca/?p=39 , there is a video widget that is mostly
incompatible with Moonlight.  I have not tried it in Silverlight so I don't
know what the exact behavior should be, but if you hover over the bottom area,
the play controls are non-functional.  The volume slider and mute button seem
to work fine.

Glancing at the source, I see the widget is meant to play the video at
"/wp-content/videos/monotouch-02.wmv", which does play fine, so it doesn't
appear to be a codec problem or anything.
