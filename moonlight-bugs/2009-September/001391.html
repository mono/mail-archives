--- Comment #7 from Rodrigo Kumpera <rkumpera at novell.com>  2009-09-05 13:13:43 MDT ---
This has been caused by a recent change to verify the outcome of vtable
layouts.

The runtime has, in so far, never bothered to ensure optimal allocation of
vtable slots, been lenient with empty slots.

The change was to enforce that vtables of concrete types have zero bubbles on
them. This, of course, had the side effect of uncovering a few bugs in the
layout code.

I have the fix for this bug, which has the added advantage of reducing vtable
sizes. I'll finish QA'ing it today.
