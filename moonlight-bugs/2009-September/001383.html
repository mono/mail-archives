--- Comment #2 from Jeffrey Stedfast <fejj at novell.com>  2009-09-04 11:47:39 MDT ---
make sure your ~/.mozilla/plugins directory isn't owned by root or something
and has write permissions for your user account.

I just nuked my ~/.mozilla directory and then launched firefox and installed
Moonlight 2.0 Beta 3, restarted firefox, and then went to
http://silverlight.net/fox to invoke the codec download and it created
~/.mozilla/plugins, ~/.mozilla/plugins/moonlight, and installed the codecs to
~/.mozilla/plugins/moonlight/silverlight-media-pack-linux-x86-15-1.so

>From the website you linked to, it sounds like older versions of the Moonlight
beta might not have created ~/.mozilla/plugins if it didn't already exist, but
Beta 3 does for sure.
