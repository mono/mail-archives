I have a page with a scrollviewer and a storyboard. The storyboard is meant to
animate the contents of the scrollviewer with a DoubleAnimation that applies a
TranslateTransform creating a slide on the X axis. The XAML is as follows (a
working demo is attached).

In ML, when beginning the storyboard, there contents of the scrollviewer will
essentially vanish. However, if you hover the mouse over where they should be
you can see them flickering and then vanishing again.

I have attached a working demo.

Reproducible: Always

Steps to Reproduce:
1. Run the TestPage.html of the attachment I am sending.
2. Notice that you have a button inside a Grid. The grid's background is khaki.
Notice as well that the grid is inside a scrollviewer. The scrollviewer's
background is salmon (no complaints about my taste for colours, please :-P).
3. Press the "Slide me to the left" button. This button will .Begin the
storyboard that is supposed to slide the khaki grid to the left, in a very slow
motion (in 30 seconds).
4. See that after you pressed the button, the only thing you see is salmon, the
scrollviewer's background. Its contents vanished.
5. Hover the mouse over where the button still is (this is easy because it
fills the entire width of the screen and because it is sliding so slowly).
6. See that even though you still cannot see the grid's background again (the
khaki), you will see the button flicker.
Actual Results:  
The scrollviewer's contents to vanish, even though the animation is taking
place indeed. When hovering the mouse over the button, the button is
flickering, but then vanishing again.

Expected Results:  
A big khaki grid with a button sliding to the left.
