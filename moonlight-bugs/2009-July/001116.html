Description of Problem:

Panopto Program does not go to the proper video playback point and does not go
to proper screen capture screen.

I am using Moonlight version 1.9.5 on Ubuntu Linux 9.04.

Steps to reproduce the problem:
1.  Go to
http://puritan.hosted.panopto.com/CourseCast/Viewer/Default.aspx?id=09952776-64d1-4b5f-b070-f7e36ece88ad
2.  Change the playback point by doing one of the 3 things:


a. On the seekbar (right below the video screen which is on the upper left
hand),  click and drag the "seeker", the white rounded rectangle to change the
playback point

OR

b. In the "Contents" tab (below the video), click one of the listed
time-points. (E.g., 2:59; 5:59; 8:59; etc.)

OR

c. On the lower right hand corner, move the slider tot he right an click one of
the other screen thumbnails.


Actual results:

Video restarts at 0:00

and

Screen Capture goes back to the first Screen Capture ("Messanging Zechariah I")



Expected Results:

Video would play at whatever point was decided on

AND

Screen Capture should change to whatever is the screen capture for the decided
playback point




How often does this happen?

Whenever I do the above steps.
