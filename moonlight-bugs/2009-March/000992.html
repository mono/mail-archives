--- Comment #6 from Aaron Bockover <abockover at novell.com>  2009-03-26 15:20:04 MST ---
I figured out the likely cause of the crash you are seeing, again another issue
in the NPAPI support in WebKit/QT. In Moonlight 1.0.1, a patch was committed to
work around the fact that WebKit/QT smashes the stack where the plugin tables
are proxied to/from the browser inside of NP_Initialize.

The patch in Moonlight is just a defensive fix to work around the browser doing
bad things. So really, this bug is probably solved by 1.0.1 Moonlight. Can you
confirm?

It should not be crashing with 1.0.1, but Moonlight/Moonshine still will not be
functional in WebKit/QT.
