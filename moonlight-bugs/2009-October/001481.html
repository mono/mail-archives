My med school (Indiana University) records and publishes lectures online using
Mediasite.  They are using Mediasite 5.0.2083 when I right click on "Powered by
Mediasite" and view properties (which the school's IT told me is 5.0.5, build
2083).  The audio comes through fine, but I have to manually switch slides,
which then pauses the audio every time.  This basically makes the lectures
useless because whenever I click through to the next slide, the audio pauses
and resets itself to where it should have been when that slide started.

I've also tried with the latest 2.0 beta (1.99.5) but that one doesn't even
start the slideshow/lecture.  The 2.0 beta used to work, but then it stopped
but I'm not sure if that was due to my school updating Mediasite or a change in
the beta version.  I don't think the slide transitions were quite on time every
time, but it at least switched to the next one.  Now the site just gives me a
link to download MS Silverlight, which is a little useless in my case.

Let me know if you need me to attach any files.  It doesn't give me any
particular error messages when viewing the lectures, though when I first open
firefox from a terminal it says:

-------------------------
(firefox:14461): Moonlight-WARNING **: Forcing client-side rendering because we
detected binary drivers which are known to suffer performance problems.
windowless mode
-------------------------

For reference, my specs are as follows:
Ubuntu 9.10 64-bit
2GB RAM
NVIDIA 1.85.18.36 driver with Compiz running
Firefox 3.0.14
Moonlight 1.0.1 (doesn't automatically progress to next slide)
Moonlight 1.99.5 (doesn't show slides or play audio at all)
