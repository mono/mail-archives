quakelight is playing a lot of different sound effects at the same time, which
requires mixing those. Moonlight leaves the mixing to the underlying audio
engine (pulseaudio or alsa), for pulse apparently this causes a lot of streams
to show up in the applications tab (Moonlight is really playing a lot of
streams).

There's not much we can do here except doing the mixing ourselves, which is
really a lot of work for no real gain.
