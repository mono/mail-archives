Steps to reproduce:
 - Moonlight r108070
 - Firefox 3
 - http://www.popfly.com

 - Click on create game
 - click on any game (Escape)
 - click on Scenes
 - click on Draw, Text

 A text box should be in the scene now
 - On the right, click on the color picker

Expected results:
 - clicking a different color should change the color.

Actual results:
 - once you click on the color picker, the selection will follow your mouse,
changing color constantly as you move.
 - This happens in all three color picker frames, the large color shade picker,
the vertical rainbow picker, and the vertical opacity picker.
