When you draw an Ellipse in xaml with equal Height and Width the shape is not a
perfect circle. Using Moonlight 1.0 and Firefox 3.0.1.

Here's the xaml:

<Canvas
   xmlns="http://schemas.microsoft.com/client/2007"
   xmlns:x="http://schemas.microsoft.com/winfx/2006/xaml">

  <Ellipse x:Name="ellipse"
     Canvas.Left="30" Canvas.Top="30"
     Height="200" Width="200"
     Stroke="Orange" StrokeThickness="10" Fill="SlateBlue" Opacity="0.5" />

</Canvas>

See also: http://bomahy.nl/hylke/wip/moonlight.png
