I created a new folder gc-wine in the System.Windows.Forms/
System.Windows.Forms to contain the differences between
gc6.1-wine.tar.gz and gc6.1.tar.gz available from Mono download page.
The files can be added to the sources of Boehm GC 6.1
and gc-wine.mak should be used to compile/install the GC.
I think that changes/additions do not influence other types of builds.
