They are actually sorted, but in a twisted kind of way.
It's alphabetical, but the controls that are not derived directly from 
control are alphabetically listed directly under the control they are 
derived from. For example, ButtonBase is the daddy for Button, CheckBox and 
RadioButton, so it's listed right underneath. There's a system to the 
madness :-)
