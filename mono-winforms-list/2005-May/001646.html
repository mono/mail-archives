You are free to create the RichTextBox stub you want/need.
Since Form ist still missing MDI, it is marked as under development, that 
simple. It has nothing to do with how much of it is done, it simply means 
that we're still actively working on it and do not consider it complete. If 
you're curious what is implemented in each class, you can look at the class 
status (http://svn.myrealbox.com/mwf/class-status-System.Windows.Forms.html) 
without having to do a code review. Attributes (or whole classes) that don't 
have a MonoTODO tag attached but are not missing are supposed to be working. 
And no, those are not hard rules.

No need to be sorry about the "1.1.7 has the same problems " stuff. MWF is 
not complete, and therefore I'm not bothered by people telling us it's not 
complete. It's not advertised as complete. As long as it's under active 
development you just have to live with that stuff. The team is working hard 
and fast on implementing the missing pieces, but you're gonna have to resign 
yourself to the fact that it's gonna take some time and that those things 
important to you might not be done first before some other things not as 
important to you.

Cheers,
  Peter
