> Try running your assembly like this: 
> 
> MONO_LOG_LEVEL=debug mono --trace TestJemForms.exe
> 
> this sets the MONO_LOG_LEVEL environment variable to debug, so you see what
> native libraries are loaded. Adding the -trace option will let you see what
> is being executed. (You could even add filtering there by namespace, so you
> see only things that are interesting to you). 
>
