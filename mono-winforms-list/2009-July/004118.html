There is currently no work going on in this area.

If anyone wants to play with this, I would suggest looking at 
implementing the System.Windows.Forms.VisualStyles namespace for 
linux/mac.  This namespace breaks it down into tiny little elements to 
implement, and then we already have George's excellent VisualStyles 
theme that puts all those elements together to paint the controls.
