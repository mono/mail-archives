wx.NET is a .NET wrapper around wxWidgets, this GUI toolkit may be
good (although I think it's a bit complicated) but is not compatible
with winforms! You can use wxWidgets by simply adding a reference to
it but keep in mind that: Winforms != wxWidgets ;)
