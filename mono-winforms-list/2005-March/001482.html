You can log bugs for MessageBox. The 'owners' list does not claim 
completeness, it only lists derivatives of Control, not every class. If 
you're curious about who wrote a particular class, you can always peek in 
the header of the file, it usually lists who worked on it.
Anything not specifically listed on the owners page is taken care of by the 
core team until it's either complete or a volunteer has mercy with it.

Specific completeness levels for *every* class in MWF can be checked here:
http://svn.myrealbox.com/mwf/class-status-System.Windows.Forms.html

(MessageBox has a TODO list in it's header, too, might list as missing what 
you are having problems with)

Cheers,
  Peter
