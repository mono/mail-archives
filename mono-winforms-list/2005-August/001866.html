So I've been watching the banter for the last week or so and it seems to me
that the current estimates for completing mwf is awfully optimistic. I
understand being "feature complete" is the goal, but as a potential
developer, I'm wondering when WinForms will be _easily_ installable,
developed against, and usable in a production environment on a Linux
desktop.

 

The timeline should have more endpoints:

 

1.	WinForms 1.0

a.	feature complete
b.	tested and performance tuned for Suse 9.x
c.	tested and performance tuned for Fedora __
d.	tested and performance tuned for Ubuntu
e.	tested and performance tuned for MacOS
f.	simple installation
g.	samples

2.	WinForms 2.0

a.	feature complete
b.	performance tuned for Suse 9.x
c.	performance tuned for Fedora __
d.	performance tuned for Ubuntu
e.	tested and performance tuned for MacOS
f.	simple installation
g.	samples

 

When you get to "1g", then I'm really going to get excited about using mono.
When will that be?
