The simplest method is to use the redcarpet mono channel to pull the
packages.

Short of that you will need:
- cairo (http://www.cairographics.org/download) and it's dependencies
(listed at the website)
- libgdiplus (mono cvs)
- winelib (mono cvs)
- wine (http://www.winehq.com/site/download)

Cheers,
