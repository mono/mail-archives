I'd like to notify the swf developers that someone had the courage to start 
working on the new uxtheme.dll for Wine. On WindowsXP/2003 this dll handles

saw that he send in a patch to make Wine use gtk colors but I haven't seen 
more. If he is still working on this I'd recommend him to contact the uxtheme 
guy(s).
