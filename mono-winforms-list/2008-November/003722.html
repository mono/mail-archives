Seems like doing a C-wrapper to be called via P/Invoke is the easier path to
do what you want: have a single managed binary and two native [C/C++]
binaries.

C++.NET and specially mixed-mode assemblies aren´t supported in mono, as
they break architecture/platform cross-portability.

Hope it helps,
