For the record, my own app uses PixelOffsetMode to work around a GDI+ problem
(rounding issues when zooming in/out), but although this method isn't
implemented in Mono, it's not actually needed for me since Mono doesn't have
this issue in the first place. So if your case is similar, you can ignore
the PixelOffsetMode part of the report.

The real issues are the P/Invokes. If you can't change these parts of the
code, this app just won't work out of Windows (Wine aside).
