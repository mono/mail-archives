Gluezilla is a helper library required for the winforms WebBrowser to
work, it's a part of the Mono project and it's distributed on the
official repositories. You're probably looking in the wrong place for
it, your system should have it available. You can go to
http://software.opensuse.org/search to look for any openSuse packages,
but it should already be available on your system, it's part of the
standard repository.
