Currently, there's no difference in the level of 'completeness' of SWF
between 0.28 and 0.31. I am tasked to have SWF complete by the time Mono
1.2 is released. You don't need to build out of CVS to always have the
latest, you should be fine by using pulling a new daily (
http://go-mono.com/daily/ )whenever you want to check progress.

In theory, things shouldn't get corrupted by a refresh, but the safe way,
if you always need a working mono version, build the latest with a
different, private <prefix> dir for testing. That keeps your RPM version
of mono untouched until you update to a newer RPM.

Cheers,
  Peter
