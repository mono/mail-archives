Hi Shivansh,

We can't tell you what you should do based on your skills. You need to
decide what you want to do and write a proposal telling us what you're
doing to do. If you need more information on the ideas that are listed
on our project page, you should ask them specifically. To ask good
questions, you need to think about the project and have an idea of the
technologies envolved, and decide which parts you understand and which
parts you don't understand.

For anything regarding Monodevelop, you should start looking at how to
create addins and extend Monodevelop. For that, you need to get the
source and figure out how it works, follow relevant tutorials, etc.
You don't have to be an expert, but you should at least be able to
know how to build an addin.

I short, you need to do a bit of research on your own.
