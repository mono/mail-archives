Hi Henry,
Our gdiplus.dll comes from a separate module called libgdiplus.
You can get it from cvs or use the rpms.
You also need to set your $LD_LIBRARY_PATH to tell the location
of libgdiplus.so to your system.

I hope Paul's URL would be quite helpful for you.

If you are interested to know more about System.Drawing and
SWF, following links would tell you about our approach,

http://www.mono-project.com/contributing/winforms.html 
http://www.mono-project.com/contributing/drawing.html
