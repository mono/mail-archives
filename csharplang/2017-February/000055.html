Hi all,

Swift-evolution, from which csharplang is heavily inspired (a good thing!),
has a common proposals document:
https://github.com/apple/swift-evolution/blob/master/commonly_proposed.md

I suggest that we have some kind of similar document in csharplang in order
to avoid having to deal with duplicate proposals.

The document should detail a description and rationale of proposals.

In my view, this document should be well linked to, and be a perquisite for
anyone posting proposals on the mailing list.

I think that the document should
- Identify the importance of source/binary compatability
- Identify the importance of consistency of features that work together
- Identify the philosophy of C#
- Emphasize that C# doesn't just want to copy other languages (e.g. copying
Swift features) to achieve feature parity

What do you think? Post some examples of commonly rejected proposals if you
want, to get the ball rolling and think about what we should include in the
document.
