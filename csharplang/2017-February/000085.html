All,

In light of the general community feedback on the choice of a mailman
mailing list as the forum for discussing language changes, a public poll
has been created to allow everyone the chance to vote on their preferred
platform. This can then be used to guiding the language team in adopting a
platform that will suit the most people.

For details, please see https://github.com/dotnet/roslyn/issues/16971.
