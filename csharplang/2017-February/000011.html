This is the only reasonable proposal that will ever be posted to this
mailing list.

The move to using mailing lists for community language proposals and
discussions is beyond unreasonable.  This medium lacks any of the features
that make collaborative discussion possible.  The experience is beyond
awful.

I would recommend quite literally anything else as an alternative.  If the
language team does not want to be bogged down in managing community
proposals then I personally offer to open a separate Github repo for the
purpose of tracking them.  I and other community members can categorize
proposals and offer them to the team for consideration.
