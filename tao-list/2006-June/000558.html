I've posted a lot of bug fixes since the last release that are only in SVN
due to the infrequency of Tao releases.  If you can compile your own
Tao.Odefrom the SVN source, do that.  Alternatively, grab the binary
from the
OdeDotNet package (
http://sourceforge.net/project/showfiles.php?group_id=153455) and see if
that works better - it's more current than the official Tao binary.  Let me
know.

I'm working on an updated release to coincide with last week's ODE
0.6release (highly recommended - many bug fixes and improvements),
which I'll
post when I finish it.  Moving forward, I think I may just post
Tao.Odereleases separately.  In retrospect, the "one big Tao release"
concept
doesn't work very well, particularly when different subprojects are handled
by different maintainers and move at a different pace.
