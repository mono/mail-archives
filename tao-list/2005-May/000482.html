Hi Ben,

Since OpenAL uses an extension scheme similar to OpenGL
(alIsExtensionPresent and alGetProcAddress), each extension needs some
special handling to be usable from the C# environment.  Unfortunately,
none of the OpenAL extensions are handled at the moment by Tao.OpenAL.
 I'm not sure when that will happen, though...

Sorry that I couldn't give you a better answer!
