Hi,

Refresh() and Invalidate() are methods inherited from Control class.

Invalidate() invalidates the entire region of the control. Refresh() does
the same plus forces redraw of all child controls.

Draw() is a SimpleOpenGlControl specific method, but does exactly the same
thing as Invalidate() (actually it just calls Invalidate())
