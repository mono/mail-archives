Hi folks,

The release files are now available on the Mono download site, and are
linked from http://www.mono-project.com/Tao .  The 1.2.0 release has
support for all known OpenGL extensions at this time, as well as
extensive improvements to Tao.Sdl and Tao.Ode. The mono and .NET
builds are not yet interchangeable; please download a specific build
for either the mono runtime or the win32 .NET runtime.

http://www.go-mono.com/tao/1.2.0/Tao-1.2.0-2-mono.zip
http://www.go-mono.com/tao/1.2.0/Tao-1.2.0-2-win32.zip
http://www.go-mono.com/tao/1.2.0/Tao-1.2.0-2-win32-Setup.exe

Many thanks to those who contributed in getting this release going,
especially to David Hudson for his work on Sdl, the win32 installer,
and various other bugfixes and improvements throughout Tao.

Future plans for Tao.OpenGL include continuing work on the OpenGL
bindings (there are still some marshalling issues with some extension
parameters), and potentially starting a more managed-feeling
lightweight OpenGL framework on top of those bindings.

Thanks!

    - Vlad
