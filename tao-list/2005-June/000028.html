I had the same problem, and I have a partial fix for you.
You need to adjust your Tao.SDL.dll.config to point at
your copy of libSDL.dylib etc...

Mine happens to be on the default dylib lookup paths path so I can get
away with...

<configuration>
     <dllmap dll="SDL.dll" target="libSDL.dylib" />
     <dllmap dll="SDL_image.dll" target="libSDL_image.dylib" />
     <dllmap dll="SDL_mixer.dll" target="libSDL_mixer.dylib" />
     <dllmap dll="SDL_ttf.dll" target="libSDL_ttf.dylib" />
     <dllmap dll="smpeg.dll" target="libsmpeg.dylib" />
     <dllmap dll="SDL_gfx.dll" target="libSDL_gfx.dylib" />
</configuration>


You might need full paths depending on your SDL set-up. Run the example 
again and you'll get lots of errors. SDL is dependant on Cocoa on OSX so 
it needs a little extra setting up. There's thread on
the mono-osx list.

http://galactus.ximian.com/pipermail/mono-osx/2005-June/000217.html
