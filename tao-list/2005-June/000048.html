glActiveTextureARB and glActiveTexture are the same function, except
that you have to explicitly obtain a pointer for glActiveTextureARB,
and glActiveTexture is supposed to be exposed by the GL implementation
if the implementation exposes OpenGL 1.2 (I believe).  So, on
Microsoft's impl, you probably want to use LoadExtension() and
glActiveTextureARB.

However, what errors are you seeing when you do LoadAllExtensions?
