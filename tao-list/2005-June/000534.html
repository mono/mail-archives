I've had a go at building Tao on OSX and it builds!

I just had to change the config files too...

--- Tao.OpenGl.Glu.dll.config and Tao.OpenGl.dll.config ---

<configuration>
     <dllmap dll="opengl32.dll" 
target="/System/Library/Frameworks/OpenGL.framework/Versions/Current/Libraries/libGL.dylib" 
/>
     <dllmap dll="glu32.dll" 
target="/System/Library/Frameworks/OpenGL.framework/Versions/Current/Libraries/libGLU.dylib" 
/>
</configuration>

--- Tao.FreeGlut.dll.config ---

<configuration>
     <dllmap dll="freeglut.dll" 
target="/System/Library/Frameworks/GLUT.framework/GLUT" />
</configuration>

---
