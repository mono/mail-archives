Tao.Sdl is meant to mirror the SDL lib as closely as possible. That is why
there are all of the IntPtrs. For a more object oriented approach, take a
look at SDL.NET at http://cs-sdl.sourceforge.net which builds upon Tao.Sdl
and includes marshaling and disposing.
