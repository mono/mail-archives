One of the things to take in mind when you use the core features that were previously extensions, is that it's not enough to test the extension string, you also have to test the opengl version.

The errors below are caused by errors in ATI driver implementation and are not tao's fault. I wouldn't worry about it unless you wanted to use those entrypoints.
