I don't have any ideas on the freeglut problem; I believe Randy posted
a DLL that he built at some point, you might check the list archives
for it.

I'd suggest using GLFW instead (glfw.sourceforge.net).  Its design is
much better, and it avoids many of the issues (like callbacks) that
glut has.  However, it also doesn't do things like menus.
