Tao simply provides wrappers for various libraries, such as GLFW and
GLUT.  It does not provide those libraries itself.  Check out
http://glfw.sf.net/ (glfw needs a bit of massaging to make it build as
a .so on linux) for libglfw.so, or freeglut for libglut.so.
