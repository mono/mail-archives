On 8/14/05, Anton Andreev <Anton.Andreev at fmi.uni-sofia.bg> wrote:
> 
>  1. There are no packages for glfw, it is not installed by default in
> Linix distros. I could not compile it myself. Shouldn't be included in
> tao source???

No, because the glfw source has nothing to do with Tao -- just like we
don't ship the SDL, OpenAL, ODE, etc. sources.  If you're having
problems building glfw, your best bet would be to use the freeglut
packages and the glut demos.

Note that the freeglut soname seems to change names often; if it's not
libglut.so.3, then edit Tao.FreeGlut.dll.config and change
libglut.so.3 to whatever the actual library is installed by your
packages.
