The idea is, Tao is meant to be a "perfect" wrap of the library, as it
is. In OpenGL, the constants are GL_xxx, and in SDL they are SDL_xxx.
This makes porting applications easier, because the author only needs
to add Sdl. or Gl. in front of all calls.

I think that's why, anyway!
