However wouldnt it be much easier/more intuitive that instead of
prepending Sdl to everything that you just make the namespaces the
equivalent of static classes and have SDL.xxx instead of SDL_xxx (as
opposed to Sdl.SDL_xxx) so that you are just using '.' instead of '_'.
 That would mirror the design of this C libraries far more closely.

On 8/3/05, Oliver Charles <depth.of.field at gmail.com> wrote:
> The idea is, Tao is meant to be a "perfect" wrap of the library, as it
> is. In OpenGL, the constants are GL_xxx, and in SDL they are SDL_xxx.
> This makes porting applications easier, because the author only needs
> to add Sdl. or Gl. in front of all calls.
>
> I think that's why, anyway!
>
> - Oliver Charles
