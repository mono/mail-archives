Terry and I worked together to create OdeDotNet 
(http://odedotnet.sourceforge.net); He says that he didn't help at all, 
but incorrect he is.  OdeDotNet is much like SDL.NET, but for Tao.Ode, 
as it provides an object oriented interface over the hard bindings.  
Although it's not quite feature complete, it does come with a couple of 
example projects.

Also be sure to join the forum (http://odedotnet.sourceforge.net/forum) 
and state your progress with the library.  It's still very young and 
definitely needs all the help it can get.
