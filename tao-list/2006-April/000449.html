Hi Chris,

 

Are you sure you have Vertical Sync ON ? Should you have it off, I would
consider this quite normal, under the fact, that SimpleOpenGlControl uses
OnPaint handler for drawing. So if you do your animations by calling
SimpleOpenGlControl.Draw() (or any other form of invalidation) at the end of
you drawing code, you essentially resend the Paint message all over and thus
your processor gets busy, because this loop can run at more than thousand
FPS.

Nevertheless if I switch vertical sync on and thus let the loop run at 60 Hz
of my monitor refresh rate, I receive about 10% processor load on my 3000+
AMD Athlon 64.
