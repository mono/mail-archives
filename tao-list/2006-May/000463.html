The Tao Framework is still under maintenance, yes.  As for Tao.OpenGl, 
Vlad is around off and on as with Randy.  Considering it's open-source 
nature of Tao, feel free to play around with it and see if you can get 
anything working.  Tao needs both users and developers.

The website was just brought back to life at http://www.taoframework.com 
.  All content isn't on there yet (it's missing almost all that's really 
needed).  The forum is also still quite young, but everything is setup 
and it's starting to bring the community back.

Stick on Tao.OpenGl and the glShaderSourceARB. Post your experiences on 
the forum and try out as much stuff as you can.  If you get it working, 
please feel free to post an article or two on the wiki.  Keep it up, 
you're doing the right thing!
