Group Leaders!

It has been a very exciting month so far and I wanted to reach out to see
how everyone was doing. Last week in San Francisco was Build and it was
great catching up with a few of you that were able to attend. If you missed
it we did an entire re-cap
blog<http://blog.xamarin.com/xamarin-c-sharp-ios-and-android-take-center-stage-at-microsoft-build/>
of
the Build events, and if you haven't already checked out
http://www.xamarin.com/shirt, it is our new C# shirt promotion for you and
your group.

Feedback from you, the group leaders, is extremely valuable to us and I
love reading from our Event Feedback
form<https://docs.google.com/a/xamarin.com/forms/d/1XY1LPbqNIcs5rEU4Gm-X_20cfPsXu1JHeGneAXElNVI/viewform>.
If you haven't done so yet for March and early April could you take a
minute to fill out this form. In addition to helping giving us insight to
the groups we will continue to unlock more awesome swag for everyone if you
continue to fill it out.

As you know Evolve 2014 tickets have just gone on sale this morning. The
whole team will be out in Atlanta, so I hope to see you all out there!

A few months back when we started the sponsorship program I sent out a
Xamarin University slide for presentations that could be used. I got a few
requests for some updated slides, so that is exactly what I did! Attached
you will fine a Xamarin Evolve and C# T-shirt slide that you can use as
part of your talks each month.

As always please let me know how we here at Xamarin can be helping you to
continue to grow this amazing community.

Best,
James
