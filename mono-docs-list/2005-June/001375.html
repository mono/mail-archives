On Mon, 2005-06-06 at 10:33 +0200, Fabian wrote:
> just tried to see monodoc's Gtk# browser's code, and I didin't find it
> in svn repository. 

It was moved out of the `monodoc' module and into the `mono-tools'
module, under the docbrowser directory.  See: 

	http://mono.myrealbox.com/source/trunk/mono-tools/docbrowser/

This was done to remove a circular build dependency between monodoc &
gtk-sharp (gtk-sharp depended on monodoc for its own documentation, but
monodoc depended on gtk-sharp for the docbrowser...).
