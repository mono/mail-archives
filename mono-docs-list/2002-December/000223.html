Hi there!

I see a lot of documentation work going on at the moment,
the GNOME.NET tutorial, a manual, http://mono.jroith.de/monkeyguide/,
and more.

What do you guys think about merging these efforts?

I suggest a name like The Mono Tutorial, by inspiration of The Java
Tutorial http://java.sun.com/docs/books/tutorial/

Everybody could work on what they are good at, the c#, basic.net 
languages, mono tools, class libraries (gtk#, qt#, ...), IDEs,
installation (linux, mac, win32), and whatever involves mono.

It would also be easy and nice to point people to just that page on the
net, and they would find everything they need, plus they would see how
large a scope mono has.
