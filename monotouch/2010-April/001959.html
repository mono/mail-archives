I think it's a bug. Kanga said that sometimes MD has trouble picking up the IP address, especially on mutli-nic'd machines.

i've had the same issue. when it happens, you can edit the MonoDevelopProperties.xml file in ~/.config/MonoDevelop. there's a key called MonoTouch.Debugger.HostIP

edit it to be the right IP, close MD and re-open.
