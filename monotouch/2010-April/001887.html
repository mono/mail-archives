I also believe that it was Adobe's flash compiler that triggered the change
in policy.

However I would be naive to think they didn't know about the existence of
Unity, MonoTouch and Titanium, etc.
Unfortunately their ruthless legal/executive team are not staffed with
muppets, they could've easily chosen wording to exclude Adobe and not other
technologies if that was their intention.
It is possible that ruling out a single competitor and not others is a
suable offence, in which case Unity/MT/Titanium/etc would be unfortunate
necessary victims.

They are not immune to public pressure though, if this draconian policy has
any effect on tarnishing their image I believe they will reverse it.
I think our best bet is to somehow get the word out. Anyone got any 'Apple:
does evil' badges lying around? :)

- Demis
