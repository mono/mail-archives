Hi,

So, for all of you, to diagnose why debugging fails:

* Add "-v - v -v" to the additional mtouch arguments.
* Rebuild & try to debug.
* When it fails, open the iOS Device Log pad in MonoDevelop and get all the
lines since the app started.
* Also get any output in the Application Output pad in MonoDevelop.
* Reply to this thread with this information.

You can also try disabling the USB debugging (in MonoDevelop's Preferences,
go to Preferences/Debugger/iPhone Debugger and check the checkbox there),
for some people it's more reliable.

Thanks!
Rolf
