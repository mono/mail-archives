Ok, so "build adhoc / enterprise" was already ticked.

I was still having code signing issues. I was creating distribution profiles
but I couldn't get code signing to work at all. I think its related to the
Identity in iPhone Bundle Signing. The distribution profile was appearing
under a different identity and I don't know why.

So, I went back to Xcode, went to Provisioning Profiles and saw an option at
the bottom called Automatic Device Provisioning. I clicked Refresh and it
downloaded a *different* profile, but that one appears under my Identity in
iPhone Bundle Signing.

And that worked! That new profile contained all my testers, and they are now
happily downloading and testing my app.

Hope this helps someone else if they get stuck in the same area.
