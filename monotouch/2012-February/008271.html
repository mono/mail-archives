Short answer: No, there isn‘t.

 

There is no real Multitasking in iOS and Monotouch cannot do magic and add
some on top.

You can have Task Completion which will allow you 10 minutes (?) of extra
time to perform a task after your app has exited, or you can make use of one
of the multitasking enabled APIS: VOIP, Location based services and music
streaming.

That’s it. If your app doesn’t deal with either of them, you’re out of luck.
