Figured it out. Just thought I'd update this thread in case anyone could
learn from it.

I ended out using:
CAShapeLayer maskLayer =  new CAShapeLayer ();
maskLayer.Frame = view.Bounds;
maskLayer.Path = MyCustomCGPath;
view.Layer.Mask = maskLayer;

and put that in the Draw() method.

Hope this helps.

Brett
