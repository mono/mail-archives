take this with a huge grain of salt...  wiser minds may correct me

but I think if your app/bundle id is the same, them your app's data should
be preserved

every iOS install, regardless of whether or not it is new install or an
update to existing app, is a complete overwrite of the existing app
package.  So from the installers perspective, it does not matter that you
are replacing an XCode app with a MT app - it will still behave the same
and overwrite the old copy of the app
