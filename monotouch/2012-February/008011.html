There are several minor issues with Xcode 4.3 support which we are
currently dealing with. I'd recommend you do not update just yet. You
should wait until we release a new version of MonoTouch and MonoDevelop
which complete the support for Xcode 4.3. Unfortunately the preview
releases of Xcode 4.3 were not feature complete so we were not able to have
everything fully prepared for an immediate release.

If you do update and choose to delete Xcode 4.2 when Xcode 4.3 prompts you
about it, you may end up deleting MonoTouch. If that happens you should
either install it again or recover it from your Trash and place it back in
/Developer.

Thanks,
Alan
