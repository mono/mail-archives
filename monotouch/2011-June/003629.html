it's definitely possible (http://bit.ly/magshop2)

in your app, check out
https://github.com/Clancey/ClanceyLib/blob/master/ClanceysLib/AppStore/InAppPurchaseManager.cs

we used server-side stuff (being .net guys) courtesy of @redth -
https://github.com/Redth/APNS-Sharp (lots of code re: notifications but also
appstore stuff there that's useful)

also - we have all sorts of weird/interesting behaviour when testing inside
our corporate network (proxies, etc) so always test on public internet when
you can (StoreKit especially!). also make sure you have 'signed out' of any
"real" store accounts on the device (sometimes i hard-restart the device
after this, just to be sure...)
