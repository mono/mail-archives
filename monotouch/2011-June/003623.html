I need to write this up as a blog post or something :(

Novell got sold to Attachmate (end of April). Attachmate fired all the
Mono* developers (Monotouch, monodevelop, etc). As they
(Novell/Attachmate) have not put a support process in place, it's hard
to get any word out of them except "we might be doing some support
sometime soon".

Tho you might get _some_ joy if you call Novell (contact info on their
website, I'd suggest trying the US number if you can and look back
thru the email list, lots of info there).

They DO have a 30 day refund policy tho, so I'm told.

On the up side, Miguel and co have formed Xamarin

http://tirania.org/blog/archive/2011/May-16.html

http://xamarin.com/

... to basically rewrite MonoTouch/MonoDroid/etc going forward, with
most of the same team. It's underway, and the timeframe was said to be
"3 months or so" - from early May.

So, unless you can get some joy out of Novell's support people, either
go thru the refund process, or contact your bank about doing a charge
back.

:(
