I guess it works something like this.

As part of the online registration it reads yr hard disk ID, sends it off to
Novell, then they hash it up and send back a registration code.

When you use monotouch it gets your current hard disk ID and hashes it up,
then compares it to your registration id if they don't match it knows your
system is not the original registered platform.
