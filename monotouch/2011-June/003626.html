Apple's policy has always been "use the latest SDK only". So once 5.0
goes RTM, you MUST compile against the 5.0 SDK, which means you can't
(I think) use MT 4.0.3.

Fingers crossed that Xamarin have their one out by then. :)

Until 5.0 is RTM, you MUST use 4.3(.3?), even if you set the target
SDK to 3.0 (target SDK just tells the phone the minimum required)

So, we should be good until September/October, I suspect.
