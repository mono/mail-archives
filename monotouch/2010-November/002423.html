A remote Safari connecting to your iPhone, or the local Safari connecting to
your server app?

In the first case, you need to get the phone IP address, localhost is not
intended as a public IP address, but an API address that TCP/IP clients can
use to connect to the local device.   As to how to get the IP address, you
can see the Reachability sample in monotouch-samples for assorted ways of
doing that.

The second case will not work, as the iPhone suspends processes when they
are in the background (unless they are doing voice-over-IP or doing audio
streaming)
