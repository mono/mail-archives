Hello,

I've got some code that dismisses one modal view (controller) and then
> shows another, and the second one isn't showing up -- I suspect
> because the first one isn't gone yet. Is that plausible?


Yes;  There is also an iOS bug caused by showing a new controller when
another one is hiding.

The easiest way of coping with that is to not use animations for hiding, and
just for showing or using an NSTimer to trigger the show after a small
amount of time.
