It's not possible, according to the docs. The docs say that it only works
in portrait mode.

However, in OS 4.1 they release a new set of APIs, the AV Assets Framework
that allow you to access the camera directly, without having to use the
image picker. It's wrapped in vnext of monotouch (I bugged it last week
and miguel and geoff were kind enough to wrap it).

So you can either wait until vnext of MT, or do the binding yourself.
