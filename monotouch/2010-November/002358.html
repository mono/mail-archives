We do not know if it takes more memory, or wastes more space.   But we
suspect that like any other large change, we need to look carefully at
the changes that were done over a period of 9 months to ensure that we
do not regress in the areas that matter to mobile development.

So it is at best a guess of the things that we will be looking at, but
it is not the reason why we are not yet shipping a 2.8-based MonoTouch.

There are a lot of bits that need to go into switching all of our users
from the 2.6-based core to the 2.8-core.  Although we have started the
work, we are nowhere ready to push this.
