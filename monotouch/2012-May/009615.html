I've found AppDesignVault to be quite good for full templates.

http://www.appdesignvault.com/

It is all in Obj-C, but you can reuse the images etc, and the
photoshop templates.

That said, you can go a LONG way with just the basic iOS stuff. Apple
have made it easy to do a decent app, even if you are not a designer.
But if you DO have one....

(eg, http://earnestapp.com was done (by me) in MonoTouch, but with a
good designer. It made a HUGE difference. That said, the company I did
it for had a very good in house designer, which helped.

Also good:

Symbolicons: http://symbolicons.com/ (also: cute robot!)
Glyphish Pro:  http://glyphish.com/

Both are worth twice what they cost :)

Thats most of what I use. I'm semi-handy with Photoshop - I can do
basic stuff, but I lack the "taste" and attention to detail of a
proper design person. Thats wasn't obvious until I worked with someone
who lives design.

Hope that helps.

Nic
