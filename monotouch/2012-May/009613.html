I think your bug is your use of the ActivityIndicatorAlertView.

change

alertView.Hidden = true;

to

alertView.DismissWithClickedButtonIndex (-1, true);

in AuthenticatePasscodeController and SetPasscodeController.

Let me know if that is what you are looking for.
