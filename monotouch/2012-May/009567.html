On Sun, May 13, 2012 at 11:53 AM, Slicc <Steven_licciardi at hotmail.com> wrote:
> Sorry, i am new to monotouch, so i am probably being a bit naive. I am ok
> using sslstream when using .net for normal windows applications, what i
> would like to know is, is it available for use in monotouch? I was under the
> impression monotouch was only a subset of the full framework.

Yes, it's available. SslStream [1] resides in System.dll, under the
System.Net.Security namespace, and this is supported by MonoTouch [2]
(even if it was not part of the Silverlight API).

[1] http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/system.net.security.sslstream.aspx
[2] http://docs.xamarin.com/ios/about/assemblies
