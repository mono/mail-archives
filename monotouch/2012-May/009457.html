What about a customview that managed a separate UITableView for each column? 
DataGrids tend to be much larger in rows than columns so a separate
UITableView for each column may not be too bad.  Then you could somehow link
the vertical scrolling of all column tableviews together.  The cell reuse
would be automatic per column tableview.  If you wanted to scroll
horizontal, maybe you put the entire customview in a scrollview that can
only scroll horizontal as the builtin vertical scrolling for each column
tableview would handle the vertical scrolling.  You could even support
reordering columns by dragging them around with a simple touchmoved override
on the customview to rearrange the separate column tableviews.

Is this a bad idea?
