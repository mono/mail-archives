On Tue, May 15, 2012 at 11:36 AM, Art <coderprojects at gmail.com> wrote:
> My iPad app doesn't start in some countries.   Based on the user reports,
> Turkey is the only country right now. Is there anything I can do to repeat
> the crash.

Try changing your device settings's Region Format to "Turkish (Turkey)".

> Is the OS different in each country?

No but the region information will be different. That will affect how
some things works, like parsing values (e.g. different decimal
separator...).
