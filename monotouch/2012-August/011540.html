Op 30 aug. 2012 om 17:47 heeft Rodrigo Kumpera <kumpera at xamarin.com> het volgende geschreven:

> I think the best way to look into this is that your App should not need to handle memory warnings.
> If it receives one, it might be too late to do anything.
> 
> The way to handle it is the same of keeping your memory usage down. Explicitly dispose
> all your large objects as soon as you're done with them. Pay special attention to webviews,
> uicontrollers and images.
> 
>
