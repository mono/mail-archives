Hello,

In short, sync methods are back :)

After another investigation, I found that ServiceContractGenerator in 
WCF RTM (yes, I
originally wrote it a few years ago) generates async and sync methods at 
a time (not exclusively), and thus I could hack svcutil to support 
async-only moonlight and both-async-and-sync monotouch proxies.

Though I haven't really tested it yet, so in case you are using mono 
from trunk, feel free to try
svcutil -monotouch.

Atsushi Eno
