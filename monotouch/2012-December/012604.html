PCI is another kettle of fish. Technically, the LineaPro isn't certifiable, but the next version will be (encrypts at the head). But then no device that is not locked to a single app can be certified either, so we're up against the wall there.

And yes - we call directly to the processors from the device so no CC details are ever transmitted to our server, and they are never stored on the device. The attack window is a few seconds while the CC details are in memory on the device - that's it.
