Happy to announce our iPhone app! Dinner Club --personalized restaurant
recommendations for individual and groups!

If you like discovering new restaurants and have a discerning taste then
you’ll like it. Try it out (for iPhone users)…rate some restaurants and get
personalized recommendations. 
If you like it, rate it in the App Store and give us feedback (and invite
your friends so you can get group recos). If you don’t, just give us
feedback :) 

Please like our FB page (http://www.facebook.com/dinnerclubapp ) to get
updates and know when we’ve added newly opened restaurants. It covers: SF &
extended Bay Area, NYC, Chicago, LA, Atlanta, Boston, DC and Seattle.
