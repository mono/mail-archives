I've seen this in dev builds I've circulated, from time to time. Power
off/power on has fixed it for me in the past. I've also noticed that it can
be de-installed and re-installed (without a power down cycle) and I can get
the same problem. It's rare though and I haven't seen it recently. (From
traces I can see the app delegate wasn't getting launched at all. Something
in the MT startup?)

Cheers, Matt
