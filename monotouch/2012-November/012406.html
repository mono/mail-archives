Dean,

I think this works… but admittedly I've never had to do it.

1) Login to iTunes connect.
2) Click on the app you want to remove.
3) Click on Rights and Pricing near the top right side.
4) Remove the checkbox for the countries (assuming USA) in which you do not want it available.

That should do it, I believe.
