Ah, in that case going "back" should be a natural process, because it means
"I might have made a mistake back there" or "I changed my mind".   You only
need to validate on forward, and you should use a "next" button on top that
performs the validation for going to the next page.

You do not need to validate on "back", you should in that case discard the
data and assume it was not used.
