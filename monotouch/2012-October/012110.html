I am working at an app that heavily uses MonoTouch.Dialog. Most
RootElements use the grouped style. Most of their sections have a footer
text with help and info about their section.

Unfortunately, I have a couple of RootElements that use the plain style
(the sections are OwnerDrawnElements). To provide help and info under
these sections, I would create a StyledMultilineElement that has the
same look and feel of the footer of a grouped RootElement Section.

Alternatively, I could use the grouped approach, but then the top and
bottom element corners stick out beyond the rounded section corners,
and that is also very ugly.

If I would know how to hide / shave off those corners from the first and
the last of my rectangular OwnerDrawnElements, I would definitely go for
that approach.

I hope that this clarifies the reasons why I asked the question.
