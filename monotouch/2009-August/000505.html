I agree with Kevin, an IOC in a resource-constrained platform doesn't
make a lot of sense.

Although I still think you should keep to the IOC spirit and have your
classes bind to interfaces while you bind to your concrete providers
in your 'host project' as it makes it easier to test, swap providers
and manage dependencies.
