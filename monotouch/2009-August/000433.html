Joel,

   The issue is your GameView constructor:

you have

		public GameView(IntPtr handle)
		{
			NSTimer.CreateRepeatingScheduledTimer(TimeSpan.FromSeconds(1), ()  
=> Update());
		}

you should have

		public GameView(IntPtr handle) : base (handle)
		{
			NSTimer.CreateRepeatingScheduledTimer(TimeSpan.FromSeconds(1), ()  
=> Update());
		}

The difference is chaining the base() call, otherwise you never  
connect the managed counterpart to the native, thus your  
setNeedsDisplay wasn't going to the real UIView.  Making this change  
and your project behaves as expected?

-g
