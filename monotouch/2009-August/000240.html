Terry,

To get HttpWebRequest working, the work around at the moment is to add  
the -nolink additional argument to the iPhone build. In MonoDevelop,  
Right click the project and select options, then select iPhone Build  
and in the "additional mtouch arguments" just add in -nolink.

Hope this helps,

ChrisNTR.
