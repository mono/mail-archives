Word to the wise for people who haven't create an iPhone Developer account:
Unless you absolutely MUST set it up as a corporate account from the get go,
create an in individual account first which can later be converted to a
corporate account by calling Apple Developer Support at a later date. If you
choose to go the corporate account route first you're looking at several
weeks, at least, before your account is ready where as the individual
account is setup and ready to go within a few hours of paying the $99.
