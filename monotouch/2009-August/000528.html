>From apple:

Tab bar items are configured through their corresponding view controller. To
associate a tab bar item with a view controller, create a new instance of
the UITabBarItem<http://developer.apple.com/IPhone/library/documentation/UIKit/Reference/UITabBarItem_Class/Reference/Reference.html#//apple_ref/occ/cl/UITabBarItem>class,
configure it appropriately for the view controller, and assign it to
the view controller’s
tabBarItem<http://developer.apple.com/IPhone/library/documentation/UIKit/Reference/UIViewController_Class/Reference/Reference.html#//apple_ref/occ/instp/UIViewController/tabBarItem>property.
