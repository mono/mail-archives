Following up my own email...

For those of you having trouble with crashes on the device (but no  
problems on the simulator), I highly recommend using the Xcode  
Organizer application while the device is plugged in. I was able to  
diagnose & fix my crashing problem pretty quickly using  
Console.WriteLine() and watching the Organizer output window.

If you open up Xcode, Organizer is available under the Window menu.

It's pretty handy for taking screenshots of the device as well...


Mark
