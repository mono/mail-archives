Hi,

You have to "Provision" the device. That means signing up for Apple's $99/year developer license, and creating certificates for yourself & application, which you can then use to enable development on that iPhone.

http://developer.apple.com/iPhone/program/

Word to the wise for people who haven't create an iPhone Developer account: Unless you absolutely MUST set it up as a corporate account from the get go, create an in individual account first which can later be converted to a corporate account by calling Apple Developer Support at a later date. If you choose to go the corporate account route first you're looking at several weeks, at least, before your account is ready where as the individual account is setup and ready to go within a few hours of paying the $99.
