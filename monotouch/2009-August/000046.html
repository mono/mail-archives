You do not have to use Interface Builder if you feel comfortable enough
customizing your UI from code yourself.   

In fact, some of the finer details of Apple samples do this (customizing
Cell views for example).   So if you do not mind writing your UI
entirely by hand, you can do it.

Aaron Bockover for instance likes to write all of his UIs by hand, with
no designer.
