On Sun, Aug 9, 2009 at 11:12 PM, karl prosser<klumsy at gmail.com> wrote:
> is there any WPF support in monotouch?

No. Mono itself does not have a WPF implementation, nor are there
plans for one (though there are stubs for much of the API). Mono's
current WPF-compatibility strategy is Silverlight out-of-browser -
Mono's Moonlight can even be embedded in desktop apps, where it has
access to the full framework.

According to the MonoTouch Roadmap
(http://www.mono-project.com/MonoTouch_Roadmap) there may be
Silverlight support in MonoTouch 2.0.
