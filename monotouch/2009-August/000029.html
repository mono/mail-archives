I know it's not MonoTouch's fault but it's highly annoying - I guess Microsoft doesn't offer Visual Studio for OSX so what could we expect?

I do know what options people are trying though:

1) This option I can't recommend because while it works technically, it violates Apple's eula.
http://wiki.osx86project.org/wiki/index.php/Vmware_how_to

2) Many developers use a Dell Mini9 Netbook for $350 which works flawlessly, again I cannot recommend violating the eula.  Besides, this uses an Intel *Atom* cpu which is single core cpu at 1.6Ghz and slow.  How would you like to develop at that speed and with a 9 inch screen?  

3) The Mac-Mini is plenty powerful at around $600.  But you need an expensive KVM DVI switch, or a keyboard and monitor.  There is no RDP (remote desktop) for Windows->OSX and VNC is not nearly as good as RDP.  Finally it's not easily mobile to take it anywhere.

4) Cheapest laptop is the 13" macbook at ~$1000+tax.  Perfect solution - Everyone have an extra grand laying around these days?

5) Used Apple hardware from EBay - Not sure what the oldest thing you could get away with and not drive yourself crazy with slowness, but I'd like to hear if anyone has opinions.
