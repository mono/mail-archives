On Sat, 2009-08-29 at 03:06 +0000, Jonathan Pryor wrote:
> Changes in Beta 7
>       * Removed the OpenTK.Graphics.ES11.GL.LoadAll() and
>         OpenTK.Graphics.ES20.GL.LoadAll() methods. Now to initialize the
>         GL types you must do one of two things: 
>               * Inherit from OpenTK.Platform.iPhoneOS.iPhoneOSGameView,
>                 and call either iPhoneOSGameView.CreateSurface() or

My apologies, it's iPhoneOSGameView.CreateFrameBuffer(),
not .CreateSurface().

>               * Call OpenTK.Platform.Utilities.CreateGraphicsContext()
>                 before making any GL calls.

We discovered that you may want to use Utilities.CreateGraphicsContext()
even if you use iPhoneOSGameView.  GLPaint-GV [0] (the sample that uses
iPhoneOSGameView) wouldn't support "shake-to-clear" until we added a
CreateGraphicsContext() call to Main.  We don't know why this is needed,
but it shouldn't cause any problems either.
