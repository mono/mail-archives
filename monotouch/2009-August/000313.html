NSTimer should have always been working, we've added a few new methods  
for cross threading tho, namely:

NSObject.PerformSelector (Selector selector, NSObject obj, float delay);
NSObject.InvokeOnMainThread (Selector sel, NSObject obj, bool wait);
NSObject.InvokeOnMainThread (Selector sel, NSObject obj);
NSObject.InvokeOnMainThread (NSAction action);
