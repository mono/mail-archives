I thought it worth pointing out the fact that the protobuf-net[1] project
headed up by Marc Gravel presents an interesting opportunity to gain
significant performance gains for applications that do a lot of external web
service calls which by their very nature require significant amounts of
serialization and de-serialization of data being passed back and forth
between the client and
server. Based on some work I did in the early days of the protobuf-net
project[2]
with getting things to work with both Silverlight and
Mono, something Marc has made a continuos effort to ensure continues
to work with each new release, I'm fairly confident
that with minimal effort protobuf support to MonoTouch-based apps can finds
its way into our developers toolbag.  I'll contact Marc to see what his
thoughts are, but unless he's of the belief that there's more to it is
immediately obviously, I'll spend some time this week working to build out
MonoTouch specific assemblies for inclusion in your iPhone
apps.  Will keep you all updated on progress.
[1] http://code.google.com/p/protobuf-net
[2] http://code.google.com/p/protobuf-net/#Mono
