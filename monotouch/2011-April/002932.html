The PNGs are probably 'optimized' rather than corrupt.

Google for pngcrush to read about the concept, or this was the first article
i found
http://iphonedevelopment.blogspot.com/2008/10/iphone-optimized-pngs.html

If you watch the build-output window closely you'll see the commands to
'optimize' your PNGs being emitted during compilation/deployment.
