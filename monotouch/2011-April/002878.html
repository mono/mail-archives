> 1: How to let the user scroll the page left/right with their finger.

You could use a UIScrollView which takes care of this.

The WWDC talk on UIScrollView covers how to do this.   This is if you
want the PDF rendering to behave like the Photos app.

I am not sure that this is what you want, chances are you would like a
funky animation.

> 2: How to bring in the next page as they scroll.

If you roll your own (and not use UIScrollView), I would have 2 views
currentPage and nextPage.   When the user swipes (use a
UIGestureRecognizer) you render the nextPage, which has
X=Screen.Bounds.X and Y=0, then you use UIView's animation transaction
to animate both the currentPage.X position to -Screen.Bounds.X and the
nextPage.x to 0.   Then iOS takes care of the rest.
