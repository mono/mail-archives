A question: I don't know why but I don't need to copy provision file. Mono
compiles it automatically inside the application and I just put plist and
ipa on server.

So if it's Enteprise, it must be inside something with user and password
because it can be downloaded for anyone...
And in an AdHoc, the application will be installed just on devices
authorized on Developer Center at apple.com.
