My understanding from Apple's
docs<http://developer.apple.com/library/ios/#documentation/uikit/reference/UIViewController_Class/Reference/Reference.html#//apple_ref/doc/uid/TP40006926-CH3-SW57>is
that reorientation doesn't necessarily happen by magic:

"If the autoresizing properties of your views are configured correctly, that
may be all you have to do. However, the UIViewController class provides
additional hooks for you to implement additional behaviors as needed."

Maybe you need to look into UIView.AutoresizesSubviews and
UIView.AutoresizingMask?
