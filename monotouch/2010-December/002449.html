Yes, put this in the reviewers notes. All of my apps need logins, so
I've made various test accounts and shared them with apple - tho one
is a twitter "client", so I just said "any valid twitter account will

Cycle Scheme to test my apps, but I think Twitter is a fair one to ask
them to use their own!

Be fairly specific with the reviewers notes - no need to treat them
like idiots, but being clear helps a lot.
