The parser does some of the boring work for you, but if you do not
understand the underlying Objective-C foundation, you are bound to:

(a) become very frustrated
(b) likely screw up the binding
(c) Get a piss-poor experience
(d) Tell the world that xxx sucks for no good reason.

I would rather help people learn the ins and outs of binding that ending up
with angry developers.

In some way, we have been doing that on the mono-osx list, where I have been
coaching guys that contribute to our open source MonoMac/MacCore projects
(MacCore is shared with MonoTouch).
