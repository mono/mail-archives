Any current Mac with 4GB RAM is definitely fine. I am sure that the 2GB ones
will handle MonoDevelop as well, but you want to be able to multi-task
during development. I have the 13-inch Macbook Pro, and it has been working
great. I agree with the previous poster that you may want a bigger screen if
you are used to a big screen or double screens during development.
