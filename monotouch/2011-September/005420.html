As a user:
Notifications ROCK.
Twitter integration: couldn't care less.
Camera off the lock screen: GOLD.
iMessage: I look forward to telling my carrier to stick it's free,
unlimited SMS's .... oh wait. it's free and unlimited. Either way:
push is more reliable here than SMS.
Wifi sync: yay!

As a developer:
New stuff is always good, but I have users who are still on 3.1.3.
Seriously. Time to cut them off tho - it'll be 4.2 or better shortly.
iCloud: nice, but I already sync to a cloud (Dropbox, via DropNet and RestSharp)

I only touch the surface of iOS 3, let alone 4 or 5, with a few minor
exceptions.

Now, MonoTouch 5 with full xcode4.2 and iOS5 tools support. Now THAT
I'm looking forward to.
