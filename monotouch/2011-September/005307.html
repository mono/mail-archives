Ensure FinishedLaunching() returns asap when your application loads. Spawn
off a new thread and do your init in that, or use this.InvokeInMainThread()
to have the FinishedLaunching() method return quicker.

Also, as jowi mentioned, placing a default.png (and a Default at 2x.png for
retina displays) in your project and mark as content will cause them to be
displayed, masking the startup time.
