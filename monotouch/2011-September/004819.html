if you're interested, the highlights (nothing earth shattering)

   - MT.Dialog will be rolled into the core once Miguel has the API nailed
   down
   - they want to have MT.Dialog available in Android too
   - they have plans to include more value-adds like MT.Dialog in the
   future, but no specifics
   - they are very heads down right now working on code, expect to see more
   from the team in terms of blogs, etc once they're past that hump
   - they're also interested in doing more to build and grow the community
   (ie, us)
   - they are working off a prioritized list from the survey they did
   earlier this year, trying to get as much done as possible
   - MD is 50% off with MT purchase in Sept (http://blog.xamarin.com/)
