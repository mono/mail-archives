Hi John,

Not really an excuse, but allow me to explain what is happening:

MonoDevelop 2.6 (and 2.4?) use versioned config directories allowing them to
be parallel installable. This has its good points (allow you to try out beta
versions w/o risk of breaking your stable version of MD), but it also has
its drawbacks (*cough* like the one you are experiencing).

The good news, however, is that your keybindings have not been lost. You
should be able to find MonoDevelop 2.4's keybindings under
~/.config/MonoDevelop/KeyBindings.xml or something like that.

MonoDevelop 2.6 saves them under a more MacOS-friendly location:
~/Library/Preferences/MonoDevelop-2.6/KeyBindings/Custom.mac-kb.xml

(actually, I'm going based off my memory, which might not be 100% accurate -
if these locations aren't correct, let me know and when I'm at the office in
~1 hour, I can look up the actual paths for you).

That said, I think MD should probably offer to migrate your keybindings. As
it is right now, this problem will happen again for MonoDevelop 2.8 and
clearly this is a Bad Thing(tm).

A bug should probably be filed for this at http://bugzilla.xamarin.com

Hope that helps,

Jeff
