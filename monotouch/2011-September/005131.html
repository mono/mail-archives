There's this: http://gamesfromwithin.com/remixing-opengl-and-uikit

which is an objc/C sample of doing it.  It's still not the most efficient
means of handling the problem (since It creates a bitmap, renders to the
bitmap, and then gives *that* to opengl - not sure if there's a way to
directly poke at the UIImage's pixel data in order to give it to opengl),
but it looks like it'll work, and all the apis used exist in monotouch.
