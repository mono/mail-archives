I'm not sure it's accurate to say it won't install/activate on
virtualized hardware. It's rather that virtualized instances often
doesn't report all hardware accurately/correctly, particularly if
you're using a 3rd party EFI layer. For what it's worth, 4.0.3
installs and activates on VMWare 7.x on PC if you use Donk's boot ISO
for EFI (google it) but the popular darwin_snow.iso is incomplete and
is enough to boot OSX, but not enough to allow MT to activate.
