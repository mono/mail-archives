Even Shorter answer (longer answer and shorter answer were both bounced by
the MT mailing list).

extra arguments:  -v -v -v -gcc_flags "-L${ProjectDir}/lib
-lSDOCertification
 -framework CFNetwork -framework MobileCoreServices -framework CoreTelephony
-force_load ${ProjectDir}/lib/OpenAPI.a"

Make a class variable for the MyOpenApiDelegate object.  del being a class
variable.
      del = new MyOpenApiDelegate();
  openApi.StartOA(del);

In the binding cs file, OpenApiDelegate is the [Model].
