This generally means you had a compiler crash.  Add "-v -v -v" to Extra Arguments and you should see the issue.

Additionally you're on a old version of the iOS SDK (4.1) that apple doesn't support anymore, you should update to the latest stable iOS SDK, and MonoTouch 3.2.4
