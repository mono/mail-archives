Did our first build for distribution last night and ran into the error
above. Basically, the Application Loader was complaining that the icons I'd
specified in MonoDevelop under Build / iPhone Application / Icons weren't
there, which they most definitely were.

My workaround was to copy the icons to their default names (Icon.png,
Icon at 2x.png, etc.) at the root of the project, re-specify them in
MonoDevelop so that the generated Info.plist entries would point to the
copied versions, and rebuild. That seemed to do the trick, at least as far
as the App Loader was concerned.

Is this a known issue, or should I file a MonoDevelop bug?
