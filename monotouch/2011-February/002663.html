You could use TestFlight. They have an upload API you could integrate in
your build process (http://www.testflightapp.com/api/doc/ - might only be
accessible for users).

Once you're set up, your boss will receive an email every time there's a new
build available (so you might not want to upload it every time).

I've been using it for a while and I think it's great.
