That iTunes error usually means MinimumOSVersion is missing.

The stock Universal template from MD is missing it so you just need to add
it to your Info.plist.

Here is a working MonoTouch universal Info.plist as a reference:

<?xml version="1.0" encoding="UTF-8"?>
<!DOCTYPE plist PUBLIC "-//Apple//DTD PLIST 1.0//EN" "
http://www.apple.com/DTDs/PropertyList-1.0.dtd">
<plist version="1.0">
<dict>
<key>UIStatusBarStyle</key>
<string>UIStatusBarStyleBlackOpaque</string>
<key>UIAppFonts</key>
<array>
<string>lcars.ttf</string>
</array>
<key>UIPrerenderedIcon</key>
<true/>
<key>MinimumOSVersion</key>
<string>3.1</string>
<key>UILaunchImageFile~ipad</key>
<string>DefaultIPad.png</string>
<key>CFBundleDisplayName~ipad</key>
<string>LCARS Reader</string>
<key>UISupportedInterfaceOrientations</key>
<array>
<string>UIInterfaceOrientationPortrait</string>
</array>
<key>UISupportedInterfaceOrientations~ipad</key>
<array>
<string>UIInterfaceOrientationLandscapeLeft</string>
<string>UIInterfaceOrientationLandscapeRight</string>
<string>UIInterfaceOrientationPortrait</string>
<string>UIInterfaceOrientationPortraitUpsideDown</string>
</array>
<key>CFBundleIconFiles</key>
<array>
<string>Icon-Small-50.png</string>
<string>Icon-Small.png</string>
<string>Icon.png</string>
<string>IconIPad.png</string>
</array>
</dict>
</plist>
