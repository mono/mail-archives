Hello Richard,

This is how you create an IPA with Terminal:

/usr/bin/xcrun -sdk iphoneos PackageApplication -v "APP_PATH.app" -o
"IPA_OUTPUT_PATH.ipa" --sign "DEVELOPER_OR_BUSINESS_NAME_ON_SIGNATURE"
--embed "PATH_TO_MOBILE_PROVISION.mobileprovision"

Mikkel
