2011/1/15 Miguel de Icaza <miguel at novell.com>

> > - Is it possible to implement the functions of normal PDF viewer like
> > showing, zooming, panning a PDF document and selecting text and
> annotating
> > on it with MonoTouch?
>
> There are APIs to scan PDF documents, and render individual pages into
> a graphics context.  It is up to you to do work at that point.
>
> > - Does MonoTouch support GPU acceleration on iOS or OpenGL well?
>
> You can invoke OpenGL methods and roll your own.
>
> Or you can use the UIKit/CoreAnimation APIs and get the same level of
> UI hardware acceleration that Objective-C apps get.
>
> > - Does MonoTouch support manipulating Zip files or does related 3rd party
> > components exist?
>
> There are third party libraries, but not bundled by default.
>
> > - Which encryption methods are supported at MonoTouch? For compliance to
> > Silverlight, supporting AES is essential.
>
> The list is quite long, AES is supported, but here is the full namespace:
> http://go-mono.com/docs/monodoc.ashx?link=N%3aSystem.Security.Cryptography
>
> > - I know that MT does not provide MultiscaleImage yet. If MT development
> > team has a plan to implement it, when will it be available?
>
> Multiscale means too many different things, so you need to be more
> specific.
>
> The iOS 4.x multi-scale image loading and rendering is supported, I am
> not sure where you get the feeling that it is not.
>
> The CoreAnimation-based tiled image loading is also supported, and has
> for a year.
>
> Miguel
