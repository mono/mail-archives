It seems as though when MT has a stack overflow, it crashes the app with
"exited abnormally with signal 11: Segmentation fault", and no stack trace
or other output. (Thought I'd get this into the public record so it's easier
to google.)

I see in the Mono readme at https://github.com/mono/mono that there's an
experimental flag --with-sigaltstack that tries to catch these errors and
turn them into exceptions, but it's annotated

Experimental: Use at your own risk, it is known to cause problems with
garbage collection and is hard to reproduce those bugs


So, two questions: (1) does this work with MT? and (2) if it does, is it
worth using?
