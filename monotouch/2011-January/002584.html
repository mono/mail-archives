El Jan 12, 2011, a las 3:23 AM, Neil Kennedy <neilkennedy at live.ie> escribió:

As far as topics are concerned I suppose you should just cover the basics
like getting a decent RSS app up and running. So things like UIView,
UITableView and downloading RSS files using LINQ. UIScrollView is also a
good one to cover as a more intermediate topic.

Craig Dunn's blog is an excellent resource. He has been covering some
advanced stuff in the past while but if you search back a few months you'll
find beginner tutorials that are very detailed and helpful.
http://conceptdev.blogspot.com/

Wally McClure has a great MonoTouch book that I've also used. It shows some
of the differences between Objective-C and C# iPhone development and goes
through some great tutorials. Its on amazon at
http://www.amazon.com/Professional-iPhone-Programming-MonoTouch-Programmer/dp/047063782X/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&qid=1294823761&sr=8-1

<http://www.amazon.com/Professional-iPhone-Programming-MonoTouch-Programmer/dp/047063782X/ref=sr_1_1?ie=UTF8&qid=1294823761&sr=8-1>
