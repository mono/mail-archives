Ah, I see what he means.   The Silverlight Multiscale image.

MonoTouch does not have any of Silverlight's UI APIs at this point,
and we do not have plans currently to bring those in.

Like Mike Bluestein said, you can use CATiledLayer to implement your
own MultiScaleImage, but it is a low-level API, so you would have to
do some of the work yourself to do network image loading.
