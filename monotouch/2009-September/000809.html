Hey Jose,

That's great, it's really coming along nicely!!!

I've seen a few people complaining about OpenAL on the iPhone that the stack
is buggy.  I decided to avoid it and go with AudioQueue instead which works
fine.  AudioUnits might be another way to go since I believe that allows
additional features like echo and speed control but I haven't tried it.
Take a look at the SpeakHere sample from the iPhone SDK on how to use
AudioQueue.

One thing you'll need to watch out for is the iPhone uses a hardware CODEC
for MP3 and AAC audio.  The problem is (at least on the 3G) it isn't
possible to play two MP3 files simultaneously since you need exclusive
access to the hardware codec.  Currently, the way I am doing it I use MP3 or
AAC for the background music and .WAV for short sound effects that are
layered on top.

cheers,
-chris
