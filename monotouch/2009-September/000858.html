> Hi,
>
> What is the difference in functionality between Person vs Enterprise 
> Edition?
I would think that:

* A MonoTouch Personal Edition license is owned by an individual.

* A MonoTouch Enterprise Edition license is owned by a legal entity for 
use by one developer at any given time,

So a "personal license" is to a single named developer, "enterprise" is 
any developer in a named organisation. This would be the most obvious 
"daily" difference at least.
