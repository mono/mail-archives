The 10MB limit isn't for all applications.  It's for downloading
applications over the 3G network.  You can download larger apps by using
Wifi or by purchasing through iTunes on your computer.

For example, you won't be able to purchase & install the Tom-Tom app
over 3G (far larger than 10MB), but you can over wifi.
