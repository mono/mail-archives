Have you seen this work in code somewhere?  I highly doubt you can just
concat these strings together and expect it to output an image plus some
text.  In essence you are calling UIImage.ToString() which will return
the type, hence why you see Monotouch.UIKit.Image.  

 

I would take a look at Simon's post on making a custom table cell...
http://simon.nureality.ca/?p=91 .  Instead of just labels, put an image
in the custom cell xib.
