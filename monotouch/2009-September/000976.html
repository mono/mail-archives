Well look in the archives, this was all discussed before the prices
were even officially announced (thread called "Commercial pricing?"),

"We might consider a Learning Edition, but right now it is a bit late to

Perhaps.

> Giving the Beta Team the price list on Friday, seeing the flurry of concern

Did you read Joseph's "Pricing and Launch Update" email?  The very
first paragraph said:

"I would like to thank everyone for the feedback that you have provided,
and would like to let you know that we understand that you have some
very valid concerns.  We will be taking steps in the next weeks to address
many of these, some actions will take longer than others, but we definitely
want to do what we can today."

and later:

"You've given us some
great suggestions regarding limited versions and trials, which we've
already been considering, and we will make a determination about the
best solution for hobby developers in the very near future."

I would think that encouraging words such as these from people who
have been very forthright in their communication with the beta testers
would help you to be a bit more patient.

I can't help but feel that some people are spending a lot more time
complaining (repeating things that have already been said) and not
much time reading.
