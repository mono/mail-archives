Hello,

> What about single-employee companies? Do they really fall under the
> 'enterprise' license?

The only reason you might want an enterprise license is:

	* You want to use the Enterprise Deployment option
	  for the iPhone (ie, you are a large, Fortune 1000
	  company and you want to roll out an in-house apps
	  to your own employees).

	* You are a company that has significant developer
	  rotation and you need the license to be reusable for
	  other folks.

	  For example, a bank that reassigns developers to
	  different workloads during the year.

Basically it was a way of separating a very large development shop from
small ISVs and regular developers.

But if you do not need those features, you should get the personal
edition.
