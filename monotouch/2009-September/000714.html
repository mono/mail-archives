     You are correct that they are old APIs and they are not related  
to MonoTouch.

     Jonathan started writing a tutorial on how to bind Objective-C  
APIs, but as far as I know it is being authored on the upcoming new  
web site for MonoTouch, and I do not even remember the url for it.

      We internally use a tool that helps us write the bindings, but  
it is difficult to use for arbitrary new bindings, which is why we  
have not released it to the public.
