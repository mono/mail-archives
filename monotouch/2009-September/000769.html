I'll pay anything up to $1,000 for the pro product.  Actually I'll pay more
than that but you're gonna have to put me on a payment plan :)

It's worth it to us because without MonoTouch we'd be dead in the water.  I
hate Objective-C and the time we've already saved is worth way more than
$1,000.

Having said that, I do think $1,000 is a bit steep right now because the
product is still somewhat immature and the word still needs to get out to a
broader audience that are not early adopters.  Better to price it low for
now to get a ton of users to try it.  Then, come in later down the road with
the Pro version that features debugging at a Pro price point.

The idea of the learning edition that only runs on the simulator is a nice
idea!

-chris
