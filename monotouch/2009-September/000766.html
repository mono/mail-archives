Let me weigh in on the pricing here.  I'm not sure if anyone from the
pricing group is listening, but just in case... It currently costs $100 to
develop for the iPhone on ObjC + a Mac if you don't have one.  Since you
need an IPhone developer account and a Mac to develop in MonoTouch, the
pricing for MonoTouch is 100% incremental.  I think developers would
get a productivity boost from MonoTouch, but anything in the $2,000 range
would be a tough sell even for me and I'm pretty darn excited about
MonoTouch.

I think a few hundred bucks makes great sense -- let's say $199 or $299.
Look at the value you get from Microsoft for  $299 (VS2008 Standard)  for
$1,200 you can get VS2008 Professional + a full year of MSDN professional.
Don't get crazy with the pricing or you will end up with a little niche
product that goes nowhere -- and makes no real money.

- Ethan
