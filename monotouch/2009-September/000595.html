Thanks Craig.  Your method worked, but there is a bug in MT where a
downloaded file is not overwritten.

Your solution led me to the following solution:

            NSData imgData = NSData.FromUrl(new NSUrl(ul));
            imgPlayer.Image = UIImage.LoadFromData(imgData);
