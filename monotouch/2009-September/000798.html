>>What is Premultiplied Alpha

Here is the cliff notes version:

·       Premultiplied alpha is just one particular way to store transparency information for an image.

·       Never work premultiplied alpha unless you are required to (see next point).

·       You will only ever be required to if an API or application says so, or in certain situations to improve performance.

·       "Straight alpha" is a much simpler and intuitive concept for people who don't have or need a lot of graphics knowledge.
