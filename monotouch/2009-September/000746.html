> I also have a related question to ask - what is the plan for

To solve this particular issue, I have made a couple of changes in the  
API:

* Now there is an AddAnnotationObject (NSObject obj) and  
AddAnotationObjects (NSObject [] objs) which allow you to provide your  
own implementation that uses [Export] attributes manually.

* I added a new set of convenience overloads: AddAnnotation and  
AddAnnotations can now also take MKPlacemark objects.
