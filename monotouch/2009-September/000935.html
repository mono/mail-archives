The concerns I'm hearing about MT seem to boil down to these:

  1) The maturity or readiness of the code to be a high quality release (release early/often should mean less features not less quality for a .0 build).

  2) Sustainability - Will it build a large and productive dev community? (even MS/Google need this).  How much per year to stay compatible with the iPhone SDK?

  3) Is the price worth it?


___ I'd to reiterate my suggestion that MT be available with a simulator only license for free, forever, with no ability to deploy to the device or app store. ___

This could put at least a good dent in all three arguments above, and should not reduce (long term) the reward to Novell for investing in a new product.
