Hmmm, what wrong with using DataContract/DataMember to define DTO's?
it's opt-in, pretty simple and is the recommended way to define DTO's
in WCF (when you have control over the schema), its supported (MS even
rewrote their serializers for it in) Silverlight etc.

Its simple, and purpose-specific design (for DTO's) means you can
re-use the same POCO's for transferring XML(+SOAP), JSON, protobuff
(http://code.google.com/p/protobuf-net/) etc.

I think it should be the first choice to serialize objects over the wire.
