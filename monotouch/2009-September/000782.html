I hear you. I'd be one of the ones who are like "just let me pay you money
so i can publish on the appstore already". But I also am aware of the
backlash of paying "traditional" customers who would be like "I paid money
for a RTM software and the docs aren't complete, and there was X , Y and Z
bug that really aren't forgivable in a RTM product of this category, and the
"I build my app, published it, and the next time an official release build
came out, my app won't compile, or crashes against it and it took me 2 weeks
to update my app, yada yada". I know that certian graces afforded to
OpenSource projects aren't there with many paying customers, and peripheral
tools and software have less stringent expectations than something as "Core"
as monotouch.
