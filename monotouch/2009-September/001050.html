Abe,

I think Mark's point is that if Apple introduce a native GC to the iphone,
we will have two GCs layered on top of each other. This would clearly be a
nightmare for memory management as once MT's GC releases an object, we'd
have to wait then for the native GC to collect it, effectively doubling the
reclamation time assuming all other things being equal.

-Oisin
