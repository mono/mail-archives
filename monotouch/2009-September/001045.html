Hi all,

I'm hoping someone can give me a bit of insight as to what may happen  
with MonoTouch if/when Apple starts supporting garbage collected ObjC  
on the iPhone OS, as they do in recent releases of the desktop Mac OS.

I ask because Monobjc, the Mono/ObjC bridge for the desktop Mac OS,  
requires use of retain/release reference counting memory management  
when calling into the Cocoa APIs from C#:
http://www.monobjc.net/index.php?page=memory-management
I also remember reading at some point (although I can't find it  
now...) that Monobjc does not support the 10.5+ Cocoa APIs that  
utilise ObjC garbage collection - something about having to  
synchronise the 2 garbage collectors, IIRC.

Will this be a similar problem for MonoTouch if Apple starts  
supporting this in the iPhone OS?

Let me know if I'm not understanding things correctly...
