I still think a 'crippled' version would be best to give away free.  Just
let us run in the simulator and on device without code signing for the
AppStore or AdHoc deployment.  This way someone could use the product,
basically build their app, and when they're ready, they have to go 'buy' it.
 So, for some, not sure if they'll actually build an app from start to end,
this would offer them the tools to do it and be able to wait and pay until
they know they will succeed with completing the app.
You'd hugely increase the the following from the Community here too.  I have
no problems paying for the product, but I know I wouldn't normally buy it if
I didn't know anything about using it before hand, or if I'd be able to
complete the app I have in my head to build :)

You really don't want to lose that community of developers!
