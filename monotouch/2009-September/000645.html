Hi

I don't know much (if anything) regarding the licensing issues of using code
from the original Xna dll, or regarding getting it past the app review. That
being said, it seems natural that XnaTouch best would benefit from working
with the Mono.Xna code. There's  probably a lot to be said for working with
OpenTK as well, but there can be no doubt that Mono.Xna would provide a
better match.

The monoxna source is still missing major parts of the XNA functionality,
but it is evolving day by day. And we certainly would appreciate the
collaboration.

-lavima
