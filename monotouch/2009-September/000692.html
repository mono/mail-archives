Although, having an GPL version will most likely be self-regulating in
this case as usually people wanting to make money with AppStore
also do not want to open-source their application, so they will be
happy to buy the commercial licence.

Essentially the same business model as other dual-licence wielding
products e.g. QT, db4o, etc.

At the same time it lets other developers who develop free (gpl'd)
apps to do so.
