because of other factors - the OS/distro, GTK# version, GTK+ version,
Mono version, etc. This is an issue on Linux, where we can't control

are only a couple of version of the OS itself, so it's much easier
test it.

> If you can instruct me about how to get usefull crash info from my MD I
> would happily fill bug reports..

You can find the log in ~/.config/MonoDevelop/log

If MonoDevelop is hanging, you can force Mono to dump stack traces of
all threads to the console by sending SIGQUIT to the MD process.

You can do this with "kill -QUIT $pid" where $pid is the pid of the MD
process. The command "killall -QUIT monodevelop" is more convenient,
unless you have other MD processes you don't want to affect, or MD has
hung before renaming itself.
