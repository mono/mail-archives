You should take advantage of a UINavigationController - it is resposible for
keeping the "back stack" of pages, plus is able to control a UINavigatoinBar
to display that info.
You need to assign a delegate to the table view that will handle the row
selected event. When a row is selected, push a new view controller onto the
UINavigationController.
