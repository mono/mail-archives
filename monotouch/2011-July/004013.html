Difficult situation. If it helps, I have succesfully compiled a MonoTouch app with my client's certificates. My MT license is Personal/Professional, the client did not have an MT license, only iOS developer enrollment. The app is not yet ready for the app store, but I have compiled for ad-hoc and they install it on their devices without problems.

I do not know what happens in the case of Enterprise distribution. My guess is that you must have the MT license in order to compile the appropriate certificates and your client must be enrolled for enterprise distribution with Apple. Don't count on this though...
