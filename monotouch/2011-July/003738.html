There is a lot of recent history you should be aware of...

Novell was recently acquired by Attachmate.  Attachmate fired all of the
Mono developers.  Attachmate is continuing to sell MonoTouch and MonoDroid,
but has given no indication that they will provide support or continue to
release new versions of the toolkit.  Nobody in the MT community has been
able to receive any support, or obtain any clear answers to our questions,
despite repeated attempts to contact Attachmate.

The good news is that most of the fired Mono team have created their own
company, Xamarin, and are working on a completely new set of cross-platform
mobile tools based on C#.  They should be backward compatible with existing
MT/MD code.  We're hoping for a preview of their new product by the end of
the summer.

http://tirania.org/blog/archive/2011/May-16.html
