Hi Greg,

A tip: If you remove your app from the device, and redeploy (via debug or
run), your app will automatically be configured with the current
computer's IP address.

Both device and mac should be on the same network or at least be able to
communticate. You may have to check firewalls if youre not on the same
subnet.
