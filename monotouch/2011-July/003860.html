We have successfully used SharpZipLib with MonoTouch (re: the error
referenced on SO, I changed 'DefaultCodePage' to '1252' in ZipConstants.cs
as a hack to get it working - has been live for over a year now with no
known problems... but then it's a closed ecosystem: we only open zips we've
created). From memory, I just downloaded it, opened in MonoDevelop and it
kinda works (except for that codepage issue).
The zips we are working with range from 50 to 200Mb in size (!)
