I think you also need to change the Window size (and incidentally, anything
in the top-left quarter of the screen probably will respond to touch
already).

eg in your AppDelegate you could do something like this:

window.Frame = UIScreen.MainScreen.Bounds;


There might be something you can do in InterfaceBuilder if that's how you
are working, but I don't use it....

HTH
