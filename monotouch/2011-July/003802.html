Hi Ray,

You might want to have a look at my fork of MT Dialog - I have added 
these properties to reflect.cs and elements.cs.

https://github.com/chrisbranson/MonoTouch.Dialog

Example usage:

[Section ("Server")]
         [Caption ("Address"), Entry ("Enter your server address", 
UIKeyboardType.Url, UITextAutocapitalizationType.None, 
UITextAutocorrectionType.No)]
         public string ServerAddress;

My fork is up to date with Miguel's.
