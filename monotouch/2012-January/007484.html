Hi,

Thanks for this, I've fixed it and it will be included in 5.0.6 and 5.1.3.

As for a workaround, you can try this:

MonoTouch.ObjCRuntime.Messaging.void_objc_msgSend_IntPtr (button.Handle,
new MonoTouch.ObjCRuntime.Selector ("setTintColor:"), IntPtr.Zero);

Rolf
