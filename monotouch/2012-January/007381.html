Hi Richard,

The NPOI project ( http://code.google.com/p/npoi/ ) is working on porting
the rest (e.g. Word and PowerPoint portions) of the Apache Foundation's POI
library over to .NET. They've already gotten the Excel bits ported.

I've been following this project because what I'd like to do is port it to
MonoTouch in my spare and provide it as part of our offerings, but I

I haven't looked over their progress since November or so, so they may
actually have docx support now - I'm not sure.

Hope that helps,
