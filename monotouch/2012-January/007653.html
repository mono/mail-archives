I would expect that it's resizing the entry box incorrectly.

you may want to put a breakpoint in ComputeEntryPosition

https://github.com/migueldeicaza/MonoTouch.Dialog/blob/master/MonoTouch.Dialog/Elements.cs#L1424-1445

and see what it's throwing out for the entry instance's size. I
suspect thats your problem - it's not finding a label, so it's making
the whole entry field way way too big.

There is a comment in there about setting it to null, too, so maybe
try that rather than a blank string.
