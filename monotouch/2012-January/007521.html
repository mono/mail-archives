Hi,

You can also add the [Adopts (NSStreamDelegate)] on
TestMonoTouchViewController class (but you'll also have to add Export
attributes on all members you want implemented of the NSStreamDelegate
protocol).

Here is an example of how to use the AdoptsAttribute:
https://github.com/xamarin/monotouch-samples/blob/master/SimpleTextInput/EditableCoreTextView.cs

Rolf
