Hi,

It looks like your running out of a certain type of trampolines.

An explanation of the problem and the solution can be found here:
http://docs.xamarin.com/ios/troubleshooting#Ran_out_of_trampolines_of_type_0

The crash report doesn't say which trampoline version you ran out of, but
this information should be printed to the iOS Device Log in MonoDevelop.

I hope this helps,
Rolf
