If you're interested in cloud-based private repository hosting - either Git
or Mercurial - https://bitbucket.org/ is free for unlimited private
repositories. It's a pretty good service and is worth it if you're not
willing to make the jump to paid hosting on GitHub.

It's also worth checking out http://www.repositoryhosting.com/ for low-cost
SVN/Git/Mercurial hosting.
