Hi,

It looks like a linker issue (members are only accessed through reflection,
so the linker doesn't realize they're used). My guess would be that you
need to put a [Preserve] attribute somewhere.

In any case I suggest filing a bug with a compilable test case, and we'll
have a look at it.
