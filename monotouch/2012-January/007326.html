Hi Xy,

It looks like perhaps you are using symbols from the Security framework but
not referencing it in the LinkWith attribute.

Try changing your LinkWith attribute in your AssemblyInfo.cs to:

[assembly: LinkWith ("libSFHFKeychainUtils.a", LinkTarget.Simulator |
LinkTarget.ArmV6 | LinkTarget.ArmV7, ForceLoad = true, Frameworks =
"Security")]


Hope that helps!

Jeff
