Another limitation is that Apple requires that binaries be statically
linked, and I doubt that this restriction has been lifted.  Without
dynamic linking, a FOSS version of mono for iPhone is ~useless, as you
can't easily follow the LGPL (unless you also shipped your .o files to
your users so they could re-link your app against an updated libmono.a,
but I'm not entirely sure how well that would work in practice).
