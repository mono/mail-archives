> Sounds like you might have the same problem I was just asking about.
> Strange as it seems, while views size themselves based on the environment,
> the window does not.  By default it's always phone size.  As a result
> touches do not register except in the upper left.  You need to tell the
> window what size to be.  In FinishedLaunching:
> 
> window.Frame = UIScreen.MainScreen.Bounds;
This worked and saved my life.
