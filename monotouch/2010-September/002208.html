Hey Miguel,

I'm sorry but I just don't think that argument flies. What if Adobe said
that about Photoshop getting a null ref every five minutes? Their customers
would quite correctly not accept that. Buggy software that end users use and
effectively become the beta testers for is fine in the world of open source
and actual betas, but commercial software at version 2.4 for the price of
$399 just shouldn't be this buggy.

And yes, I did file the bug related to expanding tree nodes, I filed it
about a year ago. The other bugs I recently encountered in the past several
days, I've not filed them just yet.

And again, I know tone is hard to read in email. I'm not trying to be
hostile or argumentative or anything like that. Like I said I love MonoTouch
and see it as a fantastic way to develop iOS apps.

But as Novell's customer I feel I have the right to a quality product for
the money I spent on it, and I feel I should let other prospective buyers
know what they could be getting into.

Matt
