Also consider this.  The limitations of MD are somewhat mitigated by the fact that you can develop some of your app in VS.  Just how much depends on the app.

I consider it a best practice to make a core assembly that is free of UI dependencies, and then keep the UI dependent assembly as limited as possible to UI concerns.  This has many benefits including a cleaner more testable design and greater potential for reuse.  A fringe benefit is that it let's me use VS, and much more importantly, Resharper more of the time.
