I agree with everything Craig said. Also, it's very lite-weight. The
integrated updating is nice, updates download very quickly, it starts up
almost instantly and doesn't require high end hardware (and if you have high
end hardware all the better). They take the best ideas from many IDEs and
blend them in with their own ideas for a very rich experience.
