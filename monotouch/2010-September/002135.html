I had a similar problem - was my config, or possibly networking.

Check to see you dont have 2 active network connections. then check

  <Property key="MonoTouch.Debugger.HostIP" value="" />

in ~/.config/MonoDevelop/MonoDevelopProperties.xml

make sure it's blank, no an IP address....

I think thats what caused mine - I had an IP in there, but it wasn't
the right one....
