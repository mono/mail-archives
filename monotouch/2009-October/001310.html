I got my deployment working.  I had to:
1.  Update the iPhone sDK to 3.1.
2.  Get a deployment certificate.  All I had previously was a  
development certificate, my mistake there.
3.  I created a provisioning profile and deployed that in the xcode  
organizer.

Now that I have done all of these, MD deployed to my ipod touch and my  
application seems to run just fine over my wifi.
