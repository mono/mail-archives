I've seen that on all versions of the OS - basically, I delete the app from the device and then re-deploy. It happens most often when I change build types - so if the device has a debug build, and I deploy a QA build, it will do this. I have to delete it, and usually I have to Build\Clean All first, then recompile and deploy to device.

This happens to me even on the simulator.

So try delete the app off the 5.0.2 device, do a Build\Clean All, and then recompile and re-deploy.
