Hi Jeff,

The async/await stuff is implemented in Mono 2.11 (which is an unstable
branch). Once that branch stabilizes, it will be released as 2.12 at which
point it will be merged into MonoTouch and Mono4Android (which are
currently based on the stable 2.10 branch).

I don't have any idea on timelines, unfortunately.

Hope that helps,
