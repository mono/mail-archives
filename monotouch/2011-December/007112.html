Hello Shawn,

Jeff is right. The
"splitViewController:shouldHideViewController:inOrientation:" selector
was misplaced, it should have been binded on the delegate [1] and not
on the controller itself.

Sorry for the inconveniance.
Sebastien

[1] http://developer.apple.com/library/IOs/#documentation/UIKit/Reference/UISplitViewControllerDelegate_protocol/Reference/Reference.html
