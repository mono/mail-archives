Hello,

We're working on a port from Windows Phone to iOS of our Flight Simulator
called Infinite Flight. We're using Monotouch and a custom branch of
Monogame with Gles 2.0 support.

We just reached a big milestone and shot a video of the game in action on
an iPad2.
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ArFc3v2NE9c

We've spent a lot of time optimizing our code and there is still work to
do, but the results so far are promising.

You can find our more at our blog:
http://flyingdevstudio.blogspot.com/2011/12/infinite-flight-for-ios-hands-on.html
