Hi Dean,

I'm also an Apple novice and not really a MonoDevelop developer either (I've
just been assigned to try and fix Gtk+ bugs on MacOS X and it just so
happens MonoDevelop is where most people are seeing crashes in Gtk+). The
only place I know of to find crash logs on Apple is in the Console.app which
you can find in Applications/Utilities. In the Console application, you can
find crash reports under:

- DIAGNOSTIC AND USAGE INFORMATION
  - User Diagnostic Reports

Under there will be a bunch of *.crash files.
