On Jul 26, 2011, at 9:14 AM, Wally McClure wrote:
> What types of tools are available for monotouch/droid beyond just using VS/monodevelop for debugging?  While I was at monospace, there was talk about being able to profile.

There was talk of Mono 2.10 having profiling _support_ -- it has the ability to capture GC data, trace the GC heap, get call stat stacks, etc.

However, (1) there's no UI for all this data, and (2) there's currently no easy way to get at the data when it's on Android/etc.

In short, it serves as the foundation for future work.
