Hi,

In general it's not your code directly that causes crashes. If you
don't keep a reference to an object when you should, it will crash
later, after the garbage collector has freed it. In most cases you can
deduce this from the stack traces, even if the frames related to mono
are just showing numbers. In any case we have to see the actual stack
trace to help - attaching them to mails to this list or adding them to
bug reports at bugzilla.xamarin.com (and if possible not only the
stack trace, but the entire crash report).

Regards,
Rolf
