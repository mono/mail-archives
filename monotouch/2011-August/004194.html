The other people *currently* working on MonoTouch (and related technologies)
are:

* Miguel de Icaza (fearless CTO and master of the iOS5 API), but I'm sure
you already know his blog & twitter accounts!

* Chris Toshok (kind of the lead on the project now that Geoff has moved on
to other exciting projects at another company). I don't think Chris has a
blog anymore, but his twitter stream is https://twitter.com/#!/toshok

* Rodrigo Kumpera (master of all things in the core Mono runtime). You can
find his twitter stream at https://twitter.com/#!/kumpera and his blog at
http://www.kumpera.net/blog/

* Sebastien Pouliot (master of Crypto and Linker fu). You can find his
twitter stream at https://twitter.com/#!/spouliot and his blog is at
http://pages.infinit.net/ctech/poupou.html

* Rolf Kvinge (mostly working on the MonoDevelop side of things). He rarely
tweets, but you can follow him at https://twitter.com/#!/rolfkvinge and his
blog is at http://rolfkvinge.blogspot.com/ (he's a quiet guy)

* Chris Hardy (documentation guru). You can find his twitter stream at
https://twitter.com/#!/chrisntr and his blog at
http://weblogs.asp.net/chrishardy/

I think that's everyone...
