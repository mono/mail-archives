Hi joclem,

You're probably referring to the LLVM compilation that MonoTouch 4.*
provides. Miguel mentioned in his blog post of the release -
http://tirania.org/monomac/archive/2011/Apr-06.html that his Tweetstation
application went from 8mb to 6mb, which is quite a big gain.

Sadly I'm not smart enough to know *why* the size is smaller apart from to
say that the LLVM compiler is smarter at compiling than the AOT compiler
that comes by default with MonoTouch.

I'm sure someone can probably pick up and answer with some more technical
details if you're interested.

Hope this helps,

ChrisNTR
