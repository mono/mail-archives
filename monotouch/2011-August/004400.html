What did you install, exactly?

You need to install:

- Xcode 3.2.6
- MonoFramework MDK 2.10.4
- MonoDevelop 2.4.2
- MonoTouch 4.0.4.1
- the MonoTouch plugin for MonoDevelop (install through MonoDevelop's addin
manager)

(Note: if you missed one or installed a different version, no need to
reinstall your OS)

Hope that helps,

Jeff
