Hi Sinaz,

This probably means that some of the fields in your class are being linked
out by the MonoTouch linker. You can add the [Preserve] attribute to the
fields or try setting the "Linker behavior" option to "Don't Link" in the
iPhone Build options.

Let us know if that works around the issue, then we'll know if it's the
problem or not. I'm pretty sure that this will fix things for you and it's
on our radar to find a solution to this issue because it seems a lot of
people run into this.

Jeff
