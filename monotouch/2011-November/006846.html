Hi Alessandro,

The "Invalid Binary" issue has caught me out a couple of times and each
time it has been because I incorrectly enabled iCloud on the Provisioning
Profile.

I don't know if that's your issue in this case (Apple emails you with more
information about the binary rejection), but if you have enabled iCloud on
the Provisioning Profile and you're not using iCloud in your app, I would
recommend logging into the Provisioning Portal, go to App IDs, check your
App is not enabled for iCloud (edit if necessary) and then re-generate the
App Store Provisioning Profile for your app.

I hope this helps!
