Generally, the support from the Xamarin team, and the community at large,
is great.  Several people from Xamarin participate in this list daily, and
also monitor activity for the MonoTouch tag on StackOverflow.com.

That being said, MT is a fairly young platform, and there are rough edges
that you might run into.  MT also doesn't magically free you from having to
understand the underlying platform - you still need to spend time getting
familiar with iOS's quirks and standard way of doing things, even if you
use MT.

Geographically, the MT community is spread all over.  There might be
someone in your neck of the woods, but if not, the magic of the internet
means you can probably find someone to help you that might be on the
opposite side of the planet.

JA
