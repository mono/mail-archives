My last information is that with iOS 5 you actually have less memory
available. It simply consumes more RAM.

The second thing is that I read is that Apple changed the memory warning
behavior: you get one warning and if you don’t react, your app will be
killed. But this one warning will come later than in previous iOS versions,
where it fired level 1, 2 and 3 warnings before killing you.
