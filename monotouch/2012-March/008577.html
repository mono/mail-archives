These steps:Installation

   1.

   Download and run the
vsix-package<https://github.com/downloads/follesoe/VSMonoTouch/VSMonoTouch%201.2.vsix>
from
   the github page.
   2.

   Copy the MonoTouch binaries from your Mac development environment to
   your Visual Studio 2010 development environment. Copy all the files from
   /Developer/MonoTouch/usr/lib/mono/2.1/ on your Mac toC:\Program Files
   (x86)\Reference Assemblies\Microsoft\Framework\.NETFramework\v1.0 on
   your PC.
   3.

   Add a RedistList-folder under your newly created v1.0-folder. Download
   the FrameworkList.xml
file<https://github.com/downloads/follesoe/VSMonoTouch/FrameworkList.xml>
and
   add it to theRedistList-folder.
