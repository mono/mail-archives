That was a fun couple of hours wading through posts.

I got it working. I have no idea why. I'm not even sure why I tried it! :)

In my last post, notice I was building RouteBoostiPhone.app. That was the
problem. I renamed my csproj to be RouteBoost.csproj, which then built
RouteBoost.app. Immediately, codesign returned a valid response and
Application Loader took my submission without any problems. Is this required
to match the Application Name in the Project Options? Was the app name too
long? Do they not like the "iPhone" word in the title of the app file? Not
sure, but it works now, and hopefully it helps someone else.
