Assuming the content doesn't change between app installs (ie, you have
to resubmit to apple to change it) you can build the index offline,
and just write something to query it on the phone. Should be minimal
on the memory.

You could extract the words from the HTML, break them up on space,
remove punctuation etc, then just have a table of words, a join table,
and a table of documents....

easy.
