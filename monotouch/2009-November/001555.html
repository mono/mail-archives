That sounds like a very strange issue, and no-one else has reported
such a thing happening. Can you tell us a little more about your
configuration? Are you perhaps using Apple's Quartz Extreme or Quartz

What do you mean by "installed last version of gtk+"? We only support
the MonoDevelop app bundle from the MonoDevelop website, which is
designed to use the GTK+ that is bundled with Mono.
