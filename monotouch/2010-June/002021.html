AFAIK you are allowed to ask for a review.

And the rest is I guess a question of how you present it.

Something like

>>Please let us know what you think about our app - so that we can make it better....

>>To honor your work we ...PRICE... to avoid missuse ... TWITTERNAME....
should be OK.

 

And I'm with you - to say something like "for positive reviews we offer a price" could be a problem.

But a request for a honest review is for sure OK (a lot of apps have this in their description / on their site).

And offering a price - as long as it is clear that this is not for "payin good reviews" should also be OK
