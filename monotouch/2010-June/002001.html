I don't know all the consequences of this, but I believe one consequence is
that there's no automatic way to transfer files saved in the one app's
sandbox to the other. Your  customers will have to re-enter any data saved
in the sandbox into the new app.

This is the main reason I've decided to go with in-app purchase instead of a
separate app when I publish the premium version of a free app already in the
store.
