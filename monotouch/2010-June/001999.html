Absolutely there's a lot of value!

Just because we have a garbage collector doesn't mean our apps are magically
slim. You must be cognoscente of how much runtime memory your app consumes.
And you have to test how much memory you free when the OS gives you the low
memory warning (if you don't free enough, you get shut down).

You must know how much CPU you consume if you do interactive/realtime apps.

If writing a simulation, then you must know what framerates you're achieving
and which calls are affecting your performance.

Instruments is incredibly useful.
