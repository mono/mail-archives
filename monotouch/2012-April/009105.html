Not sure of the details of the service you are calling but I would assume
that onPapersReceived needs to be inside an InvokeOnMainThread call.

i.e

svc.GetPapers (InvokeOnMainThread(() => onPapersReceived), onErrorReceived);

It may be that your service already does this. If not you need to do it
yourself.
