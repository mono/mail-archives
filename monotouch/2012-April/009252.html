If you're referring to saving pictures to the Camera Roll/Photos app, its
something along the lines of:

MonoTouch.AssetsLibrary.ALAssetsLibrary imageSaver = new
MonoTouch.AssetsLibrary.ALAssetsLibrary();
			
imageSaver.WriteImageToSavedPhotosAlbum(myImage,MonoTouch.AssetsLibrary.ALAssetOrientation.Up,(assetURL,
error) => Console.WriteLine("Save completed"));
