Don't be fooled by the fact that the trial/eval version will work fine on a
Hackintosh system, which may lead you to believe that the registered version
will work as well.  It won't.

I found out the hard way, after buying a license, that the registration
process will fail.  It appears that it hinges on getting a unique hardware
ID from your system which, I'm assuming, works fine on Apple hardware... but
it doesn't work on non-Apple hardware.

So make sure you have a real Mac. ;)
