I've neve experienced this, but there's a copy/paste crash mentioned  
on the MT troubleshooting page:

http://monotouch.net/Documentation/Troubleshoot#MonoDevelop_crashes_when_you_copy_text

Might be relevant... might not.

Have you tried copying and pasting this same text into another editor?  
Like TextEdit? That's the first thing I'd try - just to see what  
happens.

If it pastes just fine into TextEdit, then I'd try the following next  
(in TextEdit):

   1) Convert the document to plain text (command-shift-t (I think -  
writing this on my phone, so don't have TextEdit in front of me - if  
that key combo doesn't work, the option is in one of TextEdit's menus,  
which are simple - you'll find it)).

   2) Copy *that* text and paste it into MD.

When apps behave oddly following a copy/paste (happens more often than  
you'd think/hope), that's what I do. Usually solves my problems.

It's not a fix - might not even work - but, hey, you never know :)
