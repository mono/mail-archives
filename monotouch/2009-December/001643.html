It seems highly unlikely that Apple could discover that I built an app while
using the SDK on non-Apple hardware. However, until the agreement is tossed
out by a court, it is in effect. I personally wouldn't take the chance that
Apple could pull my apps and terminate the agreement. Others may see things
differently.

(Well, it's unlikely Apple could discover this unless they were reading my
posts on public forums and mailing lists. If they have code that
automatically checks for private APIs... Just kidding. I can dream up
conspiracy theories that I don't believe in.)
