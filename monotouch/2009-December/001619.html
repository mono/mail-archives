For "EntryPointNotFoundException: sqlite3_column_origin_name", take a look at this page:

	http://monotouch.net/Documentation/System.Data

Scroll to the bottom (or do an in-page search for "sqlite3_column_origin_name"), and you'll find some notes that should help. Seems to come down to the version of sqlite3 that ships on the phone vs. the version Mono.Data.Sqlite works with (3.0 vs. 3.5).

Don't know if that helps, but I've seen a couple other people run into the same error despite their code looking just fine (the page says Mono.Data.Sqlite is a preview version, so we can expect some weirdness).

In the meantime, I'm going to go look at what you're working on - not because I think I can help, but just out of total curiosity - neat to see what other people are doing with MT :)

- Rory Blyth
