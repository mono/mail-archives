With my hackintosh there was no issue with the sdk or MonoTouch eval version.  They worked fine and I could run monotouch apps in the simulator, etc.  The issue was with the license registration app that came with the full version.  When I tried to register my license it would always fail.  Had customer support take a look and they even looked at my system profiler output.  Apparently the system profiler output wasn't valid in some way due to it's hackintoshy-ness and was told there wasn't anything that could really be done since they don't support running monotouch on non-Macs.

I guess those that have successfully gotten theirs registered on a hackintosh lucked out. :)
