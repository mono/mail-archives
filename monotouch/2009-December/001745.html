I had the exact same problem. I solved it by going to the Organizer in
Xcode. There it indicates what is wrong with a given provisioning profile
with pretty decent information. I'm currently at work (no Mac in site), so I
can't give you details. But if you fire up the Organizer it should help, I
was able to immediately fix my problem then go back to MonoDevelop and build
for distribution just fine.
