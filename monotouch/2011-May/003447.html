After some experiments today, I see that I can restore the debugger 
by deleting the old copy of the app under development, and 
reinstalling from MonoDevelop. Once the debugger loses the connection 
to the app, it appears to stay that way, based on a few compile-debug 
iterations, unless I delete the current copy from the device.
