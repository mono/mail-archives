When running debug device builds, I leave a breakpoint on the first 
line of code in main so that I know right away whether the debugger 
is communicating with the app and the device properly. I often do not 
see the app stop at that breakpoint.

I don't know exactly what it takes to trip it up, but I wind up doing 
a clean all and rebuild all to recover debugger operation with device 
builds.

Does anyone know a better way to preempt losing the debugger's attention? :)
