Ok, so I did miss the implementation up above, sorry.

First up, it looks like you're mixing up the two binding methodologies. This line:

[DllImport("__Internal")]
Static extern void doNothing();

...is not needed. This style of binding (platform invoke or p/invoke) is for importing C-style APIs. You actually want to bind to a pure Objective-C type. Take a look here:

http://monotouch.net/Documentation/Binding_New_Objective-C_Types

-Oisin
