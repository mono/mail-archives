hmmm, odd, it's been happening to me for a few versions now... I am on th
latest builds of everything including MD 2.6b2...

Easiest way to create it is just create a new project, add a iPhone view
with controller, change the new controllers view background colour to
something other than white, save and close.

Then in main.cs instantinate the new view and add it as a subview to window.

When you launch the app (phone or simulator) you should see what I see which
is a white bar the size of the top status bar at the bottom of the screen...
