> Ok, so silence is golden. I skipped the installationcheck which I am
> presuming is bugged. I mounted the PKG file, then:
>
> cd /Developer/MonoTouch/
> sudo pax -z -p e -r -f /Volumes/MonoTouch\
> 4.0.2/monotouch-4.0.2.pkg/Contents/Archive.pax.gz
> sudo cp -r /Volumes/MonoTouch\ 4.0.2/monotouch-4.0.2.pkg /Library/Receipts/
> sudo rm /Library/Receipts/monotouch-4.0.2.pkg/Contents/Archive.pax.gz
> sudo /Volumes/MonoTouch\
> 4.0.2/monotouch-4.0.2.pkg/Contents/Resources/postflight
>
> ...and now it is installed for me. No problems so far, but your mileage may
> vary. Don't forget to upgrade Mono and MonoDevelop first, oh, and of course
> the iOS SDK to 4.3.
