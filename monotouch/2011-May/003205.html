Try executing the binding on the main thread. See the threading excerpt at http://monotouchexamples.com/ .
You may also have an issue if it truly is running on a different thread as the data may be getting nuked before you can use it (note the AutoReleasePool object in the example). Checking your program output after running may give a clue (if it's getting garbage collected w/o a pool you'll see a bunch of "Just Leaking" messages).
