If you have the MonoTouch WROX book http://amzn.to/dDtg5r see page 64
(Listing 4-5) which talks about an editable TextView -- basically it RESIZES
to be smaller (so it isn't "hidden" by the keyboard), then you can 'scroll'
to the right spot and it will be visible.

I guess it's the same for a tableview (disclaimer: someone correct me if i'm
wrong) - you need to make its frame smaller so it isn't hidden under the
keyboard, _then_ scroll to the relevant cell.

look for UIKeyboard.WillShowNotification UIKeyboard.WillHideNotification
examples using NSNotificationCenter. Us UIKeyboard.BoundsFromNotification to
adjust your tableview.

that's where i'd start, anyway... there's sure to be obj-c samples around
that might also give you hint.
