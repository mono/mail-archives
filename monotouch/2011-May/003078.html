It may be a problem in SettingsMenuTVSource, or even in the ViewDidLoad

Also, move the

var settings = new MainMenu()

out of that method - well, the declaration of settings anyway:

in the class:

MainMenu settings;

in the method:

settings = new MainMenu();

Otherwise your settings may get GC'ed before you get to use it.
