I managed to find a newer EFI boot ISO (donk's darwin ISO v3.1 - see the
list at the bottom of
http://www.insanelymac.com/forum/index.php?showtopic=243274 ) which
thankfully allowed SPMemoryReporter.spreporter to function correctly. This,
in turn, let me activate MT 4 successfully.

Yay. Thanks for being my audience which in turn made my ego refuse to let
myself give up on this. :)
