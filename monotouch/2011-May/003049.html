You can open a ViewController after login that can have a UITextView empty
(that you'll load with the first question) and can have above that the
answer area, like a spinner or a UITable with dynamic answers that when
user click and then press a button like NEXT.. You save that locally or
temporarily and changes all content, like second question and answers list
of second question.

And a Previews button to go back to change what was selected.

At the end a Finish/send to finalize that..
