No, you should be able to install it on a new machine (they say the
license is perpetual, but not that you are stuck to the physical
hardware you had when it expires), at least thats my understanding.

Try and find the registering / license app. I assume you have the
installer for your version somewhere? (or, log into your
store.xamarin.com account and download the version which you last had
access to or have already) Look in /Applications, see if it's in
there.

Or, you should be able to activate your copy using Xamarin Studio -
before you open a project, click on your avatar in the top right
corner...

Or go back to support and point this out:

"
Can I continue to use Xamarin when my subscription expires?

Yes you can. Your Xamarin license is perpetual. If you choose not to
renew your subscription, you will no longer have access to new
releases and support, and we will be very sad.
"

which should include being able to install it on a new mac (assuming
you have only installed it on 2 other macs - same rules as paying
people)
