Yup, code 99 is a license issue.

You need to re-register monotouch (Xamarin.iOS). You can either email
support at xamarin.com who should know how to help you, or find the
MonoTouchActivation.app which is often in /Applications - that may do
it.

If you didn't unregister your machine, you can also go onto the
Xamarin site (where you buy it), login, and in there you can remove a
machine so you can register another one.

It's slightly different in old versions tho (before Xamarin 2.0) -
which version do you have?
