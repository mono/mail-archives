Hey Wayne

Some thoughts (also in the SO thread):

Is SqlLite even threadsafe? I'm not sure - it may be that it's not (to
the wrapper isn't). Can you lock on a more global object, so no two
threads are inserting at the same time?

It's possible that the MT GC is getting a little overenthusiastic, and
releasing your string before it's been used. Maybe keep a local
reference to it around during the insert? I've had this happen with
view controllers, where I had them in an array (tabcontrollers,
specificially), but if I didn't keep an member variable around with
the reference, they got GC'ed.

Could you get the data in a threaded manner, then queue everything up
and insert them in a single thread? Atleast as a test anyway.
