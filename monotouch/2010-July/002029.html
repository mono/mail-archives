On Wed, Jul 7, 2010 at 8:00 PM, Frank Krueger <fak at kruegersystems.com>wrote:

> As it's my project I had better state its state.
>
> Caulker is still in its infancy. I started the project for an up-coming
> app. The goal was to have a mapping engine that utilized 3D cartesian math
> (instead of projection math) so I could do augmented reality / game style
> rendering. That is, I plan to have "chase cameras" and "arial cameras".
> Basically, I wanted a game engine that used real world data.
>
> State of the project: certainly incomplete. I will pick up active
> development toward the end of the summer (two apps to complete before the
> one that will use this engine). However, I am open to ideas / feature
> requests.
>
> Planned features include:
>
> (1) Inclusion of terrain data in the United States so that mountains are
> mountains.
> (2) More work on cameras (blimp and chase being the most important)
> (3) Addition of paths and annotations
>
> Features not planned but considered:
>
> (1) Routing library
> (2) Feature search
>
> -Frank
