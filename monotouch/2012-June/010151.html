So far, I've tended to look at things I do or use, and try to automate
them. Not always overly exciting tho :)

or, if you are just exprimenting, try to recreate something someone
else has done - eg the pulldown animations in Path, the side-ways menu
thing from facebook etc (tho that last one has been done... but could
be fun as an exercise)

I read an article by Tim Ferris (4 hour work week etc) - he (or rather
the guy he was interviewing) looks at the top 10 non-games and copies
them (pays others to develop them). Appears to work for him, but I
dont find that very creative!
