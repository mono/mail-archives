I have a very good friend who works for an Apple show room.  It would
be very unusual for a store to not have an item on the floor that's
brand new.  They always close down the store the night before and do a
big preparation for the reveal the next morning.  When in doubt you
can always call ahead of time.  The Apple Store app for the iPhone has
a feature to quickly find and phone the nearest store.  Of course it's
just as easy to Google.  But the Apple Store iPhone app has the added
bonus that you can scan a product, checkout, and leave the store all
from your phone, item in hand, and w/o any human interaction (although
is that good or bad?).  If you get stopped you have the receipt right
in the app.  In any case it's pretty fun.  Oh, and be sure to connect
to the store's wireless, otherwise you won't get the checkout option.

-Abe
