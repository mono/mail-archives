ASIHttpRequest [1] is a separate framework that is not (presently)
binded in MonoTouch.

You can either do the bindings yourself, like any other ObjectiveC
library. It does not seems to be very complication (at least for your
scenario) [2] so a partial binding should be enough.

Or you can fill a bug report (enhancement) for it (or both :-)
