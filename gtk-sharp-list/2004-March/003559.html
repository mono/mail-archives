Make your Glade GUI as usual, but leave out the TreeView. Just create
an empty ScrolledWindow. Add your MyTreeView instance to it later, in
your code. 

i.e.:

   scrolledwindow1.Add(mytreeview);
   scrolledwindow1.ShowAll();
