Hi

There is RPMS in red-carpet under evolution snapshots (the new 1.3
versions). They have gal and GtkHTML packages. Only have to make a
symlink:

$ ln -s /usr/lib/libgtkhtml-3.0.so.0 /usr/lib/libgtkhtml-3.0.so

And it works.

/Mikkel

PS: Don't know if there is packages for SuSE or if the RH packages work
for SuSE
