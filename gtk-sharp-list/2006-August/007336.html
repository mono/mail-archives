Hi Again

Here is an update for the DemoPrinting. I have wrapped Pango.Cairo (some
of it) and made a patch for it.

1. unpack pangocairo.tar.gz in base gtk-sharp.
2. Copy DemoPrinting.cs to samples/GtkDemo
3. Patch it with PangoCairoPrinting.patch
4. run bootstrap-2.10

There is no more manually wrapping in DemoPrinting.

Could someone take a look at it and see if it can go into svn or if it
needs more work and give me some feedback !!
