Hello Gtk# hackers,


I really appreciate the efforts by you all to supply a good Win32 installer
of Gtk#. Lately I wanted to get a small project of mine to run under Windows
and while some of it succeeded, its really very troublesome.

A small suggestion: As GTK+ is not very popular among Windows users, why not
put a good and known-to-work-with version of GTK+ within the Gtk# installer?
I think its not too complex, simply supplying a known-good .exe installer
within the archive and running it would be fine. Maybe that will ease the
deployment among end users.


Thanks for considering,
