Add the following to your etc/mono/config on MacOS X:

<dllmap dll="libc">
<dllentry target="bindtextdomain" dll="libintl.1.dylib" 
name="bindtextdomain"/>
<dllentry target="bindtextdomain" dll="libintl.1.dylib" 
name="bind_textdomain_co
deset"/>
<dllentry target="bindtextdomain" dll="libintl.1.dylib" 
name="textdomain"/>
</dllmap>
