> The latest Gtk# 2.10 release (2.10.0) does not work on Windows due to
> #79993[1], so installers have not yet been released that contain Gtk#
> 2.10.
>
> However, since #79993 has been fixed in Subversion, we (at Medsphere)
> have built experimental installers from Gtk# 2.10 SVN[2].  They are
> available in source form only, are known to have one significant bug in
> the uninstall process (policy.2.8.* dlls aren't properly un-gac'ed), and
> come with absolutely no warranty.  Because of this, these installers
> should only be used for development purposes.
>
> Don't fret though, because as soon as Mike et al release the next
> version of Gtk# 2.10 (*hint hint*), we'll have installers built and
> uploaded on the OpenVista project[3] on SourceForge.
>
