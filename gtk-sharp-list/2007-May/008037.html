Hi all,

Since I bitched so much about how there was no context menu code 
available, I thought I better share what I got going. I reckon it 
looks alright, especially for short menus. Of course a little more 
work would make it look a lot flashier. At least though, the menu 
items sit together well, the menu activates *immediately* on opening, 
and you can hook the items up to standard event handlers. So now you 
can right click on your canvas or whatever and get a menu that is in 
context of where the click was. It has worked a treat for me 
(compared to what I had going).

I'm not sure if I've tried submitting a file here before, either way 
we'll see how it goes - it's only 6 kB. "I mean, come on!" :-)

I sincerely hope someone finds this useful, I know I could have lived 
without having to write this myself. :-)

Aaron.
