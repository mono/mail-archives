GTK#, wraps the GTK native libs, in this case the ones compiled for
Windows/x86, that also need to be installed (and those aren't
copy-installable, as the managed ones).

The installer project, AFAIK, is in Novell Forge:
http://developer.novell.com/wiki/index.php/Special:Downloads/gtks-inst4win
