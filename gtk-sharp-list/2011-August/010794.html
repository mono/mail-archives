I am assuming your are talking about the Mono Introspection project that Alan McGovern started on GitHub?

https://github.com/alanmcgovern/mono-introspect

You might have better luck emailing him directly or emailing the maintainer (and creator) of gtk# - Mike Kestner.

In the long, long term, GObject Introspection would probably be the way to go for Gtksharp; however, for now, it is easier just to continue using gapi.

I would do a search on the gtksharp-list on the direction that Mike is taking gtksharp.


Then again, Mono Introspect is open source, so you could always contribute patches or fork it.
