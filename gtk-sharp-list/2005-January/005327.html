Hi,

I used them in my music player Bluefunk, which you can get out of CVS
from http://bluefunk.sf.net. It's only a fairly simple example, but
might help to get you going in the right direction. Useful code for
gst# is in src/GstPlayer.cs and src/GstMetadata.cs

I know there are a couple of other music players; I don't know if
there's any video C# code about the place.

The Gst svn has a couple of examples in too.
