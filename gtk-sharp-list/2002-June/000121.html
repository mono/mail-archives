On Fri, 2002-06-21 at 16:09, Guenther Roith wrote:
> gtk# builds without problems, but when I run something it complains
> about libgtksharpglue.so missing. What can I do?

Make sure your LD_LIBRARY_PATH includes the location of
libgtksharpglue.so.  (GMODULE_PATH may also work)

For me, I install all of my mono libs into /opt/mono, so my
LD_LIBRARY_PATH is set to /opt/mono/lib
