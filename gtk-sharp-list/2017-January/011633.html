Hi, Sascha,

You need to know how to create signals in MonoDevelop. Clicking the "ok"
button doesn't do anything because you haven't specified a signal
connecting the OnClick method and the button itself.

Take a look at this tutorial in youtube:
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZXaKK0IYTRg

At 5:30 the way to create signals is shown.
Hope this helps.
