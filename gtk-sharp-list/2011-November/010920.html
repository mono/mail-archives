This is probably caused by bug #731579 I just opened with mono. It

--debug leaks memory.

Also, I noticed that Application.Invoke doesn't seem to use a
threadsafe version of g_timeout_add. Is that correct??? Shouldn't it
be using: gdk_threads_add_timeout(...) or using an explicit lock (...)
