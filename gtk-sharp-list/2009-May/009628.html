Yes, it is definitely possible. For the Gtk# OpenGL widget, use
GtkGLAreaSharp:

http://www.mono-project.com/GtkGLSharp

For actually doing the OpenGL rendering, I would use the Tao wrappers:

http://taoframework.com/

Both come with examples and work quite well with each other.

-Andy
