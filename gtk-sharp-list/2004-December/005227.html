> I have libgconf-cil installed, but I still cannot install tomboy. It
> keeps asking for the 3 dlls which are not in the required folder under
> /usr/local/lib/mono/gtk-sharp. Can I get just the dlls and try copying
> them to the folder? Installing gtk-sharp is not as straight forward as
> one would expect, for one thing, Debian does not have some packages
> which it needs still in the repositories.
> Any help on installing the dll files would be highly appreciated.

Afaik, Tomboy requires libgnome-cil, libgtk-cil, libgconf-cil,
libglade-cil and libglib-cil.  All the dependencies can be perfectly
satisfied with what Debian has in Unstable, in fact, I have made my own
packages.  Unfortunately, I'm not too terribly experienced with Debian
packaging, so the dependencies may not be 100% correct, but you're
welcome to add my repository to your /etc/apt/sources.list and give me
any tips that you can.

Just add this to your apt sources:

deb http://brad.getcoded.net/debian/ binary/
