You'll need WebKitGTK[0] binaries for Windows. You can download them
from the GnuCash Sourceforge page[1] inside the unstable/win32
dependencies.

I am using it and it works fine, it has some issues when printing, but
nothing that you can't workaround.

[0] http://www.webkitgtk.org/
[1] http://sourceforge.net/projects/gnucash/files
