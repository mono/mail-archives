Hi,

You can use the methods ModifyBase and ModifyText of the Widget class.

------------------------------
myEntry.ModifyBase(StateType.Normal, new Gdk.Color(255,0,0));
myEntry.ModifyText(StateType.Normal, new Gdk.Color(0,255,0));
-----------------------------
