On Wed, 2003-02-19 at 23:25, Charles Iliya Krempeaux wrote:

> What would be the most convenient format to submit this?  (I don't have

> everything?  Or should I just send over the stuff that I've added
> or modified?

If you aren't already doing so, I'd get set up to use anoncvs until you
get a few patches under your belt and we can get you write access. 
There are instructions at:

http://go-mono.org/anoncvs.html

Make your changes and once your new code is building cleanly against a
fresh anoncvs image, use the cvs diff -u command to generate a patch for
existing files, and send it, along with a tarball of any files you've
added to the list and I'll take care of it from there.
