I'm pleased to announce a new release of the 2.12 bindings.

Source tarball is available for download:

http://ftp.gnome.org/pub/gnome/sources/gtk-sharp/2.12/gtk-sharp-2.12.2.tar.gz

Packages are in the works and will be available in the near future at
mono-project.com.

This release includes some nice new features, in addition to a
substantial number of bugfixes and performance enhancements.  The
ChangeLog has the details.  

Here are some of the highlights:

Extensive Atk# API cleanup (Andres Aragoneses, Brad Taylor, and MK)
Signal EmissionHook and Emit implementations (Andres and MK)
GObject Property Registration (Christian Hoff)
Interface and VirtualMethod improvements (Christian Hoff)
Bugfixing (Andres Aragoneses, Stephane Delcroix, Lluis Sanchez Gual,
Christian Hoff & MK)

My thanks to all the contributors.
