Hello,

It is possible to add other "gladed" components if what you want is to
design several parts in glade. Look at the attached sample.
 
The main container is in one assembly, and the component is in another
one, so you can easily achieve a separation of "business units" or
whatever name you give to the components.

Unfortunately, this sample doesn't work anymore on gtk# 0.17, and I
don't have time to finish it. The "Component1" should display a frame
with 9 buttons inside, but by now just 1 button is shown (don't remember
exactly, but i think it's a matter of reparenting each) but you can test
it in 0.16. The sample shows the idea, it just work as a proof of
concept.

Regards,

Gustavo
