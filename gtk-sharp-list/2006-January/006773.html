find the gtk headers.

To fix change
INCLUDES = $(VTE_DEPENDENCIES_CFLAGS)  -I$(top_srcdir)
to
INCLUDES = $(VTE_DEPENDENCIES_CFLAGS) $(GTK_CFLAGS) -I$(top_srcdir)
in
vte/glue/Makefile.am

someone who can change it take a look
