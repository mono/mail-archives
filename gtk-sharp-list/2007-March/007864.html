The gnome-db maintainers have removed Gda# and GnomeDb# from libgda and
libgnomedb[1], stating for several reasons that they belong in a
separate package like other GNOME bindings.

So for development to continue, these two need a new home.  AFAICS the
options would be:

a) Reintroduce them to gnome-sharp, post-2.18 of course;
b) Create a separate gnome-sharp-extras a la Python, where these and
other stray bindings could be developed and packaged without "polluting"
gnome-sharp;
c) Create separate modules for each in Mono SVN, and release them
independently.

Direction and assistance would be appreciated from the powers that be to
get this worked out.

[1] http://bugzilla.gnome.org/show_bug.cgi?id=390609
