I find it hard to search specifically for GTK# pages because search
engines like Google and Bing don't get the "#" part. Even putting
"GTK#" in quotes doesn't push the GTK# hits above the GTK+ hits.

So I created a Google Custom Search at:

http://www.google.com/cse/home?cx=000684786887132452694%3Anlpemshxrqi

The sites included are:

http://anonsvn.mono-project.com/viewvc/trunk/
http://www.devx.com/opensource/Article/32302/1954
http://www.go-mono.com/docs/
http://www.mono-project.com/
http://lists.ximian.com/pipermail/gtk-sharp-list/

If there is another solution to this problem, or you have other sites
to add to the custom search, please share.
