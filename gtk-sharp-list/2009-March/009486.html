Hi,

Searched for the same, found a solution:

TextIter ti = textview1.Buffer.GetIterAtLine(textview1.Buffer.LineCount-1);
TextMark tm = textview1.Buffer.CreateMark("eot", ti,false);
textview1.ScrollToMark(tm, 0, false, 0, 0);
