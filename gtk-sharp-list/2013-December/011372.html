libgtk-win32-2.0-0.dll is added to Mono\bin folder.

libraries, then copy libgtk-3-0.dll from gtk3+\bin folder to Mono\bin
folder. Just rename libgtk-3-0.dll to libgtk-win32-3.0-0.dll and you are
done.
