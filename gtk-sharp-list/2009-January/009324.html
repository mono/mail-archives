Just to follow up on this...

I committed the 2.14 update to svn yesterday, so now trunk requires a
gtk+ 2.14.0 or better installation.  I integrated the gio-sharp module
into gtk-sharp as part of this update.  Thanks to Stephane for all his
nice work in gio-sharp.  BTW, Stephane, I still consider you the
maintainer of gio-sharp.  ;-)

Anyone who needs to build gtk-sharp against a gtk+ 2.12 install will
have to use the gtk-sharp-2-12-branch from now on.

Anyone who has been twitching for 2.14 APIs, including gio-sharp, should
consider trunk ready for playing with, but not yet stable.  I have
audited it for compat against 2.12 already.  Please report any bugs,
warts, or whatever to bugzilla and I'll try to get to them promptly.
