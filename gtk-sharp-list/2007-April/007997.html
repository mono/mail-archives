Medsphere's installers are targeted at people using Gtk# with
Microsoft's .NET libraries, not Mono.  For your application, Novell's
Bundled Mono and Gtk# installers are better suited, but they haven't yet
updated, and won't update until (at the very least) the next Gtk#
release.

Also, let it be known that the installer that Cody linked you is not an
official release, and is not guaranteed to not set your computer on
fire, or paint your dog purple if you stare at it wrong.  We'll be
releasing official versions when the next Gtk# release occurs.
