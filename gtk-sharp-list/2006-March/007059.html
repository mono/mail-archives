You are correct.  You can create applications that use pretty much all
of what Gnome# 2.x has to offer in Linux (no libpanelapplet support) but
there is no libglade-nome.dll (the unmanged library that could provide
for Glade# and Gnome).

So if you want to have a Gnome# app that also works on Win32 you would
have to forgoe Glade -- at least for now.
