Hey guys,

    We have a couple of open tasks in gtk# but we do not have time in
our schedule to hack on those before the 1.0 release, so am posting this
hoping that someone has some interest in implementing them:

	* Databinding control.

	  Essentially the idea is to provide databinding to the
	  Gtk.ListView: avoid any of the issues of setting up the model
	  and callbacks that are so hard.

	  Lets just implement the same databinding functionality that
	  .NET has on their Web and Winforms controls for our
	   Gtk.ListView in some form or other.

	* System.ComponentModel integration: Maybe a post 1.0 feature,
	  but I would like to get the editing facilities in place, so 
	  properties in Gtk# widgets can be edited from a central
	  property editor.

	* Property Editor.

	  A rich property editor for Gtk# widgets.

Miguel
