There is a port of gtk+ to Win32.
And yes, there is gtk# for Win32 as well.
However, the gnome# pieces in gtk# do not work on Win32 because gnome 2.x
does not work on Win32.  There are people working to get gnome 2.x working
on Win32 though.
Note: gtk# on Windows is not as stable as gtk# on Linux.  This is due to
gtk+ support on Windows or Linux.
