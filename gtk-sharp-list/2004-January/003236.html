On Thu, 22 Jan 2004, Chris Vickerson wrote:

> Please, what switches to I have to include to get all of the
> following installed?
> 
> <snip>
>    Optional assemblies included in the build:
>  
>       * gnome-sharp.dll: no
>       * glade-sharp.dll: no
>       * gda-sharp.dll: no
>       * gnomedb-sharp.dll: no
>       * rsvg-sharp.dll: no
>       * gtkhtml-sharp.dll: no
>       * vte-sharp.dll: no
>  
>       NOTE: if any of the above say 'no' you may install the
>             corresponding development packages for them, rerun
>             autogen.sh to include them in the build.
> </snip>

It's automatic. Make sure gnome-devel, gtk+-devel, gtkhtml-devel, etc.
etc. etc. are all installed and the configure program will detect them
and build the glue automatically.
