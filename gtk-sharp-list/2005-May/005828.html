Many of the more obscure bits of the Gnome namespace in gtk# haven't
been tested that much, so it's entirely possible that it just doesn't
work right now. What are you having problems with?

> Another question: Is GtkIconView the successor to Gnome.IconList, and
> should I just wait for Gtk# 2?

Yes, GtkIconView is the successor to GnomeIconList. As to whether or not
you should wait, that depends on whether or not your app can wait until
Gtk# 2 has been released.
