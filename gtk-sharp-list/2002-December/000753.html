Hello,

> What I'd like to know is if there is an ETA for when RPMs
> for Gtk# are expected.

The only issue with Gtk# RPMs is that they depend on the various new
libraries (Gtk2, Pango, GnomeLibs, etc) and those have not been packaged
for most distributions.

I know Ximian plans on releasing a full set of libraries for most
popular distributions, but I do not know when that will happen.  Once
that happens, we will ship Gtk# in the Mono channel.  Until then, it
would mean that we would have to build and maintain those ourselves, and
that is not an easy task.

Miguel
