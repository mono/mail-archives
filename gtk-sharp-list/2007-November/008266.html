Hello!

I just wanted to tell you that I did a language binding for abiword /
abiwidget for C#. With this you can easily embed a abiword editing
widget in your C# application.

It's based on the SVN revision 22273 of abiword which must be compiled
with --enable-libabiword.

Beware that the language binding is not final and I was told that the
api of libabiword will probably also change in the near future.

You can get the binding with this command: 
"hg clone http://www.rtfs.org/hg/abiword-sharp/"

This binding also exposes a bug in the gapi2 parser which for now makes
it necessary to patch the abiword source a little bit.

I would be thankful for any comment or hint about this binding.

Sincerely, Fabian
