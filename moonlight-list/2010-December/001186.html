A word of caution.

Don't assume that you can develop for SilverLight and that your solution
"will 
just work" on Moonlight. I had a case recently where I was asked to
demonstrate 
proof of concept on UNIX for a project I am working on. The software would
hang 
during a section of code where we are making parallel asynchronous calls. 
Because of the small target user base we were not in a position to justify 
allocating resource for investigation of the problem(s) and to re-engineer
the 
software.

I would recommend that you build a Moonlight test environment at a very
early 
stage in your project and test regularly.
