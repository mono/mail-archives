What is the configure output of moonlight?

 

If you want mono-basic support, you either need to checkout mono-basic next to moonlight (in this case next to your where you extracted the moonlight tarball), or configure with --with-mono-basic-path=<path to mono-basic checkout >.

 

In any case the configure process should detect if mono-basic isn’t available and fail gracefully.
