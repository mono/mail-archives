not sure what you mean by "registered it globally"...

the configure script defaults --with-ff36=yes, so there's no reason to 
add it.

The trick is that you need to have the location of the .pc files 
exported in your PKG_CONFIG_PATH.  That is, if you have the .pc files in 
/opt/mozilla/xulrunner-1.9.2/lib/pkgconfig, you want PKG_CONFIG_PATH to 
contain "/opt/mozilla/xulrunner-1.9.2/lib/pkgconfig"

for ff3.6, moonlight looks for:

     libxul.pc
     mozilla-plugin.pc
     mozilla-js.pc

If configure can't find those .pc files, you won't have ff3.6 support.
