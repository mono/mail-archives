Just a quick update to clarify things regarding support for Chrome and
Firefox 3.6, on trunk.

Firefox 3.6 (and 3.7): full support, including mms

Chrome: doesn't work at the moment due to reentrancy issues. The
symptoms are, Chrome hangs on the splash screen. If it doesn't hang
for you (you lucky person), then everything except mms should work.
Chrome hasn't been thoroughly tested yet, so no guarantees.

There is a very hacky and very temporary workaround for the Chrome
reentrancy issue here: http://monobin.com/__f1aa6ccd6
It should make things load on Chrome.
