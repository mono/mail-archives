Hey everyone,

  A short follow up to this.  The moon branch is somewhat different than
the traditional release module.  We have branched our stable release
which will become moon 1.0 from the trunk yesterday, and the trunk is
now open for future 2.0 work again.  All of our beta's and rc's and gold
releases for the 1.0 set of releases will derivce from:

http://anonsvn.mono-project.com/sources/branches/moon-1-0

and be tagged from there.

If you have any questions on the release policy / commit policy / etc,
feel free to ask.

Thanks
