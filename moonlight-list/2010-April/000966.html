Hi,

 

I'm not sure I understand what you're trying to say here: there is no sense
in adding support for uploading media, since you can't (easily) modify media
on the client (you can, but you need a managed video decoder (since you
don't have access to the decoded video otherwise), code to edit the video,
then a managed video encoder (unless you want to upload huge amount of data
to the server)). It's much easier to do the actual video editing on the
server using already available tools.
