My understanding is that Intel is only targeting Moblin.  The version of Silverlight Intel produces may or may not run on other flavors of Linux but if it does, it's hard to envisage any support channels.

Moreover (again, as I understand it) this implementation will be included in the Intel SDK for Atom processors and be available for OEMs to include in Atom powered Moblin-based notebooks.  It's unclear that it will be available to organizations and individuals who are not producing Atom powered notebooks.

Meanwhile Moonlight is, apparently, Microsoft's route to supporting the main Linux distributions.
