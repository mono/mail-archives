Hi,

Have you looked at out-of-browser part of Silverlight 3/4? You can give it
elevated trust (not everything is allowed, but it might suit you). The
advantage is that you don't have to develop a different application for
Linux and windows, and it'll work with Moonlight when we get Moonlight 4 out
(Out of browser will be in Moonlight 3, possibly also elevated trust, but we
don't know yet [*]).

The desklets haven't been worked on much since we've been busy trying to
keep up with Silverlight releases.

Rolf

[*] Since out-of-browser was introduced with Silverlight 3, but elevated
trust came with Silverlight 4.
