* Terra on-line Radio with Silverlight/Moonlight

After a long time I try Terra on-line radio to check if it back to work on
Linux. I just click on Blues radio and one window pop up  to tell me that
Microsoft Silverlight was necessary to hear the Terra radio. I was surprise
when I see that in bottom of window a link say: "Linux users, click here to
see an alternative to hear Terra on-line radio". The link redirect to
Moonlight page.

So, I look at Mandriva repositories and moon was there. I installed and it
works! It is now playing "Truth" from "Jeff Beck" with a very good audio
quality.

I haven't decided if it is good or bad. Anyway, today we have an official
free alternative (despite of polemics) to play a proprietary format.
