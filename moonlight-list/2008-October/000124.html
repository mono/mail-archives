Its impossible that the plugin would be working with a "few simple
changes" on OSX.  Off the top of my head the following would be required
to make it work on OSX:

#1: Port the audio layer to CoreAudio
#2: Adapt the rendering layer to not assume that we're running in
cairo-xlib, but detect at runtime wether we're on xlib or quartz.
#3: Drop the entire glib/gtk runloop and abstract it into a modular
backend that can use glib/gtk on linux, and a NSRunLoop on a mac.
#4: We'd also have to figure out how to do windowless plugins, but this
could be done down the road and short circuit everything to windowed for
the short term, so that we can get a CGContextRef when we want to paint.

I'm sure there are other issues that I'm missing, thats just a
high-level overview.
