Heh, nothing is wrong with *you* :)

Moonlight 2.0 and Mono are intimately bound. So you need to build your
own Mono along with Moonlight. So you have to build your own Mono
before building Moonlight.

I suggest you checkout from svn the modules: mcs, mono and moon, (as
described here:
http://www.mono-project.com/Parallel_Mono_Environments).

So if you checkout the three of them in your /tmp/source folder as you
did for moon, you'll build first /tmp/source/mono, and that will build
/tmp/source/mcs for you. You'll see the assemblies that are required
for moon in /tmp/source/mcs/class/lib/net_1_1 and
/tmp/source/mcs/class/lib/net_2_1_raw.

When that is done, you'll be able to configure moon
--with-mcspath=/tmp/source/mcs and you'll be alright.
