Hello,

I have a quick survey on "missing members in
System.Xml.Serialization.dll" that Geoff showed on monoport (the full
list is appended to this message).

For items with '+', I'll try to implement them today. For '-', they
either need some help from JB, or I'll have more look later.


+ NamespaceSpecified properties: they do not seem to exist in .NET 3.5
  SP1 (at least not reported on our class status page). Some are however
  up on MSDN (as Geoff found like [*1]), some don't.
  I think they can be easily added.
- XmlConvert.ToBinHexString() and .FromBinHexString() : they exist in
  2.0 profile, so with some tuner work it could be fixed.
+ XmlRootAttribute.Key : it does not exist, but similar functionality is
  in our code.
+ XmlChoiceIdentifierAttribute.MethodInfo : it does not exist, but
  would be simply added.
+ XmlConvert.ToUri() : it does not exist in any profile, but looks easy.
- System.ServiceModel.OperationFormatStyleHelper: no quick idea.

[*1]
http://msdn.microsoft.com/en-us/library/system.xml.serialization.xmlarrayitemattribute.isnullablespecified.aspx


Atsushi Eno
