2009/3/25 Jason Wisener <jwisener at gmail.com>:
> Quick question. Ok, so let's say, I pulled the source (trunk) to
> /opt/monosrc. Does the prefix of /opt/mono tell the make system to make the
> executables & libraries to /opt/mono ?

Not quite. The prefix /opt/mono just means that all the libraries and
whatnot will be installed there instead of in /usr. The little bash
script which sets all your environment variables is the one which
makes the system load executables/libraries from /opt/mono.
