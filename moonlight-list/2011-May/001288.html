> Any word on the street about a release schedule for Moonlight 4?

Once it's ready ;-) However we're releasing previews as new features are
available. You can either (slowest to fastest answers):

1) watch the announcements on the web site;

2) install moonlight 3.99.x preview and get updated automatically when
new previews are released;

3) fill a bug report with a test case (e.g. what your application needs
from elevated trust) and you'll be notified once it gets
"fixed" (implemented).

That would likely be a few days/weeks before the release. But from that
point you can download a "nightly build" to test it (and give us a
chance to fix things further before the next release).

Have fun!
