Hey all,

  We have encountered 2 firefox bugs that can potentially affect the
Moonlight experience.  One causes a crash in certain circumstances
(mozilla bug #435764) the other causes graphics corruption in certain
windowless cases and has the added bonus of some performance
improvements.  The maintainer of the mozilla repository for openSUSE has
been kind enough to backport these patches for us (they are both
requested for 1.9.0.2, which is ff3.0.2) and has made OBS repositories
available for 10.3 and 11.0:

10.3:

http://download.opensuse.org/repositories/mozilla/openSUSE_10.3/


11.0:

http://download.opensuse.org/repositories/mozilla/openSUSE_11.0/

If you're doing moonlight testing, its strongly suggested that you
upgrade to these firefox packages until the patches are merged to a
release version of Firefox.

Feel free to contact me with any questions.

-g
