Ahoy everybody,

You may have noticed that Mono is going to have its own devroom this year at
FOSDEM (if you haven't, take a look[0]).

In order to be even more productive during this FOSDEM 2010 edition, a Mono
Hackaday is organized on the monday following the event (that is, February
8th).

We are going to be hosted by HSB[1] (Hackerspace Brussels) who have kindly
agreed to let us organize such event there. Location informations can be
found on this page[2]. Like every other hackerspace, HSB is loaded with tons
of hackers facilities to make coding even more fun/effective. The space will
be open for us *from 10am to 19pm*.

Everyone is welcome to drop by, there is no precise hacking direction or
plan so that you get to work on what you want as long as it's Mono related.

Don't miss this unique opportunity to meet other Mono fellows !

Mono Hackaday on HSB wiki : http://hackerspace.be/Mono_hack-a-day
