> The unit test framework actually has support for the concept of known
failures, 
> but they've named it a bit different: test methods which are known
failures 
> are marked with [BugAttribute]. The harness will always execute these
tests, 
> and mark them as failed when they succeed (i.e. revert the result). I
enhanced
> this a bit by adding SilverlightBugAttribute and MoonlightBugAttribute
(they 
> both inherit from BugAttribute) - a test marked with [MoonlightBug] will
be
> treated as a normal test on Silverlight and a known failure on Moonlight.
> The old KnownFailureAttribute does no longer exist. 

I also went through the tests and marked current failures with
[MoonlightBug], so as of r120593 we have a green unit test run.
