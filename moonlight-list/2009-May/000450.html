This isn’t easy, you might be able to do it with a standalone utility,
similar to our mopen program, where you run a silverlight application and
periodically walk the entire tree in the application looking for
MediaElements and getting their Source property (uri of the video), which
you can then use to download the file.

 

You might actually be able to pull the same trick with an extension.
