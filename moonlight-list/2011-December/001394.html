important they will be a smaller slice of the market.

, Notes  , Office , Early IE ) , they will not support Linux /Android . ( 
And Apple does even less for Microsoft  and Linux) .

So the real question for green fields apps is WinRt  (C++ or with a .NET 
wrapper ) or HTML5 (JavaScript or native client) ?
