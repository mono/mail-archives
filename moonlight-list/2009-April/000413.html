GPU acceleration "in general" is also a difficult answer, since we use 
cairo, which will accelerate many operations (composite, blend, scale) 
server side, where possible.  If you're referring to GPU acceleration of 
media decoding, VDPAU and VAAPI are things we'd like to support, and in 
fact proposed a GSoC project for, but didn't have any takers.  I'm sure 
we'll get it in sometime, just no firm timelines as of yet.
