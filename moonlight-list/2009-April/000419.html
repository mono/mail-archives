I checked out gtk-sharp, compiled and installed:

svn co http://anonsvn.mono-project.com/source/trunk/gtk-sharp
cd gtk-sharp
./bootstrap-2.14 –prefix=/opt/mono
make
sudo make install
cd ..

After that, I reran ./autogen.sh for moon.
