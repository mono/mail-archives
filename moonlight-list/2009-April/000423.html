﻿Like it was said before this is *not* an issue. Moonlight embeds its
won copy of Cairo. Moonlight has no need for PNG (the browser gives this
to us among other formats), SVG or PDF. Enabling them in cairo just
takes more memory (RAM and disk space).

Note that this is no different from what libgdiplus has been doing for
many years.

> and that that
> is really not a good idea.

Wrong. It would only be a bad idea if you were using this custom cairo
as your system cairo. Otherwise it depends on what is being used by its
caller (libmoon).
