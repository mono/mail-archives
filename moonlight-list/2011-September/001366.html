@djfw:
IIRC, there's an option to disable compatibility checking in a banner across the top of the Extensions panel.


Is Moonlight listed among your extensions when you hit cntrl-shift-a?  If not, locate the xpi on your machine or go to the ML site and right-click the download link and save.  Double-check the file's permissions and ensure FF can read the file.  Make sure the ACR is installed and enabled.  Then, from FF6, hit cntrl-o and open the xpi.  That will install it.  Here's more on manual installation: http://www.accessfirefox.org/Install_Addon_Manually.php.

If that doesn't work, then try the file editing method described here: http://support.mozilla.com/en-US/questions/839794


I don't know Linux well enough to comment on Gnome vs KDE.
