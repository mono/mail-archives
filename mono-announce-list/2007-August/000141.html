Hey Everyone,

Banter 0.1.10 has been released:

What is it?
===========
Banter is a next generation real time collaboration client focused on
the big three: text, voice and video.  It's centered on a dynamic,
innovative and easy to use contact management system.

What's new?
===========
Version 0.1.10
* UI bling, new notification badges on incoming requests
* Full support for inviting people, accepting invitations, and removing people
* General bug fixing and stabilizing
* Added mono-cairo dependency (note for packagers)
