Hello all,

Yesterday I released the very first (and very preliminary) version of ilasm
clone written in C#.
Source code is available on my Mono-page at http://mono.eurosoft.od.ua
Feel free to take a look, but don't expect too much :-)
You can compile the sources, but note that the included makefile requires
NMAKE.
Another dependency is Jay parser-generator (the same thing used by mcs).
Later it will be integrated into the Mono CVS and will be available in daily
snapshots etc.

Feel free to contact me for any related questions and have fun,
