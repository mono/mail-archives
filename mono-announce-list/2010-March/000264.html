This week we released Mono 2.6.3, a bugfix release in the 2.6.x series
containing more than 100 bugfixes.

The release notes are here:
        http://go-mono.com/archive/2.6.3

and downloads are available here:
    http://go-mono.com/mono-downloads/

This release includes updated LiveCD and VM Appliance images including
some applications not included before such as Pinta[0] and KeePass[1].

Thanks to all those who contributed to this release!



[0] http://www.pinta-project.com/
[1] http://keepass.info/
