Banshee 0.98.2 (aka 1.0 Alpha 2) has been released.  This release has
many great features, including brand new video management and
playback.  It does not have all the features of 0.13.2 yet, but it
will by 1.0 final.  It can be installed along side 0.13.2 or before.

Release notes with screenshots, ChangeLog, openSUSE 10.3 1-click
install, and full alpha-release disclaimer:
http://banshee-project.org/Releases/0.98.2
