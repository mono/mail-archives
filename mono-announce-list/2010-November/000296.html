Good news everyone! There will be a Mono Developer Room at FOSDEM
2011. We need your talks to make sure it's at least as big of a
success as last year!

We are looking for speakers to fill up the room, which we have on
Saturday, February 5 (13:00 – 19:00). If you have made nice and cool
things with or related to Mono and you want to show it to the world,
this is your chance!

If you're interested in speaking, please fill in this form:
http://bit.ly/mono-fosdem-2011

Do it before Dec 25, as we’ll need some time to handle confirmations
and finalize the schedule in time before the deadline imposed to us by
the FOSDEM organizers.

Also, please forward this CFP to whoever might be interested.

Cheers and I hope to see everyone there!
