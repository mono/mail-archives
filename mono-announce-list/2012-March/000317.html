We are happy to announce the release of Banshee 2.4, which is the
culmination of six months' work by 31 developers, 33 translators and
dozens of bug reporters and testers. It is a stable release, the
successor to Banshee 2.2.

Release notes: http://banshee.fm/download/archives/2-4-0/

Changes :
   * DVD playback
   * More flexible browsing of your media
   * UPnP support, to browse and play music or videos shared over your
local network
   * 91 bugs fixed since 2.2, and dozens of other enhancements

Download: http://banshee.fm/download/

Source:
http://ftp.gnome.org/pub/GNOME/sources/banshee/2.4/banshee-2.4.0.tar.xz

Windows installer:
http://ftp.gnome.org/pub/GNOME/binaries/win32/banshee/2.4/Banshee-2.4.0.msi

Mac OS X binary and Linux bundles should be available shortly.

Thanks to everybody who helped make it happen!
