We are proud to announce the release of Banshee 2.0, a stable release,
the successor to Banshee 1.8.

Release notes: http://banshee.fm/download/archives/2.0.0/

Changes:

      * Artist/Album Browser Track Actions
      * 'Play After' Queue Options
      * User Interface Improvements
      * Ubuntu One Music Store and SoundMenu Extensions
      * Subtitles
      * 277 bugs fixed since 1.8, and dozens of other enhancements

Download:
http://download.banshee.fm/banshee/stable/2.0.0/banshee-2.0.0.tar.bz2
http://banshee.fm/download/

Thanks to everybody who helped make it happen!
