Hello,



As you guys have noticed, the experience to setup Mono with Unreal Engine

is not very pleasant.   It involves compiling code from source, it involves

a specific version of Unreal and has a very long and tedious installation process.



The Unreal EULA is hostile to third party vendors and prevents us from creating the kind of integrated experience that we would like to have.



Based on the above, we do not anticipate that we will continue to develop the bindings of Mono for Unreal Engine at this point.



Miguel
